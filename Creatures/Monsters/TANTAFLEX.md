## Description

The TANTAFLEX or Chaos-spawn, is thought to

have been created millennia ago by evil Brain

Slayer magi, as they a;empted to summon avatars

of their demonic gods to serve them on Titan. The

result was something terrifyingly out one’s worst

nightmares; human once but now an oozing be-

tentacled horror, that made sucking noises as it

slithered across stone floors towards its victims.

The tentacles sprout from where the Tantaflex’s

shoulders should be, and there are scaly forearms

that end in clawed hands. The beast stands on

webbed feet, and piercing eyes glare out from

above a beak-like mouth. As avatars of Chaos, the

Tantaflex were hunted down and largely destroyed

during the War of the Wizards, caused when they

were released from their tombs in the Dead City by

Khulian adventurers and sorcerers from Zagoula.

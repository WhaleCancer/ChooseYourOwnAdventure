## Description

Thought to be related to the Groundhog, although

somewhat smaller, the GLACIATOR is yet another

bizarre creature from Khul. They have a wide, flat

body, covered in leathery hide, supported by eight

legs with club-like feet. On the underside of their

body is a small fanged mouth, while strange sense

organs line the creature’s top rim, between each leg.

Glaciators maintain their own body’s temperature

by extracting warmth from the surrounding air. If

they stay in one place, their surroundings soon

freeze (if not frozen already), into a polar scene of

icicles and ice sheets on the ceiling, walls and floor,

hence the creature’s name.

Anyone fighting a

Glaciator in combat must reduce their SKILL by 2

points due to the uncontrollable shivering caused

by the freezing temperatures surrounding the beast.

## Description

The MUD SLIME is a terrifying though thankfully

rare aquatic predator found in Allansian waterways

such the Deedlewater and the Spider River. In

appearance it is a large brownish jelly-like blob that

is nearly indistinguishable from the muddy water it

inhabits. Typically, the Mud Slime will lie in wait

for large prey to stumble into the stream or pool in

which it dwells. Then it will strike – flowing like

liquid over the body of its victim, working its way

up their chest and neck, before reaching their

mouth and nose and pouring deep down into their

throat and nostrils, suffocating them completely.

Once the victim’s body sinks to the bo;om, the

Mud Slime will then digest it at its leisure.

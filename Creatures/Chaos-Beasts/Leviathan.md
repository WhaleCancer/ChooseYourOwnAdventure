# Leviathan

**Type:** Chaos beast / Primeval monster  
**Origin:** Biblical / Primeval chaos  
**Era:** Ancient, potentially active in 1985

## Overview

**Leviathan** is a beast from the biblical Book of Job, and is a form of the primeval chaos-monster created by God at the beginning of creation. It is paired with the other chaos-monster, [[Creatures/Chaos-Beasts/Behemoth|Behemoth]], and according to later Jewish tradition both would become food for the righteous at the end-time.

Metaphorically, the name has come to be used for any extremely large or powerful entity.

## Nature

Leviathan represents the primeval chaos of the sea, a massive aquatic monster of immense power and destructive capability. In biblical tradition, it is one of the great chaos beasts created at the beginning of creation, representing the untamed forces of nature and the deep.

## Relationship with Other Elements

- **Behemoth:** Leviathan's counterpart, representing the chaos of the land
- **Biblical tradition:** Connected to the creation myths and end-time prophecies
- **Outsiders:** May be connected to the cosmic forces represented by the Outsiders/Elohim
- **Primeval forces:** Represents ancient, pre-human forces that existed before order

## In Play

Leviathan serves as:

- **Cosmic horror element** - A primeval force beyond human comprehension
- **Biblical/mythological connection** - Links to ancient religious traditions
- **Aquatic threat** - Massive sea monster encounters
- **End-time prophecy** - Connection to apocalyptic scenarios
- **Metaphorical entity** - May represent other massive threats or forces

## Mountain Orcs as Characters

**Source:** AFF Advanced Fighting Fantasy 2nd Ed - New Races Rules

Mountain Orcs can be played as player characters using the following racial profile:

### Starting Profile

- **SKILL:** 5
- **STAMINA:** 7
- **LUCK:** 8

### Characteristic Bonuses

- None (base value matches benchmark)

### Starting Talents

- **Dark Seeing** (Minor Talent) - Can see in complete darkness
- **Survivor (mountains/hills)** (Minor Talent) - Advantage in mountainous and hilly terrain

### Racial Special Skills

- **Bow**
- **Mountain Lore**
- **Awareness**

### Languages (Free)

- **Native:** Orcish
- **Common:** Common Tongue

### Special Skill Choices

Three Special Skills at 2 points, Six Special Skills at 1 point

### Social Class

0 or 1 (Mountain Orcs are evil and hostile, rarely accepted in civilized society)

### Design Point Allocation (8 points total)

- Languages: Free (not counted)
- Dark Seeing (Minor Talent): 2 points
- Survivor (mountains/hills) (Minor Talent): 2 points
- 3 Racial Special Skills: 3 points
- **Total: 8 points**

### Design Notes

Mountain Orcs have a base value of 8 (SKILL 5 + STAMINA 3), which exactly matches the benchmark of 8. This grants them the standard 6 design points, but they require 8 points for their full specialized build. Their high starting SKILL (5) and STAMINA (7) reflect their battle-hardened nature and ferocity. Their mountain adaptation is reflected in their Survivor (mountains/hills) talent and Mountain Lore skill. Their preference for swords, axes, spears, and longbows is represented by their Bow skill and combat focus. Their higher intelligence (Average-High) and organized tribal structure are reflected in their Awareness skill.

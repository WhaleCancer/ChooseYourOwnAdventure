- **REACTION:** Hostile

## Description

THE CLEAVER is a terrifying reptilian creature, over two and a half metres tall, hunched and stooped, covered in tough greenish-black scales. It lives in underground caverns and forgotten caves, stalking humans and other prey. Its weapon is a massive blood-encrusted iron cleaver.

The Cleaver is rarely encountered but is infamous in southern Allansia.

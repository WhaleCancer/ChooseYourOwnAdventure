## Description

In the dank, forbidding depths of dungeons deep below the ground, many perils stalk the darkness in search of their next meal. The **Night Stalkers** were perhaps human once, but they are something less than human now. Bent over from their endless wanderings in the oppressive tunnels, they are sinewy beings, covered in folds of leathery grey skin.

## Description

Grub, the TREMOR WORM is a gigantic insect

known from the grassy plains and barren flatlands

of the Old World. They are monstrous worm-like

creatures, hence their appellation, about the size of

a hay-cart or even larger and looking like a cross

between a white caterpillar and a maggot. Tremor

Worms eat anything they can sniff out with weird

dome-like olfactory organs on their eyeless heads,

bite through plate armour. They hunt in small

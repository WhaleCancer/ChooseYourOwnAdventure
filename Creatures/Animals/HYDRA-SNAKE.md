## Description

The opinions of scholars and sages are divided on

the origins of the dreaded HYDRA-SNAKE. Some

contend it is indeed the offspring of both Hydra

and snake, though others claim it instead represents

a stable population of heavily mutated Serpent.

Regardless, its appearance is remarkably uniform,

being a scaly reptile with a ridge of spines down its

back and a great black serpent-like body. The

Hydra-Snake takes its name from its two heads,

both of which bear rows of serrated teeth, though

unlike true Hydras it will never have more than

two heads. It is found across southern Allansia,

from sandy deserts to stinking marshes, and its

usual tactic is to wait under the surface of the

dunes, or swamp, until prey approaches. At this

point it will erupt from the ground and lunge

forward, a;acking its victim with both heads.

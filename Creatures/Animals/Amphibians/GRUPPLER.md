## Description

Thought to be related to the Slykk of the northern

fens, the GRUPPLERS are a tree-dwelling race of

creatures found only in the deepest jungles and

swamps of southern Allansia. They are a metre tall,

half amphibian and half human in appearance, with

yellow-green frog-like skin and bulging eyes. Their

bodies

are

covered

in

strange

orange,

furry

filaments, and their mouths of full of numerous tiny

triangular teeth. Grupplers favour wooden spears

and stone knives as weapons, and these are also

used to butcher whatever they can catch for food.

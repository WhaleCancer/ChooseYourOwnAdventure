# Energy Leech

**Source:** CB77019 - Return to the Pit, Page 122
**Type:** Creature

## Statistics

- **SKILL:** 1
- **STAMINA:** 1
- **ATTACKS:** 1
- **WEAPON:** Energy Drain (see below)
- **ARMOUR:** None
- **DAMAGE MODIFIER:** None
- **HABITAT:** Marshes, Rivers, Sea-shore
- **NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-6
- **TYPE:** Animal
- **REACTION:** Neutral
- **INTELLIGENCE:** Low-None

## Description

The ENERGY LEECH is a rare kind of leech that

subsists on the magical stamina and auras of its

victims, rather than drinking their blood. It looks a

like a gigantic red slug, as big as a man’s fist, and

will latch on to its victims unobtrusively, as they

wade through its watery home. The Energy Leech

will then suck vital resources from their host’s

body, who must immediately roll a die and lose

that many STAMINA points. It may be removed just

before it commences sucking, by either tearing it

off, causing 1 STAMINA damage, or striking it with a

knife or dagger requiring a successful Test for Luck

(see also Out of the Pit, p. 73). Unlike the Giant

Leech however, the Energy Leech is immune to salt,

and indeed, can be found in shallow sea-waters and

shore-line caves that are otherwise anathema to the

creature’s relatives. Indeed, it is rumoured that

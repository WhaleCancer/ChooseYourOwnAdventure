The WIND SPRITES are cousins of the Sea Sprites,

and children of the wind god Pangara. They hail

from the Elemental Plane of Air but have been

encountered on Titan, flying through the air, riding

the breezes of the hill-valleys, the great mountain

gales and even the thrashing tempests of the seas.

In appearance they are humanoid in form but

comprised entirely of swirling nebulous gases with

two glowing yellow motes for eyes. Wind Sprites

communicate in breathy gusts of air and react

neutrally to people, though they are met but rarely

and always in isolated, lonely places.

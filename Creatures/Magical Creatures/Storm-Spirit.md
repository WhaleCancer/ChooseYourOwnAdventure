## Description

Any mariner worth their salt knows the storms that

rage across the seas and oceans of Titan are best

avoided if possible. This is not just because of their

power to wreck ships, but also because the black

bands of clouds that race across the sky may

harbour the dreaded STORM SPIRITS. These are

fierce elemental creatures that consider themselves

the children of the raging tempests. Storm Spirits

appear as crackling electrical humanoids of pure

energy. Despite being wingless, they are capable of

flying at terrifying speeds, racing down to a;ack

the crew of ships trapped at the mercy of a storm.

These beings strike with talons like coruscating

barbs of electricity. Also during combat they can

unleash up to two energy bolts of lightning at any

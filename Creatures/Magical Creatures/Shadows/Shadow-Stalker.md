## Description

The SHADOW STALKER is an evil fiend from

another dimension, trapped on Titan until it has

harvested its fair share of the souls of the innocent,

slain by its own shadowy blade. The creature has

two forms: inert, where it resembles a small pile of

black cloth, and aggressively active. In this guise it

will spring into the air and disappear, concealing

itself within the shadow of its victim. Then the

Shadow Stalker will strike! Revealing itself as a

spindly humanoid with a snaggle-toothed grin and

armed with a long dagger, it will strike at the

shadow of its victim, with each hit causing normal

damage. Daylight, and multiple light sources that

cause it to fade will make the Shadow Stalker flee.

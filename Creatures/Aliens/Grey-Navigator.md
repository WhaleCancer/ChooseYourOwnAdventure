# Grey Navigator

**Type:** Grey specialist / Pilot  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

**Grey Navigators** are the pilots of the flying saucers. A Navigator will typically have *[Mental Pilot](./Mental Pilot.md)* active and be connected to the flying saucer it is assigned to.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 8  
**PSIONICS:** 7  
**PSI POINTS:** 8  
**HABITAT:** Aboard their flying saucers  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3  
**TYPE:** Humanoid  
**REACTION:** Friendly-Hostile (but see below)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** None (but see below)

## Abilities

### Mental Pilot

These are the pilots of the flying saucers. A Navigator will typically have *Mental Pilot* active and be connected to the flying saucer it is assigned to. This psionic ability allows the Navigator to:

- **Control the craft** - Directly pilot the UFO through psionic connection
- **Interface with systems** - Operate all craft systems through mental connection
- **Enhanced performance** - Achieve flight characteristics impossible for normal pilots
- **Remote operation** - Potentially control craft from a distance

## In Play

Grey Navigators serve as:

- **UFO pilots** - Operating flying saucers and other alien craft
- **Craft control** - Directing UFO movements and operations
- **System operation** - Managing all craft systems through psionic connection
- **Mission coordination** - Leading abduction or other operations
- **Combat encounters** - Piloting craft in combat situations

### Game Considerations

- Navigators are essential for UFO operations
- The Mental Pilot ability creates unique piloting mechanics
- The psionic connection to craft suggests advanced technology
- Destroying a Navigator may disable or crash the craft
- The enhanced psychic scream reflects their importance

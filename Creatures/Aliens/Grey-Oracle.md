# Grey Oracle

**Type:** Grey specialist / Intelligence and prediction  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

**Grey Oracles** are specialized Greys with enhanced psionic abilities focused on remote sensing, prediction, aura reading, and sensing the presence of specific people. They typically work alongside the Commander, with smaller vessels having none and larger vessels having multiple.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 8  
**PSIONICS:** 7  
**PSI POINTS:** 8  
**HABITAT:** Aboard their flying saucers  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3  
**TYPE:** Humanoid  
**REACTION:** Friendly-Hostile (but see below)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** None (but see below)

## Abilities

### Psionic Powers

An Oracle is able to:

- **Remote sense** - Perceive distant locations or objects
- **Predict the future** - See potential future events
- **Read auras** - Perceive the emotional or spiritual state of individuals
- **Sense specific people** - Locate and track specific individuals

## Role in Operations

Oracles typically work alongside the Commander, providing:

- **Intelligence** - Information about targets, locations, and threats
- **Prediction** - Warning of potential dangers or opportunities
- **Targeting** - Locating specific individuals for abduction or other operations
- **Strategic planning** - Providing insights for mission planning

## In Play

Grey Oracles serve as:

- **Intelligence gathering** - Collecting information through psionic means
- **Prediction** - Warning of future events or threats
- **Target location** - Finding specific individuals for operations
- **Strategic support** - Providing insights for mission planning
- **Combat intelligence** - Perceiving threats and opportunities in combat

### Game Considerations

- Oracles are intelligence specialists, not combat-focused
- The prediction abilities create opportunities for dramatic reveals
- The remote sensing creates investigation challenges
- The connection to Commanders suggests high-level operations
- The enhanced psychic scream reflects their importance

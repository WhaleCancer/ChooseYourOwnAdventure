## Description

seven feet tall on a dozen slime-trailing tentacles,

with pitch-black glistening skin, that shuffles about

dungeon passages in search of prey to suck the

brains out of. Equip it with a band of waving, sting-

tipped tentacles, and a ring of eyes that allows the

monstrosity to look in all directions at once (so the

creature can never be surprised), and you have a

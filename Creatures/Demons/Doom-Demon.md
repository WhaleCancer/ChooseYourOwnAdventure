## Description

and all mankind fears him greatly. He rides a

flaming iron chariot, pulled by a huge two-headed

Devil Dog (or Xlaia), as strong as a cart-horse and

u;erly loyal to its master. The Doom Demon can

only stalk the land at night, when he rides out and

roams the Desert of Skulls, up to the southern

banks of the Whitewater River, where his domain

ends. It is believed he is tied to the desert in some

way – perhaps his mortal form once lived there,

many oceans ago, before the War of the Wizards.

During the day the Doom Demon must lie dormant

in his Castle of Black Steel, at the fiery edge of Hell.

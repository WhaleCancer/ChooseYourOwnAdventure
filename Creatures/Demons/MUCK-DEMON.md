## Statistics

- **SKILL:** 8
- **STAMINA:** 12
- **LUCK:** 11
- **Intelligence:** Average
- **Natural Weapon:** Small Claw
- **Natural Armour:** None
- **Carrying Capacity:** 10

## Description

Muck Demons are made of mud, slime, and filth. They can be summoned using a precious gem as a "heart" or form spontaneously. They are often used as guards in dirty locations. They are roughly humanoid, their form and clothing are compacted earth and slime, and they smell very bad.

## Special Abilities

- The Muck Demon can animate dust, mud, and detritus, causing a -3 penalty to Attack Skill for anyone fighting it in hand-to-hand combat.
- Muck Demons' bad smell gives them a -2 penalty to any stealth attempt.

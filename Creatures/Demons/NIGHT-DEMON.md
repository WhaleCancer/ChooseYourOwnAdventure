## Statistics

- **SKILL:** 14
- **STAMINA:** 27
- **LUCK:** 5
- **Intelligence:** High
- **Natural Weapon:** Large Claws
- **Natural Armour:** Medium
- **Carrying Capacity:** 16

## Description

There are four Night Demons who are minor Princes of Hell, used by their greater brethren as messengers, envoys, and commanders. They appear approximately 10 feet tall with a red-skinned, muscled, vaguely human body. Its hands and feet are scaled and clawed, and it possesses two black dragon-like heads. It also has bat-like wings that allow it to fly, albeit slowly.

## Special Abilities

- These Demons are incredibly strong and inflict double the rolled damage in hand to hand combat.
- Each round a Night Demon can project a Fire Bolt that hits on a roll of 1-3 on a D6. This bolt will inflict 1D6 STAMINA damage and armour does not apply.
- An attack on a Night Demon has a -2 penalty on all damage rolls made by non-magical weapons.
- A Night Demon has huge bat wings that allows it to fly.

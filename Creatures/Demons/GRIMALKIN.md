## Description

The GRIMALKIN is a dire Imp of sorts, often

summoned to Titan from its home in the Demonic

Pit, to act as a familiar for a witch or wizard. In

appearance it is a tiny humanoid creature, covered

in red fur, with a lashing tail, clawed hands and

stubby horns. The Grimalkin’s face is snarling and

bestial, with an ugly snout, and framed by pointed

ears. It is typically bidden with instructions to cause

mayhem and mischief at the behest of its magic-

using master. This is because the claws of the

Grimalkin taint everything it touches, causing milk

to curdle, food to spoil, and fruit and vegetables to

rot and decay. The creature’s tainted touch also

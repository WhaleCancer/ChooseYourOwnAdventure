**Type:** Creature

> **Note:** These statistics represent player character Demon forms from Demons of Doom. Enemy Demons may use different statistics from other sources.

## Statistics

- **SKILL:** 8
- **STAMINA:** 8
- **LUCK:** 11
- **Intelligence:** High
- **Natural Weapon:** Small Claw
- **Natural Armour:** None
- **Carrying Capacity:** 3

## Description

These are intelligent, stubborn, and willful familiars summoned by wizards. They are physically described as being a foot tall, with green scaled skin, bat-like wings, horns, and claws, moving with a jerky motion.

## Special Abilities

- Homonculi can fly for short periods.
- Enemies attacking a Homonculus suffer a -1 penalty to their SKILL due to erratic movement.
- Homonculi have a -1 penalty to all combat damage rolls, except for spell attacks.

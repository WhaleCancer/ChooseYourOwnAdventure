## Statistics

- **SKILL:** 8
- **STAMINA:** 13
- **LUCK:** 9
- **Intelligence:** High
- **Natural Weapon:** Scimitar
- **Natural Armour:** Leather Cuirass and Small Shield
- **Carrying Capacity:** 12

## Description

These Demons serve as guards for Demon Princes, equipped with scimitars, armor, and shields upon summoning, which they can recreate if lost. They are patient and can enter a trance-like state. Physically, they have a skeletal spider-like lower body with six to eight legs, and their upper bodies resemble warped and twisted zombies, being dense, heavy, and tough.

## Special Abilities

- Their tough nature reduces all damage inflicted upon them by 1 STAMINA point.
- Their spider legs grant a +3 bonus to all climbing tests.

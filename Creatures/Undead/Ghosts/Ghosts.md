# Ghosts

**Source:** CB77008 - Beyond The Pit, Pages 57-58
**Type:** Undead Creatures

---

## Overview

**Ghosts** are spirits of the dead; non-corporeal entities trapped between the Spirit Plane and our own world, until some deed or action is completed that will allowed them to be released from their torment. They usually appear as glowing apparitions of how they were in life, or at the moment of their own death, translucent figures in ancient garb, radiating a chilling aura and wailing at their tragic plight.

Unlike Spectres and Wraiths, many Ghosts have no hatred for the living, and will gladly exchange scraps of lore or omens of fortune for a means of liberation from their existence. However, some Ghosts have begun to succumb to the evils of undeath, and will attack immediately.

---

## Ghost Types

### [Common Ghost](Common-Ghost.md)

The standard **Common Ghost**, found in dungeons, ruins, caves, and wilderness areas. They can be neutral and helpful, or unfriendly and hostile, depending on their nature and circumstances.

**Key Features:**

- Can be helpful or hostile
- May seek adventurers to complete quests
- Can curse those who refuse (loses 2 LUCK and 1 SKILL)
- Only harmed by silver, enchanted weapons, or magic
- Drain strength rather than cause physical wounds

### [Ice Ghost](Ice-Ghost.md)

Also known as Snow Ghosts, these merciless fiends are confined to the wintery northern wilderness, though some rogue individuals are found further south.

**Key Features:**

- Always hostile
- Attack with freezing cold touch
- First touch causes hypothermia (can be saved with healing)
- Second touch is instantly fatal
- Victims become Ice Ghosts themselves
- Presence preceded by cold, howling wind

### [Sulphur Ghost](Sulphur-Ghost.md)

Rare nebulous beings that haunt volcanic wastelands and rock-strewn wildernesses, reputed to be the spirits of unfortunate wanderers who died in desolate places.

**Key Features:**

- Always hostile
- Resemble discoloured puffs of smoke
- Attack with suffocating poisonous fumes
- Cause 2 STAMINA damage per Attack Round
- Armour provides no protection
- Skip about in the air before attacking

## Common Traits

All Ghost types share these common characteristics:

- **Type:** Undead
- **Armour:** None
- **Damage Modifier:** None
- **Vulnerabilities:** Can only be harmed by silver, enchanted weapons, magic spells, or priest powers (Ward/Banish)
- **Protection:** Repelled by Spirit Doors (blackwood doors with silver sigils)
- **Capture:** Can be captured by Spirit Snares (silver bear-traps) and transferred to silver flasks
- **Running Water:** Some Ghosts cannot cross running water except via a bridge
- **Holy Water:** Can be injured by Holy Water (1-6 STAMINA damage if successfully thrown)

## Special Abilities

### Ghostly Touch (Common Ghost)

Ghosts drain strength rather than cause physical wounds. The damage depends on the number of hits:

| Number of Hits | SKILL lost | STAMINA lost | LUCK lost |
|----------------|------------|--------------|-----------|
| 1-2 | 1 | 2 | 1 |
| 3-4 | 2 | 4 | 2 |
| 5-6 | 4 | 8 | 3 |
| 7-8 | 7 | 14 | 4 |
| 9-10 | 11 | 22 | 5 |

Anyone hit more than ten times by a Ghost is automatically slain.

### Ice Touch (Ice Ghost)

Anyone hit once by an Ice Ghost will begin shiver uncontrollably, turn deathly white and lapse into unconsciousness. If immediately Healed by a Priest, or the cold is drawn from their body by placing certain magical stones upon their forehead, they will survive with the loss of 2 STAMINA points. Otherwise, they will die of hypothermia. A second touch by an Ice Ghost is instantly fatal! The body of anyone slain by an Ice Ghost will then vaporise, becoming an Ice Ghost themself.

### Sulphur Touch (Sulphur Ghost)

The sulphurous touch automatically causes 2 STAMINA points of damage per Attack Round with armour providing no protection. Some adventurers claim that nose plugs provide the only defence against such an attack, but this has yet to be successfully verified.

## Recovery and Weaknesses

- **STAMINA and LUCK:** Can be restored through normal means
- **SKILL Points:** Return at the rate of 1 point per 30 minutes if resting completely, or 1 point per hour if not resting
- **Holy Water:** Causes 1-6 STAMINA points damage if successfully thrown
- **Spirit Door:** Prevents entry of any non-corporeal undead
- **Spirit Snare:** Modified bear-trap on a chain made from silver; caught Ghosts can be transferred to a silver flask
- **Ancestral Appeal:** Some Ghosts with strong ties to an area may be quietened with an appeal to one's ancestors, if they hail from the same place

## Lore and Traditions

- **Trial of Ghosts:** An arbitrary adjudication where the spirits of the living are caught in the Dream World, and are judged on matters of luck by three old ghostly crones in a manner reminiscent of a witchcraft trial
- **Night of the Ancestors:** A celebration in Titan
- **Spirit-Watching:** The evening before the Night of the Ancestors

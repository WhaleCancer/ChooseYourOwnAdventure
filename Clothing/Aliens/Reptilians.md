# Reptilians

**Type:** Alien species / Conspiracy entity  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

**Reptilians** are commonly mentioned in conspiracy theories, particularly in the United States and United Kingdom. They are described as humanoid reptiles, often associated with shapeshifting abilities and infiltration of human society.

## Characteristics

Reptilians are typically described as:

- **Humanoid reptiles** - Bipedal creatures with reptilian features
- **Shapeshifting** - Ability to appear human
- **Infiltration** - Operating within human society
- **Conspiracy** - Part of larger conspiracies and cover-ups
- **Malevolent** - Generally portrayed as hostile or manipulative

## Distribution

### North America

- **Greys** are the most commonly reported alien in North American abduction cases
- **Reptilians** are commonly mentioned in conspiracy theories
- Reports are more common in conspiracy literature than direct encounters

### United Kingdom

- **Reptilians** are commonly mentioned in conspiracy theories (David Icke, etc.)
- More associated with conspiracy theories than direct encounters

### South America

- Some limited reports of Reptilians
- Chupacabra - Reports of alien-like creatures attacking livestock

## Theories

### Conspiracy Theories

Reptilians are often associated with:

- **Government infiltration** - Shapeshifters controlling governments
- **Bloodline manipulation** - Controlling human bloodlines
- **Ancient presence** - Having been on Earth for millennia
- **Hybrid programs** - Creating human-reptilian hybrids

### The Truth

> [!Danger] The Truth
> The exact nature of Reptilians in the EPIC setting may vary. They could be:
> - Another type of alien species (distinct from Greys)
> - Shapeshifting entities related to Outsiders
> - Hybrid beings created through genetic engineering
> - Misidentified Greys or other entities
> - Pure conspiracy theory with no basis in reality

## In Play

Reptilians serve as:

- **Conspiracy elements** - Part of larger conspiracy theories
- **Infiltration threats** - Shapeshifters operating in human society
- **Government manipulation** - Controlling or influencing governments
- **Investigation targets** - Cases involving shapeshifting or infiltration
- **Moral ambiguity** - Unclear whether real or conspiracy theory

### Game Considerations

- Reptilians provide a different type of alien threat than Greys
- The shapeshifting angle creates paranoia and infiltration scenarios
- The conspiracy theory connection adds layers of uncertainty
- The government infiltration angle creates political thriller elements
- The ambiguity (real vs. conspiracy) creates investigation opportunities

# Grey Terrorist

**Type:** Grey specialist / Combat operations  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

**Grey Terrorists** are specialized Greys focused on hostile operations and combat. They are equipped with advanced weapons and trained for aggressive missions.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 8  
**PSIONICS:** 7  
**PSI POINTS:** 8  
**HABITAT:** Aboard their flying saucers  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3  
**TYPE:** Humanoid  
**REACTION:** Friendly-Hostile (but see below)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** None (but see below)

## Equipment

Grey Terrorists are equipped with:

- **Grey Laser Pistol** - Advanced energy weapon
- **Grey Laser Rifle** - More powerful energy weapon

## In Play

Grey Terrorists serve as:

- **Combat specialists** - Aggressive operations and hostile encounters
- **Terror missions** - Large-scale operations to create fear and chaos
- **Assassination** - Eliminating specific targets
- **Sabotage** - Destroying infrastructure or equipment
- **Combat encounters** - Dangerous opponents in firefights

### Game Considerations

- Terrorists are combat-focused and highly dangerous
- The advanced weapons create significant threats
- The terror mission angle connects to emotional harvesting
- The combat focus makes them primary antagonists in action scenarios
- The connection to larger operations suggests coordinated attacks

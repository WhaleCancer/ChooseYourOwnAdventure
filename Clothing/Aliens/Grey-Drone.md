# Grey Drone

**Type:** Grey basic unit / Worker  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

**Grey Drones** are the basic worker units of Grey operations. They are the most common type of Grey encountered and perform general tasks aboard flying saucers and during operations.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 8  
**HABITAT:** Aboard their flying saucers  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 2-7 (1d6+1)  
**TYPE:** Humanoid  
**REACTION:** Friendly-Hostile (but see below)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** None (but see below)

## Characteristics

Grey Drones are:

- **Basic units** - Most common type of Grey
- **Workers** - Perform general tasks and operations
- **Crafting aptitude** - Skilled at technical and mechanical tasks
- **Non-combat focused** - Not specialized for combat operations

## In Play

Grey Drones serve as:

- **Basic encounters** - Most common Grey type in operations
- **Workers** - Performing tasks aboard craft or at facilities
- **Crafting** - Technical and mechanical operations
- **Support** - Assisting specialized Greys in their operations
- **Cannon fodder** - Basic units in combat encounters

### Game Considerations

- Drones are the most common Grey type
- Their crafting aptitude makes them useful for technical operations
- They are less dangerous individually but appear in larger numbers
- The basic psychic scream is less powerful than specialized types
- They provide a baseline for understanding Grey capabilities

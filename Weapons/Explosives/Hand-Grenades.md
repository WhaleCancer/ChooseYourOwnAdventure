# Hand Grenades

**Various Manufacturers**  
**Type:** Fragmentation / explosive grenades

Military hand grenades, primarily fragmentation types used by Canadian and Commonwealth forces. In 1985 British Columbia, encountered almost exclusively with military personnel, special operations units, or in highly unusual circumstances involving stolen military equipment. Hand grenades are prohibited weapons for civilians. The presence of hand grenades indicates serious military operations, organized crime with military connections, or highly dangerous situations. Most commonly encountered types would be Canadian or British military issue fragmentation grenades.

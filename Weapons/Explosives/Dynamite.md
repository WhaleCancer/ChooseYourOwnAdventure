# Dynamite

**Manufacturer:** Various (commercial explosives manufacturers)  
**Type:** Commercial explosive

Commercial dynamite used for mining, construction, and demolition. In 1985 British Columbia, dynamite would be available to licensed individuals in mining, construction, or demolition industries. Encountered with legitimate users in rural areas, mining operations, or construction sites. Can also be encountered in criminal hands through theft from legitimate sources or illegal acquisition. The presence of dynamite indicates either legitimate industrial activity or serious criminal intent. Requires proper licensing for legal possession, but theft or illegal acquisition makes it available to criminals.

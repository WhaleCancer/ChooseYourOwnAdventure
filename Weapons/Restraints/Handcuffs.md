# Handcuffs

**Manufacturer:** Various (Smith & Wesson, Peerless, etc.)  
**Type:** Restraint device

Metal restraints used by police to secure suspects. In 1985 British Columbia, handcuffs are standard equipment for all police officers. Available in various designs including chain-link, hinged, or rigid styles. Encountered with all police officers during arrests or when securing suspects. Handcuffs are essential police equipment, and their presence is routine in any police encounter involving an arrest. Can also be encountered in criminal hands if stolen from police or acquired illegally. The presence of handcuffs indicates police operations, arrests, or, rarely, criminal use for kidnapping or restraint.

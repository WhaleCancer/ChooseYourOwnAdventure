# C6 General Purpose Machine Gun

**Manufacturer:** Fabrique Nationale (FN)  
**Caliber:** 7.62×51mm NATO  
**Type:** General purpose machine gun (belt-fed)

The Canadian designation for the FN MAG (Mitrailleuse d'Appui Général), adopted in the late 1970s and standard issue by 1985. A belt-fed, air-cooled machine gun capable of sustained fire. Chambered in 7.62×51mm NATO with a rate of fire of approximately 650–1,000 rounds per minute. The C6 serves in both infantry support roles (with bipod) and vehicle-mounted configurations (with tripod). In 1985 British Columbia, encountered primarily with Canadian military units, especially infantry sections, armored vehicles, or fixed defensive positions. The C6 represents the primary sustained fire capability of Canadian forces. Not available to civilians, and extremely rare in criminal hands due to size, weight, and difficulty of acquisition.

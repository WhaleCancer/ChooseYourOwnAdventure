# Rifle Scopes

**Manufacturer:** Various (Leupold, Redfield, Bushnell, etc.)  
**Type:** Optical sighting device

Telescopic sights mounted on rifles for improved accuracy at range. In 1985 British Columbia, rifle scopes would be commonly encountered on hunting rifles, sniper rifles, and some combat rifles. Available in various magnifications from low-power (2-4x) for hunting to high-power (10x and above) for precision shooting. Encountered with hunters, military snipers, police marksmen, or anyone requiring precision shooting. Rifle scopes significantly improve accuracy at longer ranges and are essential for sniper operations. The presence of scoped rifles indicates serious shooting requirements, whether for hunting, law enforcement, or military purposes.

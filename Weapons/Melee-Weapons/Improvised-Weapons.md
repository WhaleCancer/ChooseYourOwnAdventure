# Improvised Weapons

**Various Sources**  
**Type:** Various (clubs, pipes, tools, etc.)

Weapons improvised from everyday objects. In 1985 British Columbia, improvised weapons could include baseball bats, tire irons, pipes, tools, or any object used as a weapon. Encountered with criminals, individuals in altercations, or anyone who needs a weapon but doesn't have access to firearms. Improvised weapons are common in criminal contexts, bar fights, or situations where individuals need to defend themselves without access to proper weapons. The presence of improvised weapons indicates spontaneous violence, criminal activity, or situations where proper weapons are not available.

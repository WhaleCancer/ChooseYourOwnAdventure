# Hunting Knives

**Manufacturer:** Various  
**Type:** Fixed-blade knives

Knives designed for hunting, field dressing, and outdoor use. In 1985 British Columbia, hunting knives are extremely common among hunters, outdoorsmen, and rural residents. Available in various sizes and designs, from small skinning knives to large Bowie-style knives. Encountered with virtually all hunters and many rural residents. Hunting knives are essential tools for field dressing game and general outdoor use. The presence of hunting knives is routine and expected in hunting contexts, though they can also be used as weapons if necessary.

# Ruger Security Six

**Manufacturer:** Sturm, Ruger & Co.  
**Caliber:** .357 Magnum / .38 Special  
**Type:** Revolver

A popular civilian revolver known for its rugged construction and ability to fire both .357 Magnum and .38 Special rounds. Widely available to licensed firearms owners in British Columbia. Often encountered in the hands of private security, hunters, or civilians who prefer a more powerful sidearm than the standard police .38 Special.

# Intratec TEC-9

**Type:** Submachine gun / Machine pistol  
**Era:** 1985  
**Caliber:** 9mm

## Overview

The **Intratec TEC-9** is a semiautomatic pistol that is regularly illegally converted to full automatic. As such, it is popular with both criminals and cultists wanting to pack a punch in a small package.

This weapon was a new hot weapon introduced in the 1980s; easy to convert to automatic, used by gangs and cults for that purpose.

## Characteristics

- **Damage Track:** Light Pistol
- **Ammunition:** 9mm
- **Magazine Capacity:** 20, 32, or 50 rounds
- **Rate of Fire:** Semiautomatic (can be illegally converted to full automatic)

## In Play

The TEC-9 serves as:

- **Criminal weapon** - Popular with gangs and organized crime
- **Cult weapon** - Used by cults and extremist groups
- **Illegal conversion** - Easy to convert to full automatic, making it dangerous
- **Scenario element** - Presence indicates serious criminal activity or cult involvement

### Game Considerations

- The TEC-9's reputation as a "gang weapon" makes it distinctive
- Illegal full-auto conversions make it particularly dangerous
- Its presence suggests organized crime or cult activity
- Popular culture association (Miami Vice) adds flavor

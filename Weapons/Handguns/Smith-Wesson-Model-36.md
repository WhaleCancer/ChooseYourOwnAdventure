# Smith & Wesson Model 36

**Manufacturer:** Smith & Wesson  
**Caliber:** .38 Special  
**Type:** Revolver (Compact)

A compact, snub-nosed revolver popular with plainclothes officers, detectives, and civilians seeking a concealable sidearm. The "Chief's Special" is a five-shot revolver that fits easily in a pocket or ankle holster. Commonly encountered as backup weapons or personal defense firearms.

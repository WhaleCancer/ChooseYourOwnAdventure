# Walther PPK

**Type:** Pistol  
**Era:** 1985  
**Caliber:** .380 ACP / 9mm Kurz

## Overview

The **Walther PPK** is a compact and easily concealable handgun iconic and practical for an agent needing self-defense without drawing attention. Its association with espionage in popular culture adds a layer of intrigue.

## Characteristics

- **Damage Track:** Light Pistol
- **Ammunition:** .380 ACP (9mm Kurz)
- **Magazine Capacity:** 6 or 7 rounds
- **Concealability:** Excellent - compact design ideal for concealed carry

## In Play

The Walther PPK serves as:

- **Concealed carry weapon** - Ideal for agents needing discrete self-defense
- **Espionage weapon** - Associated with spies and intelligence operatives
- **Backup weapon** - Compact size makes it a good secondary weapon
- **Cultural reference** - Iconic weapon from James Bond and spy fiction

### Game Considerations

- The PPK's compact size makes it ideal for undercover operations
- Its association with espionage adds flavor to intelligence scenarios
- Limited magazine capacity requires careful shot placement
- Popular culture recognition makes it memorable

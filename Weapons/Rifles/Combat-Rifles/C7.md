# C7 Assault Rifle

**Manufacturer:** Diemaco (Colt Canada)  
**Caliber:** 5.56×45mm NATO  
**Type:** Assault rifle (selective fire)

Introduced in 1984, the C7 is a Canadian adaptation of the M16A1E1, becoming the new standard issue rifle for the Canadian Armed Forces. Features a heavy barrel and improved sights compared to the standard M16A1. Chambered in 5.56×45mm NATO with 30-round magazines. In 1985 British Columbia, the C7 is newly issued and increasingly common among Canadian military personnel as it replaces the older C1A1. The lighter cartridge and modern design represent the future of Canadian military small arms. Encountered with forward-deployed Canadian units or personnel who have received the new equipment.

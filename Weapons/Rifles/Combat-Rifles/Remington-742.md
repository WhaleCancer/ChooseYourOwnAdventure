# Remington Model 742

**Manufacturer:** Remington Arms  
**Caliber:** Various, including .30-06 Springfield, .308 Winchester, .280 Remington  
**Type:** Semi-automatic hunting rifle

A semi-automatic hunting rifle introduced in 1955, providing hunters with faster follow-up shots than bolt-action rifles. Chambered in various hunting calibers with a 4-round magazine (extended magazines available). The Model 742 was popular among hunters who wanted semi-automatic capability without the military appearance of assault rifles. In 1985 British Columbia, encountered with hunters who prefer semi-automatic rifles for big game hunting, especially in situations where quick follow-up shots might be necessary. Less common than bolt-action rifles but still widely available. The Model 742's semi-automatic action and hunting-oriented design make it a practical choice for serious hunters.

# Modified Hunting Rifles

**Various Manufacturers**  
**Various Calibers**  
**Type:** Various (typically bolt-action or semi-automatic hunting rifles)

Common hunting rifles that have been illegally modified for criminal use. Modifications might include sawed-off barrels or stocks for concealability, removal of serial numbers, or other alterations to make the weapon more suitable for criminal activity. In 1985 British Columbia, common hunting rifles like the Remington Model 700, Winchester Model 70, or semi-automatic hunting rifles could be modified and used criminally. These weapons are often stolen from legitimate owners and then altered. Encountered with criminals who have access to firearms but lack the connections or resources to acquire true military weapons. The modifications make these weapons illegal and their presence indicates criminal intent, though they lack the sophistication of true military firearms.

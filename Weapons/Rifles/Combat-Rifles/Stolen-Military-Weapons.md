# Stolen Military Weapons

**Various Manufacturers**  
**Various Calibers**  
**Type:** Various (typically C1A1, C2A1, or other military rifles)

Military weapons acquired through theft, illegal sale, or other criminal means. In 1985 British Columbia, the most common stolen military rifles would be C1A1 battle rifles, C2A1 automatic rifles, or occasionally C7 assault rifles if they had been recently issued. These weapons might be encountered with organized crime, serious criminals, or individuals with connections to military personnel. The presence of stolen military weapons indicates serious criminal activity, as these firearms are restricted and their acquisition requires illegal means. Encountered in contexts involving organized crime, armed robberies, or other serious criminal enterprises. The military origin of these weapons makes them particularly dangerous and indicates a high level of criminal sophistication.

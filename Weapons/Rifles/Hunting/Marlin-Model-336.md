# Marlin Model 336

**Manufacturer:** Marlin Firearms  
**Caliber:** .30-30 Winchester  
**Type:** Lever-action hunting rifle

Introduced in 1948, the Marlin Model 336 is a popular lever-action rifle among deer hunters. The side-ejection and solid top receiver make it suitable for mounting scopes, unlike many other lever-action rifles. The .30-30 Winchester cartridge is ideal for short to medium-range hunting in dense forests. Commonly encountered in British Columbia, especially in areas with thick brush where quick follow-up shots and maneuverability are valued. A classic American hunting rifle that remains popular.

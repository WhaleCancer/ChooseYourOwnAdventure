# Browning A-Bolt

**Manufacturer:** Browning  
**Caliber:** Various, including .30-06 Springfield, .270 Winchester, .308 Winchester  
**Type:** Bolt-action hunting rifle

Introduced in 1984, the Browning A-Bolt is a modern hunting rifle known for its accuracy and smooth bolt operation. Features a short 60-degree bolt lift and free-floating barrel. As a relatively new design in 1985, it would be encountered with hunters who prefer modern, high-quality firearms. Less common than the Winchester Model 70 or Remington Model 700 but appreciated by those who own one for its precision and innovative design.

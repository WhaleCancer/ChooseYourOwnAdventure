# Remington Model 700

**Manufacturer:** Remington Arms  
**Caliber:** Various, including .30-06 Springfield, .270 Winchester, .308 Winchester, .243 Winchester  
**Type:** Bolt-action hunting rifle

Introduced in 1962, the Remington Model 700 is renowned for its accuracy and versatility. Widely used by hunters, law enforcement, and military forces. The Model 700's strong action and wide range of available calibers have contributed to its popularity. In British Columbia, commonly encountered with hunters of all experience levels. Also serves as the basis for many sniper rifle conversions, making it versatile for both civilian and professional use.

# Weatherby Mark V

**Manufacturer:** Weatherby  
**Caliber:** Various, including .300 Weatherby Magnum, .270 Weatherby Magnum, .30-06 Springfield  
**Type:** Bolt-action hunting rifle

Introduced in 1958, the Weatherby Mark V is designed for high-velocity cartridges and features a distinctive nine-lug bolt and strong action. Known for its premium quality and long-range capabilities. The Mark V is a high-end hunting rifle, less common than more affordable options but encountered with serious hunters who pursue game at extended ranges or who simply prefer premium firearms. The Weatherby Magnum cartridges provide exceptional velocity and flat trajectories.

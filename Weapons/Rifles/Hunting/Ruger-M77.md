# Ruger M77

**Manufacturer:** Sturm, Ruger & Co.  
**Caliber:** Various, including .30-06 Springfield, .270 Winchester, .308 Winchester, .243 Winchester  
**Type:** Bolt-action hunting rifle

Introduced in 1968, the Ruger M77 is known for its ruggedness and reliability. Features a Mauser-style controlled-round feed and three-position safety. The M77's robust construction and reputation for durability make it popular with hunters who need a rifle that can handle harsh conditions. Commonly encountered in British Columbia, especially among hunters who value reliability over flashy features. The M77's solid construction appeals to those who view their rifle as a tool rather than a status symbol.

# Cooey Model 84 (Rifle)

**Manufacturer:** Cooey (Canadian manufacturer)  
**Caliber:** .22 LR, .22 WMR  
**Type:** Bolt-action rifle

A Canadian-made single-shot or bolt-action .22 caliber rifle, simple and affordable. Widely used for small game hunting, plinking, and as a first rifle for new shooters. The Model 84 rifle is the long gun counterpart to the Cooey Model 84 shotgun. Very common in rural British Columbia, especially among those who hunt small game or need an inexpensive, reliable rifle for pest control or target practice. The .22 LR version is extremely common and ammunition is readily available.

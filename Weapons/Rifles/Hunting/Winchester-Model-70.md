# Winchester Model 70

**Manufacturer:** Winchester Repeating Arms  
**Caliber:** Various, including .30-06 Springfield, .270 Winchester, .308 Winchester  
**Type:** Bolt-action hunting rifle

Often called the "Rifleman's Rifle," the Winchester Model 70 has been a staple among hunters since 1936. Known for its controlled-round feeding and robust design, it's a reliable choice for big game hunting. Commonly encountered in British Columbia among serious hunters pursuing deer, elk, moose, and bear. The Model 70's reputation for accuracy and reliability makes it a popular choice for those who can afford quality firearms.

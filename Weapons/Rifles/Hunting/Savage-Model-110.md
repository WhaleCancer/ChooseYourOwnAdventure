# Savage Model 110

**Manufacturer:** Savage Arms  
**Caliber:** Various, including .30-06 Springfield, .270 Winchester, .308 Winchester  
**Type:** Bolt-action hunting rifle

Introduced in 1958, the Savage Model 110 has been appreciated for its affordability and accuracy. Features a floating bolt head design that contributes to its reliability. Commonly encountered with budget-conscious hunters or those who value the rifle's performance-to-price ratio. Widely available and popular in British Columbia for hunting deer, elk, and other game. The Model 110's reputation for accuracy despite its lower cost makes it a common choice.

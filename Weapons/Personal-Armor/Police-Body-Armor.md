# Police Body Armor

**Manufacturer:** Various (Second Chance, Point Blank, etc.)  
**Type:** Soft body armor (Kevlar vest)

Soft body armor vests made from Kevlar or similar materials, designed to protect against handgun rounds. In 1985 British Columbia, police body armor was becoming increasingly common, with many RCMP and municipal police officers wearing Kevlar vests under their uniforms. These vests provide protection against most handgun rounds while remaining relatively lightweight and concealable. Encountered with all police officers as standard equipment, though adoption was still increasing during this period. The presence of body armor significantly improves officer survival in armed confrontations. Body armor was becoming standard issue but not yet universal across all police forces in 1985.

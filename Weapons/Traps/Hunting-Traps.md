# Hunting Traps

**Manufacturer:** Various  
**Type:** Leg-hold traps, snares, etc.

Traps used for legally trapping animals for fur or pest control. In 1985 British Columbia, hunting traps would be commonly encountered with trappers, fur harvesters, or individuals engaged in legal trapping activities. Available in various types including leg-hold traps, snares, and other designs. Encountered with legitimate trappers in rural areas, especially in northern British Columbia where trapping is a traditional activity. Hunting traps are legal tools when used properly and with appropriate licensing. The presence of hunting traps indicates legitimate trapping activities, though they can also be encountered in contexts involving illegal trapping or misuse.

# Stevens 311

**Manufacturer:** Stevens Arms  
**Gauge:** 12-gauge (most common), also 16, 20, and .410  
**Type:** Double-barrel shotgun (side-by-side)

A classic double-barrel side-by-side shotgun popular for hunting and sport shooting. Simple, reliable, and affordable. The side-by-side configuration is traditional for upland bird hunting. Commonly encountered with hunters, especially those pursuing game birds. Less common in law enforcement or tactical contexts, but a staple of civilian hunting in 1985 British Columbia.

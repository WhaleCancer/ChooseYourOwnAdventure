# Pancor Jackhammer

**Type:** Automatic shotgun  
**Era:** 1985 (prototype/concept)  
**Caliber:** 12 gauge

## Overview

The **Pancor Jackhammer** is a prototype automatic shotgun that was never fully produced, but exists as a concept weapon. In the EPIC universe, this weapon may have been developed further or exists as a rare prototype.

## Characteristics

- **Damage Track:** Shotgun
- **Ammunition:** 12 gauge
- **Magazine Capacity:** 10 rounds (rotary magazine)
- **Rate of Fire:** Fully automatic
- **Design:** Rotary magazine, bullpup configuration

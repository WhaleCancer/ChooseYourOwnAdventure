# Mossberg 500

**Manufacturer:** O.F. Mossberg & Sons  
**Gauge:** 12-gauge (most common), also 16, 20, and .410  
**Type:** Pump-action shotgun

A versatile and affordable pump-action shotgun popular with civilians, hunters, and some police departments. Known for its rugged construction and modular design, allowing for easy customization. Also used by U.S. military forces. Commonly encountered in 1985 British Columbia among civilian firearms owners, hunters, and occasionally with law enforcement or private security.

# Savage Model 24

**Manufacturer:** Savage Arms  
**Gauge:** .410, 20-gauge, or 12-gauge over rifle barrel  
**Type:** Combination gun (over/under shotgun and rifle)

An unusual combination gun featuring a shotgun barrel over a rifle barrel in an over/under configuration. Popular with hunters who want versatility in a single firearm. The rifle barrel is typically chambered in .22 LR, .22 WMR, or .30-30 Winchester. Less common than dedicated shotguns but occasionally encountered with hunters who value the combination of shotgun and rifle capabilities in one weapon.

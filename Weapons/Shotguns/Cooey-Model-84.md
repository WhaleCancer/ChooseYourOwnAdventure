# Cooey Model 84

**Manufacturer:** Cooey (Canadian manufacturer)  
**Gauge:** 12-gauge, 16-gauge, 20-gauge, .410  
**Type:** Single-shot break-action shotgun

A Canadian-made single-shot break-action shotgun, simple and affordable. Widely used by hunters, especially those on a budget or who prefer the simplicity of a single-shot design. Very common in rural British Columbia. The Model 84 was a popular entry-level shotgun and family firearm. Encountered frequently in hunting contexts or as a first firearm for new shooters.

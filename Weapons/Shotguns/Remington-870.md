# Remington 870

**Manufacturer:** Remington Arms  
**Gauge:** 12-gauge (most common), also available in 16, 20, 28, and .410  
**Type:** Pump-action shotgun

The most common shotgun encountered in 1985 British Columbia. Widely used by RCMP, municipal police forces, hunters, and civilians. A reliable, durable pump-action shotgun with a reputation for versatility and ease of maintenance. Also standard issue for U.S. military and law enforcement agencies, so may be encountered with American personnel as well. Available in various barrel lengths and configurations for different applications.

# Browning Auto-5

**Manufacturer:** Fabrique Nationale (FN) / Browning  
**Gauge:** 12-gauge (most common), also 16 and 20-gauge  
**Type:** Semi-automatic shotgun

A distinctive semi-automatic shotgun recognizable by its humpbacked receiver design. Popular among hunters and sport shooters for its reliable semi-automatic action. Less common than pump-action shotguns but still frequently encountered, especially among more serious hunters or competitive shooters. The Auto-5 was one of the first successful semi-automatic shotguns and remained popular decades after its introduction.

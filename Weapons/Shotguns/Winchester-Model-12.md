# Winchester Model 12

**Manufacturer:** Winchester Repeating Arms  
**Gauge:** 12-gauge (most common), also 16 and 20-gauge  
**Type:** Pump-action shotgun

A classic pump-action shotgun popular among hunters and sport shooters. Known for its smooth, reliable action and quality construction. While production ended in 1963, the Model 12 remained widely available and in use throughout 1985. Commonly encountered in rural areas, with hunters, or as family heirlooms. Less common in law enforcement use by this period, but still occasionally seen.

# Stun Guns / TASER

**Manufacturer:** Various (TASER was early in development in 1985)  
**Type:** Electroshock weapon

Electroshock weapons used to incapacitate targets through electrical discharge. In 1985 British Columbia, stun guns would be relatively new technology, primarily in the hands of specialized police units or private security. Early TASER devices existed but were not yet widespread. Encountered with specialized police units, private security, or, rarely, civilians with proper licensing. Stun guns represent cutting-edge less-lethal technology for the period, though they were not yet standard issue for regular police officers. The presence of stun guns indicates specialized equipment or forward-thinking law enforcement.

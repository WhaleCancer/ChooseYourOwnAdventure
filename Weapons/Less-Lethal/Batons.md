# Batons

**Manufacturer:** Various  
**Type:** Impact weapon

Police batons used for crowd control and self-defense. In 1985 British Columbia, batons are standard issue for police officers. Available in various forms including straight batons, expandable batons, or side-handle batons. Encountered with all police officers as standard equipment. Batons are less-lethal weapons used to subdue suspects or control crowds without using firearms. The presence of batons is routine with any police encounter. Some civilians may also possess batons for self-defense, though regulations may restrict their use.

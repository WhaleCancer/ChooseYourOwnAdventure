# Pepper Spray / Mace

**Manufacturer:** Various  
**Type:** Chemical irritant

Chemical sprays containing capsaicin or similar irritants used for self-defense and law enforcement. In 1985 British Columbia, pepper spray would be available to civilians for self-defense and used by some police forces as a less-lethal option. Available in various sizes from small personal defense canisters to larger law enforcement models. Encountered with civilians for personal protection, some police officers as an alternative to other less-lethal options, or criminals who have acquired it. Pepper spray is a common less-lethal weapon that can incapacitate targets without causing permanent harm. More accessible than other less-lethal options and widely available to civilians.

# Compound Crossbow

**Manufacturer:** Various  
**Type:** Compound crossbow

Modern crossbows with compound limbs and pulleys, providing higher power and accuracy than traditional crossbows. In 1985 British Columbia, compound crossbows would be relatively new technology, encountered with serious hunters or archery enthusiasts. The compound design allows for higher draw weights with less effort, making them more powerful than recurve crossbows. Encountered with specialized hunters who want the power of a crossbow with modern technology. Compound crossbows represent the cutting edge of crossbow technology for the period, offering improved performance over traditional designs. Less common than recurve crossbows but increasingly popular.

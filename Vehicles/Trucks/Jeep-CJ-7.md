# Jeep CJ-7 (1985)

**Type:** Off-road vehicle  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** Remote access, search and rescue, law enforcement, park operations

## Overview

Canada's answer to the mountain goat. Jeeps like the CJ-7 (or local AMC Eagle 4x4) took RCMP, park rangers, wildlife officers, and Search & Rescue teams into wild places that no other vehicle would go. Instantly recognizable, ragtop/no doors or hardtop.

## Modules

- **Winch [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** For personal rescue, logs, or recovery.
- **Rollbar [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Factory option, essential for safety!
- **Or swap for:** First Aid Kit, Tool Rack, or Extra Fuel.

## In Play

The Jeep CJ-7 serves as:

- **Off-road vehicle** - Access to remote locations inaccessible to other vehicles
- **Search and rescue** - Finding and rescuing people in wilderness areas
- **Law enforcement** - RCMP and park ranger operations
- **Wildlife management** - Park and wildlife officer transportation
- **Remote operations** - Access to logging roads, trails, and backcountry

### Game Considerations

- The CJ-7's off-road capabilities provide access to extremely remote locations
- The excellent agility makes it ideal for rough terrain
- The rollover risk adds danger to high-speed off-road chases
- The vehicle's association with official operations provides legitimate cover
- The modular system allows customization for different mission types

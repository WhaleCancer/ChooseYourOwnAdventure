# Ford F-150 (1985, 4x4 Pickup)

**Type:** Pickup truck  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** Logging, ranching, public works, general utility

## Overview

A best-selling workhorse, the Ford F-150 is omnipresent in BC logging, ranching, and public works. Rugged, reliable, and able to haul, ferry, or tow in any season. Lift kits or oversize tires are common for backcountry use.

- **Winch [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** Heavy-duty front winch for recovery and logs.
- **Toolbox/Bed Organizer [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Racks for tools, chainsaw, axes, and fuel cans.

## Characteristics

The F-150 is known for:

- **Durability** - Tough frame designed to take abuse
- **Versatility** - Suitable for a wide range of work and utility tasks
- **Off-road capability** - 4x4 configuration handles rough terrain
- **Cargo capacity** - Large bed for hauling equipment and materials
- **Common modifications** - Lift kits and oversize tires for backcountry use

## Common Uses

- **Logging operations** - Transporting equipment and personnel to remote logging sites
- **Ranching** - Farm and ranch work in rural areas
- **Public works** - Municipal and provincial maintenance operations
- **General utility** - Personal and commercial transportation

## In Play

The Ford F-150 serves as:

- **Work vehicle** - Standard transportation for logging, ranching, and utility work
- **Off-road transport** - Access to remote locations for investigations
- **Cargo transport** - Hauling equipment, evidence, or supplies
- **Common vehicle** - Blends in with normal traffic and doesn't draw attention
- **Rural settings** - Fits naturally in logging, mining, and agricultural scenarios

### Game Considerations

- The F-150 is extremely common in 1985 British Columbia, making it perfect for blending in
- The off-road capability provides access to remote investigation sites
- The cargo capacity allows for transporting equipment and evidence
- Common modifications (lift kits, oversized tires) reflect regional customization
- The vehicle's ubiquity makes it unremarkable, perfect for undercover operations

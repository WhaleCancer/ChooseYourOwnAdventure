# Toyota Land Cruiser (FJ60, 1985)

**Type:** Off-road vehicle / Expedition vehicle  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** Forestry, resource survey, ranch, coastal operations, expeditions

## Overview

Legendary worldwide, in Canada the Land Cruiser is a favorite for forestry, resource survey, ranch, and coastal crews. Unstoppable in mud and snow, easily modified for expedition, and more reliable than almost any domestic rival.

## Modules

- **Winch [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** Heavy-duty front winch for steep trails.
- **Roof Rack [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Camping, rescue, or expedition gear.
- **Extra Fuel Tank [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** 400km+ range for remote travel.

## Characteristics

The Toyota Land Cruiser FJ60 is known for:

- **Durability** - Thick steel construction, almost overbuilt for a civilian truck
- **Reliability** - Indestructible inline-six engine, legendary for reliability
- **Off-road capability** - Unstoppable in mud and snow, excellent for rough terrain
- **Expedition ready** - Easily modified for long-range expeditions and remote operations
- **Versatility** - Suitable for forestry, resource survey, ranching, and coastal operations

## Common Uses

- **Forestry operations** - Access to remote logging and forest management sites
- **Resource survey** - Geological and mineral exploration in remote areas
- **Ranching** - Farm and ranch work in challenging terrain
- **Coastal operations** - Access to remote coastal areas and beaches
- **Expeditions** - Long-range travel to remote locations

## In Play

The Toyota Land Cruiser serves as:

- **Expedition vehicle** - Long-range travel to remote investigation sites
- **Off-road transport** - Access to locations inaccessible to standard vehicles
- **Reliable workhorse** - Durable vehicle for extended field operations
- **Remote operations** - Suitable for investigations in wilderness and remote areas
- **Professional vehicle** - Fits naturally with forestry, survey, and resource industry operations

### Game Considerations

- The Land Cruiser's legendary reliability makes it ideal for remote investigations
- The off-road capability provides access to locations other vehicles cannot reach
- The expedition modifications (roof rack, extra fuel) support long-range operations
- The vehicle's reputation for durability reduces maintenance concerns
- The high center of gravity affects handling but provides ground clearance
- The vehicle's association with professional operations provides legitimate cover

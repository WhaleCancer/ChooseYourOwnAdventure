# Volkswagen Vanagon (1985)

**Type:** Van / Minibus / Camper  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** Family transportation, work crews, camping, government operations

## Overview

The classic VW Vanagon/Transporter is a boxy, rear-engined, rear-wheel-drive van with a worldwide reputation for durability, versatility, and fun. Whether used as a microbus for transporting people, a cargo/panel van, or a self-contained camper, it's a fixture in city, town, or backcountry. Common with families, work crews, and outdoor adventurers alike.

**Note:** Whole squad of men in black rolling up in these.

## Modules (Pick 2)

- **Bench Seats [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Additional padded seating for 4, folds flat (civilian minibus).
- **Cargo Organizer [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Storage racks/dividers for tools, camping, or scientific kit.
- **Pop-Top Camper Roof [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** Raises for standing headroom and 2 extra sleeping bunks in Westfalia campers.
- **Sleeping Bunks [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Fold-flat or permanent beds, up to 4 people sleeping inside.
- **Kitchenette [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** Sink, water tank, 2-burner propane stove, folding table.
- **Surveyor's Desk [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Map/work table, drawers, office supplies (govt/forestry/academic).
- **Long-Range Radio [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** For remote work or government use.
- **First Aid Kit [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Extensive med gear and supplies.

## Example Variants

### Family Minibus

**Modules:** Pop-Top Camper, Kitchenette

**Modules:** Surveyor's Desk, Long-Range Radio  
Can swap one for First Aid or Cargo Organizer.

### Basic Cargo/Tradesman's Panel Van

**Modules:** Cargo Organizer, First Aid Kit or Bench Seat (for work crew)

### Men in Black Van

**Modules:** Cargo Organizer, Long-Range Radio (or other government modules)  
Used by government agents, Men in Black, or other official operations.

## In Play

The Volkswagen Vanagon serves as:

- **Family vehicle** - Transportation for families and groups
- **Work vehicle** - Crew transportation and cargo hauling
- **Camper** - Self-contained camping and travel
- **Government vehicle** - Used by RCMP, M-EPIC, or other agencies
- **Men in Black vehicle** - Whole squads of agents in these vans

### Game Considerations

- The Vanagon's versatility makes it suitable for many different uses
- The modular interior allows customization for different missions
- The vehicle's common appearance helps blend in
- The connection to Men in Black creates opportunities for encounters
- The durability and reliability make it ideal for remote operations

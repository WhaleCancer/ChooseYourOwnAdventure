# Chevrolet Astro (1985)

**Type:** Van / Film crew vehicle  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** Film production, equipment transport, mobile base

## Overview

This is the film guys' car, it is the good guy version of the vanagon. A rugged, compact rear-wheel drive van with surprising interior space, the Astro hauls film crews and payloads to city streets, remote BC forests, or logging roads. Sliding side doors, rear twin doors, and flat floor make for quick loading and flexible configurations. Outfitted as a rolling equipment room or even a "mobile set base," it's a classic sight on any location shoot.

## Film Crew Modules (Pick/up to 3)

- **Camera/Grip Rack [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Heavy-duty floor and wall racks for camera tripods, C-stands, booms, and dollies. Includes bungee tie-downs, foam slots, and secure storage. In-game: +1 to avoid equipment damage during rough transit.
- **Portable Generator/Power Bank [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** Compact propane or gasoline generator and/or high-capacity battery units to power lights, monitors, or charge gear at any location. In-game: Gives grid power anywhere; runs for hours unless refueled.
- **Editing Bay [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** Fold-down workbench, splicing blocks, small color TV or portable VCR monitor, and tool chest for on-set editing, tape prep, and emergency fixes.
- **Sound Isolation Kit [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Foam panels, sandbags, and wind screens to build on-the-fly voice/sound booths in the van or on set. In-game: +1 to sound recording tests in windy/busy settings.
- **Coffee/Craft Services Kit [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Mini fridge, coffee thermos, snack dispenser for tired crew to recharge morale.
- **Fold-down Seats [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Rear seats (removable for more gear), fits an extra 3 crew if not full of gear.
- **Long-Range Radio [1 Slot, 1,000Cr]:** For location-to-hq or emergency forest communication.
- **First Aid/Fire Safety Kit [1 Slot, 500Cr]:** Well-stocked first aid box, fire extinguisher, and burn gel; required for union set work.

## In Play

The Chevrolet Astro serves as:

- **Film production vehicle** - Mobile base for film crews and equipment
- **Equipment transport** - Hauling cameras, lights, and production gear
- **Remote location support** - Power and facilities for on-location shoots
- **Mobile workspace** - Editing and production work on the go
- **Good guy vehicle** - Positive alternative to the Men in Black Vanagon

### Game Considerations

- The Astro's film crew focus creates unique investigation opportunities
- The modular system allows customization for different production needs
- The vehicle's association with film production provides cover for investigations
- The "good guy" angle contrasts with Men in Black operations
- The equipment and power capabilities support extended field operations

# BMW 3 Series (E30, 1985)

**Type:** Sport sedan / Luxury vehicle  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** Business, personal transportation, undercover operations

## Overview

The BMW 3 Series is a rear-wheel-drive sport sedan/coupe, highly sought after for its razor-sharp handling, punchy engine, and understated luxury. Perfect for business trips, impressive drives along BC's winding highways, or even getaway scenes. Popular among Vancouver's rising professionals, successful tradesmen, and "cool" cops, but also draws the envy (and ire) of joyriders and gearheads.

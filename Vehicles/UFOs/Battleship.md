# Battleship (UFO)

**Type:** Alien spacecraft / End-game threat  
**Era:** Active in 1985 (Season 3+)

## Overview

**Battleships** appear in Season 3 as end of game aerial threats. These are the largest and most powerful alien craft, representing the ultimate threat in alien operations.

## Characteristics

- **Largest craft** - Most massive and powerful UFO type
- **End-game threat** - Ultimate challenge for player characters
- **Combat focused** - Heavily armed and armored
- **Command vessel** - Likely carries high-ranking Greys (Commanders, Oracles)
- **Strategic operations** - Used for major alien operations

## Typical Operations

- **Major operations** - Large-scale alien activities
- **Combat engagements** - Heavy combat with military forces
- **Strategic targets** - Attacking important locations or facilities
- **Command and control** - Coordinating multiple smaller craft
- **Invasion preparation** - Potential prelude to larger alien activities

## Season Progression

Battleships represent the ultimate escalation:

- **Season 1** - Small operations, individual encounters
- **Season 2** - Terror Ships appear, large-scale operations
- **Season 3** - Battleships appear as end-game threats

## In Play

Battleships serve as:

- **End-game threats** - Ultimate challenges for campaigns
- **Major encounters** - Significant events requiring all resources
- **Campaign climax** - Potential final encounters
- **Military engagement** - Requiring military cooperation or resources
- **Strategic targets** - Important craft worth significant effort to destroy

### Game Considerations

- Battleships are extremely powerful and should be used sparingly
- The end-game nature creates opportunities for campaign climaxes
- The combat focus makes them extremely dangerous
- The strategic importance suggests major alien operations
- The season progression creates a sense of escalating threat leading to a climax

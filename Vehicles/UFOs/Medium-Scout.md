# Medium Scout (UFO)

**Type:** Alien spacecraft / Reconnaissance vehicle  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

A larger version of the [[Vehicles/UFOs/Small-Scout|Small Scout]] with the same mission. This class of ship is most associated with the crop circle phenomenon.

## Characteristics

- **Larger than Small Scout** - More crew and capabilities
- **Reconnaissance** - Designed for sensor readings and monitoring
- **Crop circles** - Most associated with creating crop circle patterns
- **Multiple crew** - Can carry more Greys than Small Scout
- **Enhanced capabilities** - More powerful sensors and systems

## Typical Operations

- **Sensor readings** - Monitoring environmental conditions, energy signatures, or other data
- **Crop circle creation** - Creating geometric patterns in fields
- **Reconnaissance** - Scouting locations before larger operations
- **Multiple abductions** - Taking several individuals for examination
- **Surveillance** - Monitoring human activity or specific locations

## Crop Circle Connection

Medium Scouts are most associated with the crop circle phenomenon. They may:

- **Create patterns** - Using advanced technology to create geometric patterns
- **Communication** - Using patterns as messages or signals
- **Navigation** - Using patterns as navigation aids
- **Ritual** - Creating patterns as part of alien ceremonies

## In Play

Medium Scouts serve as:

- **Reconnaissance encounters** - Larger craft for more extensive operations
- **Crop circle creation** - Creating mysterious patterns in fields
- **Multiple abduction scenarios** - Taking several individuals at once
- **Surveillance** - Monitoring player characters or locations
- **Combat encounters** - Larger engagements with alien craft

### Game Considerations

- Medium Scouts are larger and more capable than Small Scouts
- The crop circle connection creates unique investigation opportunities
- The larger size makes them more visible and easier to detect
- The multiple crew creates opportunities for varied encounters
- The enhanced capabilities suggest more sophisticated operations

# Abductor (UFO)

**Type:** Alien spacecraft / Abduction vehicle  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

A flying saucer designed for the specific mission of abducting humans. The **Abductor** is specialized for abduction operations, with facilities for holding, examining, and processing human subjects.

## Characteristics

- **Abduction specialized** - Designed specifically for human abduction operations
- **Holding facilities** - Areas for containing abductees
- **Examination rooms** - Medical and research facilities
- **Processing capabilities** - Equipment for implanting, examining, and returning subjects
- **Stealth capabilities** - Designed to avoid detection during operations

## Typical Operations

- **Individual abductions** - Taking specific individuals for examination
- **Multiple abductions** - Taking several individuals in a single operation
- **Examination** - Conducting medical and biological procedures
- **Implant installation** - Placing tracking devices or other implants
- **Return operations** - Returning abductees to their original locations

## Connection to Abduction Phenomenon

The Abductor is directly connected to:

- **[[Phenomena/Alien-Abductions|Alien Abductions]]** - The primary vehicle for abduction operations
- **Missing time** - Time spent aboard the craft
- **Medical procedures** - Examinations and experiments conducted aboard
- **Implant operations** - Installation of tracking devices

## In Play

Abductors serve as:

- **Abduction scenarios** - The primary vehicle for abduction operations
- **Investigation targets** - Craft to be tracked, intercepted, or captured
- **Rescue missions** - Rescuing abductees from the craft
- **Combat encounters** - Engaging abduction operations
- **Evidence** - Physical proof of alien abduction activities

### Game Considerations

- Abductors are the primary vehicle for abduction scenarios
- The specialized design creates unique encounter opportunities
- The holding facilities create opportunities for rescue missions
- The stealth capabilities make them difficult to detect or intercept
- The connection to abduction phenomenon ties into broader alien operations

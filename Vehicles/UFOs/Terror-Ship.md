# Terror Ship (UFO)

**Type:** Alien spacecraft / Terror operations vehicle  
**Era:** Active in 1985 (Season 2+)

## Overview

In Season 2, Terror Ships begin to appear and engage in large scale terror attacks. They unleash [[Creatures/Aliens/Grey-Harvester|Grey Harvesters]] and are designed for operations that create fear, chaos, and emotional harvesting on a massive scale.

## Characteristics

- **Terror operations** - Designed for large-scale fear and chaos operations
- **Harvester deployment** - Carries and deploys Grey Harvesters
- **Mass operations** - Capable of affecting large numbers of people
- **Emotional harvesting** - Operations designed to harvest powerful emotions
- **Combat capable** - Armed and dangerous for hostile encounters

## Typical Operations

- **Large-scale terror attacks** - Creating fear and chaos in populated areas
- **Harvester deployment** - Releasing Grey Harvesters for emotional harvesting
- **Mass abductions** - Taking large numbers of individuals
- **Psychological warfare** - Operations designed to terrorize populations
- **Combat operations** - Engaging in large-scale hostile encounters

## Season Progression

Terror Ships represent an escalation in alien activity:

- **Season 1** - Smaller operations, individual encounters
- **Season 2** - Terror Ships appear, large-scale operations begin
- **Season 3** - [[Vehicles/UFOs/Battleship|Battleships]] appear as end-game threats

## In Play

Terror Ships serve as:

- **Major threats** - Large-scale terror operations
- **Escalation** - Representing increased alien activity
- **Harvester deployment** - Releasing specialized Greys for emotional harvesting
- **Combat encounters** - Large and dangerous engagements
- **Campaign milestones** - Significant events in the campaign

### Game Considerations

- Terror Ships represent a major escalation in threat level
- The large-scale operations create opportunities for dramatic scenarios
- The Harvester deployment connects to emotional harvesting operations
- The combat capabilities make them extremely dangerous
- The season progression creates a sense of escalating threat

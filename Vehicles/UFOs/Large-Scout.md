# Large Scout (UFO)

**Type:** Alien spacecraft / Reconnaissance vehicle  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

The **Large Scout** is the largest type of scout craft. It is a more capable version of the [[Vehicles/UFOs/Medium-Scout|Medium Scout]] and [[Vehicles/UFOs/Small-Scout|Small Scout]], designed for extended reconnaissance operations and larger-scale missions.

## Characteristics

- **Largest scout type** - Most capable of the scout craft
- **Extended operations** - Can operate for longer periods
- **Multiple crew** - Carries larger crews for complex operations
- **Enhanced capabilities** - More powerful sensors, weapons, and systems
- **Mission flexibility** - Can perform a wider variety of operations

## Typical Operations

- **Extended reconnaissance** - Long-duration monitoring and surveillance
- **Complex missions** - Operations requiring multiple specialists
- **Multiple abductions** - Taking larger numbers of individuals
- **Strategic surveillance** - Monitoring important locations or targets
- **Combat operations** - Engaging in hostile encounters

## In Play

Large Scouts serve as:

- **Major encounters** - Significant UFO operations
- **Extended operations** - Long-duration missions
- **Multiple crew** - Encounters with various Grey specialists
- **Combat encounters** - Larger and more dangerous engagements
- **Strategic targets** - Important craft worth capturing or destroying

### Game Considerations

- Large Scouts are the most capable scout craft
- The larger size makes them more visible and easier to detect
- The multiple crew creates opportunities for varied encounters
- The enhanced capabilities suggest high-priority operations
- The combat capabilities make them dangerous opponents

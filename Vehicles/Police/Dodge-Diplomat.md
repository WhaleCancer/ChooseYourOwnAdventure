# Dodge Diplomat (1985 RCMP Cruiser)

**Type:** Police cruiser  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** RCMP patrol vehicle

## Overview

The Dodge Diplomat was a staple of Canadian police fleets in the early to mid-1980s, highly similar in role and construction to the Plymouth Gran Fury. Compact for a North American "full-size" V8, with lean lines, a tough frame, and a no-nonsense bench seat. The RCMP depended on its simplicity, cheap parts, and good city handling for urban and rural patrol alike.

## In Play

The Dodge Diplomat serves as:

- **RCMP cruiser** - Standard patrol vehicle for Royal Canadian Mounted Police
- **Police operations** - Law enforcement and pursuit operations
- **Urban patrol** - City and town police work
- **Rural patrol** - Highway and remote area operations
- **Common vehicle** - Familiar sight in 1985 British Columbia

### Game Considerations

- The Diplomat's simplicity makes it reliable and easy to maintain
- The smaller size provides better maneuverability than larger cruisers
- The police package provides necessary law enforcement equipment
- The common appearance helps blend in with normal traffic
- The RCMP connection provides official authority

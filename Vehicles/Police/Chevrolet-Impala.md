# Chevrolet Impala (1985)

**Type:** Police cruiser / Full-size sedan  
**Era:** 1985  
**Use:** Police patrol vehicle

## Overview

The Chevrolet Impala is a full-size sedan that served as a common police cruiser in 1985 British Columbia. Similar to other full-size sedans of the era, it provides reliable transportation for law enforcement operations.

## Vehicle Profile

| Stat            | Value                                                      |
| --------------- | ---------------------------------------------------------- |
| **Size**        | Small Vehicle                                              |
| **Crew**        | 1 driver + passengers                                       |
| **Speed**       | Fast                                                       |

| **Hull**        | 10                                                         |
| **Armor**       | None (Police models have Light armor)                      |
| **Price**       | 3,100 CAD (1985)                                           |

## Variants

**Police models** have Light armor, providing additional protection for law enforcement operations.

## In Play

The Chevrolet Impala serves as:

- **Police cruiser** - Standard patrol vehicle for law enforcement
- **Pursuit vehicle** - High-speed chase capabilities
- **Common vehicle** - Familiar sight in 1985 British Columbia
- **Reliable transportation** - Dependable vehicle for daily operations

### Game Considerations

- The Impala's reliability makes it ideal for police work
- The police package provides necessary law enforcement equipment
- The common appearance helps blend in with normal traffic
- The full-size design provides room for passengers and equipment
- The pursuit capabilities make it suitable for high-speed chases

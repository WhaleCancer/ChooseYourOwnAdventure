**Goddess of Grace**

**Specific Power:** Courga's Grace

Once per day, the Priest may imbue themselves or one other target with supernatural physical grace. Lasting for a number of minutes equal to the Priest's Devotion score, the target gains a +1 bonus to most physical actions (including combat), or a +4 bonus to any physical actions involving grace, balance, or poise. This latter category could include acrobatics, dancing, etc.

The daughter of [Usrel](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#usrel), and rather confusingly also the wife of her brother, [Fourga](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#fourga), Courga is a minor Goddess of physical and personal grace. Other names for Courga include Tuanar, Kirilin and Nureya. Her Priests are always graceful in their actions and words. Courga is also the mother of [Telak](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#telak). Priests of Courga have no standard dress but do have the symbol of a cat. In Khul, the month of Days Lengthen is dedicated to Courga. Holy days of Courga include the 16th of Unlocking.

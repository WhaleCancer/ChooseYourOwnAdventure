# Makroides

**Source:** CB77030 - Gods of Titan

**Page Images:**

- [View Page 69](../../CB77030%20-%20Gods_of_Titan_Pages/page_0069.png)

**The Crab God**

**Specific Power:** Animal Command

The Priest will never be attacked by, and may communicate freely with, animals of the type they serve, which includes all types of crustaceans, whether normal or giant-sized.

**General Powers:**

- Bravery
- Protection vs tentacled creatures
- Shape Change

Makroides is the God of Crabs and other crustaceans, including lobsters, shrimp, crayfish, prawns, hermit crabs and barnacles. He is associated with [Hydana](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#hydana), [Aqualis](Aqualis.md) and [Ichthys](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#ichthys), and a sworn enemy of [Behemoth](Behemoth.md), Chaos Demigod of the Sea.

Appearing as a gigantic scarlet crab with enormous curve-clawed pincers, Makroides is worshipped by fishermen, especially in places such as Lobster Sands and the Port of Crabs, as well as by inhuman creatures including Krell, Crab Folk, and the Exo-Beast.

There are rumours of Shrines raised on isolated islands from driftwood by such fishermen, with the doorway formed of a pair of gigantic crab claws from a real monster, even among Giant Crabs!

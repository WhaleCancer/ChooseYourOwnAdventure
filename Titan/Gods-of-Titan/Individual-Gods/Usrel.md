## Goddess of Peace

**Specific Power:** Zone of Peace

A Priest of Usrel may, once per day, create a 3-metre radius zone of peace, inside which no aggressive action may be taken by anyone, including the Priest themselves. This lasts for a number of A0ack Rounds equivalent to the Devotion score of the Priest.

**General Powers:**

- Banish
- Heal
- Ward

The third daughter of [Sindla](Sindla.md) (the others being [Asrel](Asrel.md) and [Libra](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#libra)), Usrel is a minor Goddess, though still a member of the Hall of the Mind, worshipped primarily wherever unwanted wars occur.

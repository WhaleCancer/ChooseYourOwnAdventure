## Lord of Exenteration

**Specific Power:** Pain of Trabaten

Once per day the Priest may cause one touched target to suffer such excruciating pain that they immediately reduce their SKILL score by 1. This is considered a Wound, and will require either Magic healing or a week of rest to recover.

**General Powers:**

- Curse
- Ill-Luck
- Weakness

Trabaten is an Evil Demigod allied to the [Demon Prince](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#demon-princes) [Ishtra](../Chapter-3-The-Gods-of-Titan.md#ishtra). He is the Patron God of torturers (exenteration means the practice of disembowelling a victim), obscure cults such as the Acolytes of Pain, and is also worshipped by other foul beings such as Chaos Warriors and the Kassistra or Cobra People.

The Temples of Trabaten are almost always hidden away from the sight of civilised people due to the u0er revulsion they create. Anyone unfortunate enough to see the inside of one is either a member of the awful cult or is a victim taken there for a long and terrible sacrifice.

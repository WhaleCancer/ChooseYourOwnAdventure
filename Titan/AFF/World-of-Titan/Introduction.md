# The World of Titan

**Source:** AFF Advanced Fighting Fantasy 2nd Edition

**Page Images:**

- [View Page 100](../../../AFF_Advanced_Fighting_Fantasy_2nd_Ed_Pages/World%20of%20Titan/page_0100.png)

The World of Titan is the setting for Advanced Fighting Fantasy adventures. It is a world of diverse landscapes, from deserts to mountains, sweeping vistas, teeming alleyways, trap-filled dungeons, ferocious monsters, deadly animals, farming villages, traders, and temples. Titan is a world of adventure needing heroes.

# Backgrounds

## Overview

Each Background has a set of Skill bonuses, starting equipment, as well as an initial encounter with an unexplained phenomenon.

## Combat Focused Backgrounds

These backgrounds excel in combat, having multiple Combat oriented Skills and related starting equipment.

### The Breacher

**Emergency Response Team Officer**

An ERT Member who is part of a raid on a satanic cult in Victoria.

**Skills:**
- Armor +1
- Demolitions +1
- Lock Picking +1
- Firearms +1
- Strength +1

**Starting Equipment:**
- Smith & Wesson Model 10 Revolver
- Heckler & Koch MP5
- FNC2
- Military shotgun

**Parapsychological Exposure:** This agent was part of the disastrous raid on the Kelowna branch of the Aetherius Society. A damaged prayer battery somehow exploded, even those these devices were thought to be phony.

**Special Ability:** You are authorized to just shotgun locks or kick them down.

### The Operator

Tactical team operators carry out the tactical plan. They should be in top physical condition and possess excellent tactical, physical and firearms skills.

**Skills:**
- Awareness +1
- Firearms +1
- Melee +1

**Starting Equipment:**
- Pistol
- SMG
- Knife

### The Shield

There is an adage that is still true today. "It is easier to throw a spear than to carry a shield." Skill, strength and endurance are necessary to be effective with a ballistic shield.

**Skills:**
- Armor +1
- Firearms +1
- Shield +1

**Starting Equipment:**
- Smith & Wesson Model 59
- Ballistic Shield
- Light Ballistic Armor
- Medium Ballistic Armor (with Armor 2)
- Heavy Ballistic Armor (with Armor 3)

**Note:** The ballistic shield is typically attached to the top of the trunk of a Plymouth Gran Fury.

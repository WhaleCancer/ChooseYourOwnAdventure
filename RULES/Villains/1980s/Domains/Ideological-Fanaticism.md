# Ideological Fanaticism

This section describes villains driven by extreme beliefs - whether religious, political, or philosophical. These could be cult leaders, UFO believers, fanatical followers of a cause, or those who believe they are serving a higher purpose (whether human, alien, or something else entirely).

Roll 1d6 to determine the specific type of ideological servant: 1-2: Cult Leader/Prophet (charismatic leader with followers), 3-4: Zealot (fanatical follower, no special leadership but gains +1 SKILL in combat), 5-6: Other or roll again (ancient order keeper, alien entity servant, etc.).

## Characteristics and Skills

Those that rely on ideology are often charismatic and determined. They have SKILL = 4+1d3, STAMINA = 10+1d6, and MAGIC = 0 (though some may have psionic abilities or alien powers). Cult Leaders/Prophets and Political Extremists receive 3 points in Leadership and Social special skills. Religious Zealots and Supremacists have no special leadership abilities but gain +1 SKILL in combat due to their fanaticism. All ideologues have 2 points in Knowledge special skills, 2 points in Armour (if they wear any), and one type of hand weapon (handguns, melee weapons, etc.).

**Note on Zealots:** A zealot is a villain with fanatical devotion but no special leadership abilities. They gain +1 SKILL in combat due to their fanaticism, making them dangerous opponents even without followers.

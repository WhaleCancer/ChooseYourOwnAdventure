# Political Intrigue

These villains are a varied group, including corrupt officials, spies, manipulators, or talented individuals with charisma and connections. They are referred to as 'the professionals' who rely on acquired skills rather than brute strength or advanced technology. Examples given are a politician who knows how to break the law or a corporate executive who incites conflict.

Roll 1d6 to determine their specific role:

- 1: Corporate Executive or Wealthy individual
- 2: A criminal of some kind
- 3: A government official or politician
- 4-6: Other (your choice - spy, media manipulator, social engineer, etc.)

## Characteristics and Skills

These villains focus on using their special skills to achieve their aims and are generally not afraid of combat, but prefer to avoid it. They have SKILL = 4+1d3, STAMINA = 10+1d6, MAGIC = 0. They have 2 points in Stealth special skills, 2 points in Armour (if they wear any), one type of hand weapon (handguns, etc.), and 2 points in two out of the following skills: Bargain, Con, Etiquette, Law, or Leadership.

# Lust for Power

These villains are a varied group, including merchants, thieves, nobles, or talented individuals with charisma. They are referred to as 'the professionals' who rely on acquired skills rather than brute strength or magical powers. Examples given are a magistrate who knows how to break the law or a braggart who incites riots.

Roll 1d6:

- 1: Merchant or Wealthy individual
- 2: A criminal of some kind
- 3: A noble
- 4-6: Other (your choice)

## Characteristics and Skills

These villains focus on using their special skills to achieve their aims and are generally not afraid of combat. They have SKILL = 4+1d3, STAMINA = 10+1d6, MAGIC = 0. They have 2 points in Stealth special skills, 2 points in Armour, one type of hand weapon (e.g., swords, axes), and 2 points in two out of the following skills: Bargain, Con, Etiquette, Law, or Leadership.

# Potion of Stamina

**Category:** Scrolls, Potions & Sundries  
**Enchantment required:** 1  
**Cost of recipe:** 100gp  
**Ingredients:** Red Lotus Root, Anaesthetic Moss  
**Cost of ingredients:** 25gp  
**Time to craft:** 1 Hour

Probably the single most common magical item on Titan, Potions of Stamina restore the drinker's STAMINA score to its initial level. There is usually a strong market for these potions amongst adventurers, soldiers and travellers.

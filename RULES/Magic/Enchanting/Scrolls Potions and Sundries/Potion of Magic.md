# Potion of Magic

**Category:** Scrolls, Potions & Sundries  
**Enchantment required:** 1  
**Cost of recipe:** 125gp  
**Ingredients:** Manaweed, Powdered Ghoul Bone  
**Cost of ingredients:** 30gp  
**Time to craft:** 1 Hour

Potions of Magic are sought after, rather unsurprisingly, by Wizards and spellcasters of similar magics. The ingredients are not common, but are also not that dangerous to gather and so they will turn up from time to time in the market places and back-room dealers of most cities.

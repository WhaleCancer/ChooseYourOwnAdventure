# Arrow of Trollslaying

**Category:** Trinkets  
**Enchantment required:** 3  
**Cost of recipe:** 500gp  
**Ingredients:** Red Firestone, Ebony tree wood, Feathers from a Deathawk  
**Cost of ingredients:** 250gp / 6 arrows  
**Time to craft:** 1 Week

Arrows of Trollslaying have a black shaft and fletching and a bright red arrowhead. If a Troll of any kind is hit with one of these arrows, it will take 2D6 damage instead of normal damage. Armour will still apply.

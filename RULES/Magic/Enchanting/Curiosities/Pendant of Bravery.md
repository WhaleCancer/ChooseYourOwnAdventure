# Pendant of Bravery

**Category:** Curiosities  
**Enchantment required:** 2  
**Cost of recipe:** 20gp  
**Ingredients:** Heart of a Sabretooth Tiger, Bi-coloured Topaz, Silver Chain  
**Cost of ingredients:** 125gp  
**Time to craft:** 1 Day

The pendant of Bravery has a two colour Topaz suspended on a fine silver chain, and will grant the wearer a bonus of +2 to any Luck tests against fear or fear magic.

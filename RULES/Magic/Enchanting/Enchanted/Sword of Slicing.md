# Sword of Slicing

**Category:** Enchanted  
**Enchantment required:** 5  
**Cost of recipe:** 1600gp  
**Ingredients:** White steel from the Mountains of Grief, Bomba tree wood, Wyvern Scales  
**Cost of ingredients:** 900gp  
**Time to craft:** 1 Month

A sword of slicing is a much sought after weapon by warriors. The white, inlaid blade and dark wooden grip make it instantly recognisable, and its ability to ignore all non-magical armour make it feared. Only magical armour will provide protection against a blow from this sword.

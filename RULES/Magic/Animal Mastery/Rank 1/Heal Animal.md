# Heal Animal (1/2/4/6)

This spell restores health and vitality to animal companions by touch, with varying effects based on Magic Point cost:

- **1 Magic Point:** Rids parasites (fleas, ticks, worms), makes skin/pelt healthy, restores 1 STAMINA point if wounded.
- **2 Magic Points:** Same as 1 MP, but restores 1-6 STAMINA points if wounded.
- **4 Magic Points:** Same as 2 MP, plus cures poisons, diseases, or insanity.
- **6 Magic Points:** Same as 4 MP, plus restores STAMINA score to its initial level.

> ## Plant Mastery variant
>
> This spell works identically for plants or fungi.

# Summon Guardian Beast-Spirit (8)

This spell summons a "swirling, pale, glowing, ghostly version" of the Beastmaster's chosen animal type, ready for combat. The Guardian Spirit has the same combat scores as a normal animal of its type, with an additional +2 to SKILL and +2 to STAMINA. It can only be harmed by silver or magical weapons. The spirit serves the caster until dawn the next day or until slain.

> ## Plant Mastery variant
>
> This spell is effective with plant or fungi monsters that actively attack prey, creating a spirit version of them.

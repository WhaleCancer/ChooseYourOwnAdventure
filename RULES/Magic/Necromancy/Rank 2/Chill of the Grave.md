# Chill of the Grave (2)

**Effect:** The target is allowed a Test for Luck to avoid the effects.

**Impact:** If unlucky, the target is chilled to the bone and cannot stop shivering, even with a Heat cantrip.

**Penalty:** The target suffers a -2 penalty to combat, spellcasting, and other physical actions.

**Duration:** Lasts for a number of rounds equal to the necromancer's arcane power.

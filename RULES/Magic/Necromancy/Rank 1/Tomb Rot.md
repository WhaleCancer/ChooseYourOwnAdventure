# Tomb Rot (1)

**Effect:** The target must immediately Test their Luck to avoid being infected.

**Infection Progression:** If infected, the target must Test their Luck every morning.
- **Failure:** Indicates loss of one SKILL point and two STAMINA.
- **Success:** Indicates the disease does not worsen that day.

**Recovery:** Two consecutive successful tests mean the Hero has recovered and is no longer infected.

**Death:** If SKILL or STAMINA are reduced to 0, the Hero dies.

**Nature:** This disease is non-magical and can be cured by normal means.

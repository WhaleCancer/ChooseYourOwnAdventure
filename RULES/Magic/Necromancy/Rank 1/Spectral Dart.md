# Spectral Dart (1)

**Effect:** Causes a small translucent dart to fly through the air and strike a living target.

**Accuracy:** No roll is required for the caster to hit the target.

**Defense:** The target may attempt to use the Dodge special skill or Test their Luck to avoid the dart.

**Damage:** If the dart hits, it inflicts 1-3 STAMINA damage.

**Protection:** Only magical armor will provide protection.

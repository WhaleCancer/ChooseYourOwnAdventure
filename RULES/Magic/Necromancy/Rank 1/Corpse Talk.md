# Corpse Talk (1)

**Effect:** Allows necromancers to speak to the dead.

**Casting:** Must be cast directly onto the dead body or the spot where the creature died.

**Target:** Can be cast on any formerly living creature.

**Requirement:** The necromancer must understand the language spoken by the deceased to communicate.

**Interaction:** Allows asking three questions, which must be asked promptly ("The dead do not like to be kept hanging around!").

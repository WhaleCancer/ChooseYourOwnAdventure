# Minor Skeleton (Minor)

**Effect:** Animates a small animal skeleton or corpse (e.g., mouse, rat, monkey). Cannot animate anything larger than a domestic cat.

**Control:** The animated corpse is under the caster's full control as long as it's within sight.

**Duration:** Remains animated until the following dawn.

**Combat:** If used in combat, the corpse has SKILL 3, STAMINA 1, and inflicts 1 point of damage on a successful hit.

**Utility:** Can be used to move or manipulate objects the necromancer cannot otherwise reach.

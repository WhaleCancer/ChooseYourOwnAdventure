# Clumsy (1) Combat Charm

If the target fails a Test for Luck, they become badly coordinated and clumsy. It reduces one opponent's skill by 2 points. Can be cast outside of combat. An affected target will drop handheld objects on a roll of 1-3 on 1D6, checked each round. In combat, the target will Fumble their weapon on any double roll. If a double 6 is rolled, the victim must make two rolls on the Fumble Table.

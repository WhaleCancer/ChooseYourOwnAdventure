# Enchantments

This page contains more Enchantment recipes to supplement those in the Heroes Companion. For the complete Enchanting magical style, see [Enchanting](Enchanting.md).

## Additional Recipes

The following recipes are additional examples beyond those found in the Heroes Companion:

- [Potion of Magic](Enchanting/Scrolls%20Potions%20and%20Sundries/Potion%20of%20Magic.md) - A potion sought after by spellcasters
- [Boundary Ring](Enchanting/Curiosities/Boundary%20Ring.md) - A curiosity that enhances spellcasting at dawn and dusk
- [Dagger of Retribution](Enchanting/Trinkets/Dagger%20of%20Retribution.md) - A trinket that unleashes a wave of force
- [Staff of Energy](Enchanting/Enchanted/Staff%20of%20Energy.md) - An enchanted staff that stores magic points

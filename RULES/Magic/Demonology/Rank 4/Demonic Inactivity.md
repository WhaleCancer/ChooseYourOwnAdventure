# Demonic Inactivity (4)

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 60

**Page Image:** [View Page 60](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0060.png)

**Rank:** 4

**Magic Point Cost:** 4

## Description

This spell causes one Demon to freeze in place for a number of rounds equal to the caster's Magic-Demonology score.

## Effects

The Demon cannot defend itself, but any successful physical or magical attack will break the spell's effects.

# Restore Demon (4)

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 60

**Page Image:** [View Page 60](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0060.png)

**Rank:** 4

**Magic Point Cost:** 4

## Description

This spell can be cast on a single Demon within 20 feet.

## Effects

The Demon immediately recovers 2D6 STAMINA points, though its initial STAMINA cannot be exceeded.

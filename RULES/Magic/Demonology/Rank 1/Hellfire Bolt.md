# Hellfire Bolt (1)

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 60

**Page Image:** [View Page 60](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0060.png)

**Rank:** 1

**Magic Point Cost:** 1

## Description

This spell fires a bolt of hellfire at an enemy within 100 feet. The target can Test LUCK or make a Dodge Special Skill test to avoid the bolt.

## Effects

- **Against Truly Good Beings:** Deals 1D6+2 STAMINA damage to truly Good beings (e.g., Elves, Good priests) and ignores their armor.
- **Against Demons:** If cast on a Demon, it heals 1 point of STAMINA instead of causing damage.

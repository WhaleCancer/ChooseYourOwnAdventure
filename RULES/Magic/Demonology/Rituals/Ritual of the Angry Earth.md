# Ritual of the Angry Earth

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 62

**Page Image:** [View Page 62](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0062.png)

**Magic-Conjuration:** 4

**Type:** Summoning

**Components:** The eye of an Earth Elemental

**Time Taken:** Twelve hours

**Rarity:** Rare

## Description

This ritual, known by few, summons a Rock Demon. These Demons are typically furious and go on a rampage upon release. Summoners often use this ritual near enemies, exerting control for an hour to allow the Demonologist to escape.

# Ritual of the Firelord

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 62

**Page Image:** [View Page 62](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0062.png)

**Magic-Conjuration:** 6

**Type:** Summoning

**Components:** A ruby baked in a forge for a year and a day

**Time Taken:** Twenty four hours

**Rarity:** Rare

## Description

This ritual summons a Fire Demon, often used as a lieutenant by Demonologists, granting it great freedom. The ritual must be completed in one continuous 24-hour block, demanding significant stamina and strength from the Demonologist.

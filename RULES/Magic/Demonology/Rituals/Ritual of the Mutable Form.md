# Ritual of the Mutable Form

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 61

**Page Image:** [View Page 61](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0061.png)

**Magic-Conjuration:** 1

**Type:** Summoning

**Components:** A Chaos Warrior bone

**Time Taken:** One hour

**Rarity:** Common

## Description

This spell will create a gate to the chaotic part of the Demonic Plane and draw through a Chaos Demon of some sort. As with any Chaos Demon, the exact form and appearance will be uncertain until the Demon actually appears.

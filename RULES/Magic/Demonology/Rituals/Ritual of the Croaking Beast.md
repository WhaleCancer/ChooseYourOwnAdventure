# Ritual of the Croaking Beast

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 61

**Page Image:** [View Page 61](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0061.png)

**Magic-Conjuration:** 1

**Type:** Summoning

**Components:** The tongue of a Giant Toad

**Time Taken:** Four hours

**Rarity:** Rare

## Description

This Ritual is rarely used, even though the ingredients are relatively easy to obtain as components go. Although the Demon Toad summoned can be a very effective guard, few evil Wizards want one of these stinking and slimy creatures lurking around their tower or lair.

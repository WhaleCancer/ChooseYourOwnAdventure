# Ritual of the Eternal Hound

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 61

**Page Image:** [View Page 61](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0061.png)

**Magic-Conjuration:** 2

**Type:** Summoning

**Components:** A stone from the slopes of Hell

**Time Taken:** Two hours

**Rarity:** Uncommon

## Description

This Ritual is used by Demonologists to summon a Hellhound as a guard for their fortress or tower. Hellhounds are unpredictable beasts and so are often locked into an area to guard where they cannot cause any damage to the servants of the summoner.

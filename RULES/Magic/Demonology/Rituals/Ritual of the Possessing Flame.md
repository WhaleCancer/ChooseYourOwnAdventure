# Ritual of the Possessing Flame

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 62

**Page Image:** [View Page 62](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0062.png)

**Magic-Conjuration:** 3

**Type:** Summoning

**Components:** The hand of a man who has burned to death

**Time Taken:** Six hours

**Rarity:** Rare

## Description

This ritual summons a bodiless Hellfire Spirit. If a recently deceased corpse is available, the spirit will possess it and become a Demon. Otherwise, it can be bound to an area to await a victim. If the spirit doesn't possess a corpse within 24 hours, it returns to Hell.

# Enhance Demon (2)

**Source:** CB77015 - Demons of Doom, Page 60

**Page Image:** [View Page 60](../../../../CB77015%20-%20Demons%20of%20Doom_Pages/Demonology/page_0060.png)

**Rank:** 2

**Magic Point Cost:** 2

## Description

This spell increases the basic abilities of a single Demon touched by the caster for ten minutes.

## Effects

The caster can apply two benefits from the following list:

- +1 to Attack Total
- +1 to damage rolls
- +1 to armour rolls
- +1 to spell casting rolls
- +2 to initial STAMINA

A Demon can only benefit from one "Enhance Demon" spell at a time.

# Flameskull (4)

**Element:** Fire

**Engraved Upon:** Any item worn on the head

Upon activation the user's head is transformed into a burning skull with flames leaping upwards and spewing from your mouth. Animals that fear flame will retreat from you and any thinking creature that is hostile to you or your companions and within 30' must test vs SKILL at the start of each round or suffer -2 penalty due to fear. The effect lasts for rounds equal to half your Runic Knowledge rounded down. Provides light as a torch.

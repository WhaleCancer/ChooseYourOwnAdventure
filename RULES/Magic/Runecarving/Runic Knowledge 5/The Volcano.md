# The Volcano (5)

**Element:** Earth/Fire

**Engraved Upon:** Any object

Upon use, narrow cracks open in the ground around the carver up to a radius of 20' from which scorching flames shoot upwards. Each round anyone except the Rune Carver, friend or foe, must test vs SKILL (Dodge) or be hit by a gout of flame for 1d6 points of damage. Lasts rounds equal to half your Runic Knowledge rounded down.

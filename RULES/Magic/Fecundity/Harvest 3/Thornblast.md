# Thornblast (3)

**Range:** Cone, 5m in length and 3m at the wide end

**Duration:** Instant

This spell consumes one seed pod to cause a blast of long, sharp thorns to emanate from the outstretched hand of the grove tender causing 1d6+3 damage to all in its path unless they succeed in a test of LUCK in which case they take half damage (rounded down).

# Summon Tree Man (4)

**Range:** See Description

**Duration:** Minutes = Harvest Score

This spell consumes a seed pod to summon a Tree Man SKILL 8 STAMINA 12 Attacks: 2, Weapon: Crushing branches, treat as Morning Star with +2 to the damage roll, Armour: Monster Heavy. If it hits, the Tree Man will grapple an opponent whilst attempting to punch them with its other hand (grappled opponents are at -3 until they make an opposed Strength or Acrobatics test to escape, they may try once each round). The Tree Man grows out of the seed pod in one round, it must be within metres equal to the caster's Harvest score when the spell is cast but after that it can wander as it likes until the spell expires.

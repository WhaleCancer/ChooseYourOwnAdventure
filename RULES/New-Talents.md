# New Talents

New Talents can be taken when creating a new Hero, or acquired by existing Heroes through experience points and an explanation (e.g., performing a great service for a God or training with a tactical genius). The Director has the final say on Talent acquisition.

**Source:** Heroes Companion (Chapter 1)

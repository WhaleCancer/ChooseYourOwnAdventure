# RCMP Smith & Wesson Model 10

**Manufacturer:** Smith & Wesson  
**Caliber:** .38 Special  
**Type:** Revolver

**Tech Level:** Low Tech

**Range:** Short

**Ammo:** 6 (revolver cylinder capacity)

**Ammo/Slot:** 3 speed loaders

The standard issue sidearm for RCMP and most police forces in British Columbia. A reliable, double-action revolver with a solid reputation for durability and accuracy. Commonly encountered in holsters of law enforcement officers throughout the province. The Model 10 features a 6-round cylinder and is chambered in .38 Special, making it a trusted sidearm for Canadian law enforcement.

## Suggested Variations

Here are some ways to differentiate the Model 10 from other pistols:

**Reliability Advantage:** Revolvers are less prone to jamming. Could grant a bonus to combat rolls in adverse conditions (wet, dirty, extreme cold) or reduce fumble chances.

**Stopping Power:** .38 Special can be loaded with +P rounds. Could grant +1 to damage roll on a successful hit (treating a roll of 3 as 4, etc., up to maximum of 7+).

**Accuracy at Close Range:** Revolvers can be more accurate at point-blank range. Could grant a +1 bonus to combat rolls at Very Short range.

**Simpler Operation:** No safety to forget or magazine release to fumble. Could reduce fumble chance or grant a bonus when drawing/readying the weapon.

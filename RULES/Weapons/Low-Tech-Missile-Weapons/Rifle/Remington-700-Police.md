# Remington 700 Police

**Manufacturer:** Remington Arms  
**Caliber:** .308 Winchester (most common), also available in .223 Remington, .300 Winchester Magnum, and other calibers  
**Type:** Bolt-action sniper rifle

**Tech Level:** Low Tech

The Remington 700 Police is a precision bolt-action rifle based on the popular Remington Model 700, specifically configured for law enforcement and tactical use. In British Columbia, the Remington 700 Police would be encountered with police tactical units, RCMP sniper teams, and specialized law enforcement units. The weapon features a heavy barrel for improved accuracy, a precision trigger, and is typically equipped with a high-quality scope. The Remington 700 Police is designed for accuracy and reliability in tactical situations, making it the weapon of choice for police snipers who need to make precise shots at longer ranges. The bolt-action design ensures reliability and accuracy, though it limits the rate of fire compared to semi-automatic rifles. The weapon's reputation for precision and its use by law enforcement makes it a trusted choice for tactical operations.

Here are some ways to differentiate the Remington 700 Police from other rifles:

**Scope:** The weapon is typically equipped with a high-quality scope. Could grant bonuses to spotting targets, or allow for more precise targeting at longer ranges.

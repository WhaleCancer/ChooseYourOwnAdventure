# Energy Weapons

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Lasers, Blasters, Plasma weapons and other directed energy forms are amongst the most powerful weapons available. These weapons require a power source to generate the effect that inflicts the damage, and most power sources can be recharged given enough time. Energy weapons are mostly classified as Military grade and may be restricted in some systems and jurisdictions. The use of Energy weapons aboard starships is not recommended.

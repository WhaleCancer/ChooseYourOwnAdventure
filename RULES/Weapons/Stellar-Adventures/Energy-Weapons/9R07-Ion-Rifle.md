# 9R07 Ion Rifle

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Meteor Technologies, the primary manufacturer of weapons for the Blazing Meteor mercenary company, was asked to design a handheld Ion weapon for use against robot armies. Although the weapon causes little damage to an organic target, it will inflict great damage against robots and other electronics. The 9R07 features twin energy cells to ensure power reaches the target.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 6
- **Ammo/Slot:** 2
- **Range:** Med
- **Price:** 360 Cr

# X91 Blaster

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Frost produced the X91 as an upgraded and improved version of the classic X74 design. All of the internal components have been upgraded or redesigned and the casing and controls simplified and refined. The X91 offers the same significant damage output as the earlier version with less residual heat, a smaller recoil and greater reliability.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 16
- **Ammo/Slot:** 4
- **Range:** Med
- **Price:** 2600 Cr

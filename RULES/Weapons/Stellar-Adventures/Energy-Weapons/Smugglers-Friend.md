# Smugglers Friend

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Our in-house weapons technologists produces this innovative weapon at the request of one of our galactic delivery pilots. Faced with entering dangerous areas under the control of strict weapons restrictions, he was unable to be armed and was robbed more than once. This weapon is undetectable by 97.2% of scanners and further can be broken down in seconds into 17 components.

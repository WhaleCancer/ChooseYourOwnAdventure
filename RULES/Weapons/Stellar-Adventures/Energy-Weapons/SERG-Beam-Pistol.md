# SERG Beam Pistol

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Working on the same principle as their popular Beam Cannon, the SERG from SCT is a newly-produced sidearm version. Featuring smoother lines and with a more compact design, this weapon is designed to look good as well as shoot a long way.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 16
- **Ammo/Slot:** 4
- **Range:** Long
- **Price:** 440 Cr

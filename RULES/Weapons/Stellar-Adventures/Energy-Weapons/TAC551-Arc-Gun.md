# TAC551 Arc Gun

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Created by Meteor Technologies as a smaller and lighter version of the TAC550, Employing the same technology and styling, this weapon is a favourite of paramilitary units who enjoy the fear this weapon generates in ordinary people. The smaller and lighter frame is able to generate the same power at the cost of reduced 'ammunition'.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 6
- **Ammo/Slot:** 6
- **Range:** Sh
- **Price:** 345 Cr

## Damage Table

| D6 | 1 | 2 | 3 | 4 | 5 | 6 | 7+ |
|----|---|---|---|---|---|---|----|
| Damage | 4 | 4 | 4 | 5 | 5 | 5 | 6 |

## Special Rules

On a damage roll of a 6+, a Robot target will suffer a penalty of -4 to all actions for one round

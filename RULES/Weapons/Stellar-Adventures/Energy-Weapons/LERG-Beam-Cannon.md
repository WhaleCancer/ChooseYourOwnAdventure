# LERG Beam Cannon

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

This large weapon from SCT Enterprises provides good damage at extreme ranges. A modified laser generator compresses the wavelengths to extend the range whilst retaining most of the energy. An inverted grip and trigger at the rear are complemented by an over-barrel support bar for ease of use.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 24
- **Ammo/Slot:** 3
- **Range:** V.Lo
- **Price:** 600 Cr

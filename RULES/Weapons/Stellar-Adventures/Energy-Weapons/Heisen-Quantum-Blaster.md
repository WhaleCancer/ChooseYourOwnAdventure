# Heisen Quantum Blaster

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

This intriguing weapon from Meteor Technologies uses the latest research to introduce uncertainty to the individual molecules of the unfortunate target. This can inflict great damage on a target by causing part of the body to not even know whether they actually exist or not. It is not a cheap weapon and is only sometimes available, but is much sought after by serious soldiers.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 4
- **Ammo/Slot:** 1
- **Range:** Sh
- **Price:** 1100 Cr

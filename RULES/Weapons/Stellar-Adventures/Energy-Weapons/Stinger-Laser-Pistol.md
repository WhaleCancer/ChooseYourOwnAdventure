# Stinger Laser Pistol

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Created by Frost Armaments as a concealed weapon for those who need to be armed without appearing to be so. The weapon is slim and light, as are all of the weapons from this manufacturer, and is supplied with a chest harness suitable for humans and aliens of all types. Range and power are limited, but are still sufficient for self defence.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 4
- **Ammo/Slot:** 4
- **Range:** Sh
- **Price:** 140 Cr

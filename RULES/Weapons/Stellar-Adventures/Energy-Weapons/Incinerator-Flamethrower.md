# Incinerator Flamethrower

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Another iconic SCT design, the Incinerator has been produced in almost this format for many hundreds of years. The simple, sleek lines, separate pilot light and easy-change fuel bottle are a design that does not need to be changed! The Incinerator is suitable for anti-personnel use, as well as clearing vegetation and dealing with vermin.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 6
- **Ammo/Slot:** 2
- **Range:** V.Sh
- **Price:** 70 Cr

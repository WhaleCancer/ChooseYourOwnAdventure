# Shard Heavy Pistol

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

Developed as an an alternative sidearm for those troopers who need to pack more of a punch, this heavy laser pistol has a MkVII focussing unit and an XC class amplifier for optimal damage output. As with all of the Shard pistol range, a custom designed holster is provided to hold the gun securely during frantic activity yet still allow a rapid draw.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 12
- **Ammo/Slot:** 4
- **Range:** Med
- **Price:** 300 Cr

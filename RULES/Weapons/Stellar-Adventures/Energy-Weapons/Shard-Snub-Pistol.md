# Shard Snub Pistol

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

This weapon is issued to undercover agents of the Shard Military Foundation who require a discreet but effective weapon. The included holster has numerous attachments to allow it to be fixed inside or outside clothing and the weapon itself is light and non-metallic to prevent detection by basic scanners. The power pack can also be charged directly from a public powerpoint if required.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 4
- **Ammo/Slot:** 4
- **Range:** Sh
- **Price:** 70 Cr

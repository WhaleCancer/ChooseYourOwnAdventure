# PBW2000 Laser Rifle

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

This hip-fired laser rifle has no stock and a double power pack mounting for extended options in the field. The high-intensity laser generator produces an increased power output at the cost of a shorter focal range making it ideal for shock troops and special forces. This weapon is of high quality making it both reliable and resilient and armourers like the lack of maintenance required.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 36
- **Ammo/Slot:** 3
- **Range:** Med
- **Price:** 260 Cr

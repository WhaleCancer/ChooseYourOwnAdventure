# Hellstorm Plasma Cannon

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

This premium weapon from Achenar Laboratories is almost entirely hand-crafted by master gunsmiths. The high quality of components and the care and skill lavished on the construction results in a weapon of the highest calibre. AL report that the plasma produced by this weapon can reach 40,000C, some thirty percent higher than comparable weapons from other manufacturers.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 6
- **Ammo/Slot:** 2
- **Range:** Short
- **Price:** 1100 Cr

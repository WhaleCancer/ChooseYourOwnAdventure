# GB61 Heavy Pistol

**Source:** Stellar Adventures Weapons and Armour Catalogue

It is rumoured that the ancient company of Gralax and Baltar employed one of the Galaxy's top fashion designers to help them style their 6-series weapons. This Blaster Rifle employs standard technologies but packages it in a stylish and distinctive unit. G&B are reported to have sold huge quantities of these weapons to the elite units of numerous planets.

## Stats

- **Ammo:** 14
- **Ammo/Slot:** 4
- **Range:** Med
- **Price:** 500 Cr

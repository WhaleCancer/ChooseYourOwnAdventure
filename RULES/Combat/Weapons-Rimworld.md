# Weapons - Rimworld

**Note:** Statistics have been converted from Rimworld's combat system to Advanced Fighting Fantasy damage track system. Original Rimworld values (damage, DPS, range, etc.) are provided in individual weapon files for reference. Rimworld uses different statistics including damage types (sharp, blunt, heat), accuracy percentages, and DPS values. These have been converted to AFF's 1-7+ damage track format.

## Core Game Weapons

For detailed weapon statistics, refer to the individual weapon files or the [RimWorld Wiki](https://rimworld.fandom.com/wiki/Weapons).

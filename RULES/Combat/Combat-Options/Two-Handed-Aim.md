# Two-Handed Aim

**Type:** Ranged Combat Option  
**Available to:** Handguns and other one-handed weapons

When using a weapon that supports two-handed aiming, you may use both hands to steady the weapon for improved accuracy.

## Effect

- Gain a **+1 bonus** to your attack roll when aiming with two hands
- You **cannot move** in the same round when using Two-Handed Aim
- The weapon must be designed to be used with two hands (or have this option listed in its Combat Options)

## Usage

Declare Two-Handed Aim before making your attack roll. You must remain stationary for the round, but gain improved accuracy from the steady two-handed grip.

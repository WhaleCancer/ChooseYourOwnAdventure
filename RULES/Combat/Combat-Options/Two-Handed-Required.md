# Two-Handed Required

**Type:** Weapon Limitation  
**Available to:** Heavy handguns and automatic weapons

Some weapons are too heavy, have too much recoil, or are too difficult to control to be effectively used with one hand.

## Effect

- The weapon **requires two hands** to use effectively
- If fired with only one hand, you suffer a **-1 penalty** to your attack roll
- The weapon's statistics list "Hands Required: 2" to indicate this limitation

# RCMP Security Secretariat Operative

**Type:** RCMP Security Secretariat field operative / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives** are field operatives of the RCMP Security Secretariat, the secretive intelligence branch that continues to operate in 1985. These operatives are the boots-on-the-ground agents who carry out the Security Secretariat's most dangerous and sensitive missions. They're highly trained, well-equipped, and operate with extreme autonomy, often working outside normal legal constraints to protect national security.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 10  
**STAMINA:** 11  
**HABITAT:** Classified operations, field operations, and high-risk missions  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** High

## Characteristics

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives are:

- **Elite** - Most highly trained operatives in the Security Secretariat
- **Autonomous** - Operate with extreme independence and authority
- **Ruthless** - Willing to use extreme measures to complete missions
- **Well-equipped** - Access to the best weapons and equipment
- **Dangerous** - Extremely effective in both intelligence and combat operations
- **Secretive** - Operate completely outside normal law enforcement visibility

## Abilities

### Elite Training

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives are highly trained for specialized operations. They gain +2 to SKILL for tactical operations, coordinated attacks, and using specialized equipment.

### Autonomous Operations

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives operate with extreme autonomy. They can make decisions, authorize operations, and use resources without normal legal constraints when national security is at stake.

### Covert Mastery

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives are masters of covert operations. They gain +2 to SKILL for stealth-related actions and may appear suddenly from concealment, gaining +3 to SKILL for the first attack if they successfully ambush opponents.

### Precision Elimination

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives are skilled at eliminating threats. When making attacks against unaware or surprised targets, they gain +2 to damage and may attempt to eliminate targets with a single strike (GM's discretion).

### Classified Resources

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives have access to classified resources. They can call for support, access specialized equipment, or coordinate with other intelligence agencies within 1d4+1 rounds.

### Mission Focus

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives are completely focused on their missions. They gain +1 to SKILL and +1 to STAMINA when operating in their area of expertise or completing mission objectives. They will not stop until the mission is complete or they are eliminated.

## In Play

RCMP Security Secretariat Operatives serve as:

- **Elite threats** - Most dangerous RCMP opponents
- **Covert operations** - Operating completely in the shadows
- **Elimination** - Sent to eliminate specific high-value targets or threats
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as major antagonists
- **Classified missions** - Involved in the most sensitive operations
- **Story elements** - May be investigating or opposing the same threats as player characters

### Game Considerations

- Security Secretariat Operatives are elite and extremely dangerous
- They operate with extreme autonomy and may use methods regular law enforcement cannot
- Their covert capabilities make them difficult to detect or track
- They may have knowledge of classified or paranormal threats
- Their presence indicates serious national security concerns
- They represent the highest level of RCMP capability and autonomy

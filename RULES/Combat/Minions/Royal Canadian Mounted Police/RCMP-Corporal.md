# RCMP Corporal

**Type:** Royal Canadian Mounted Police supervisor / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**RCMP Corporals** are supervisory officers who lead detachments, coordinate operations, and make tactical decisions. These officers have risen through the ranks through experience and leadership ability, serving as the bridge between constables and senior command. They're experienced, tactical, and responsible for directing RCMP operations in their areas of responsibility.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 10  
**HABITAT:** RCMP detachments, operational command, and field operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Unfriendly-Hostile (depending on circumstances)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-High

## Characteristics

RCMP Corporals are:

- **Experienced** - Years of service and leadership experience
- **Tactical** - Skilled at coordinating operations and making decisions
- **Authoritative** - Command respect and can direct other officers
- **Dedicated** - Committed to protecting the public and enforcing federal law
- **Strategic** - Think beyond immediate situations
- **Responsible** - Accountable for their detachments and operations

## Equipment

RCMP Corporals typically carry:

- **Handguns** - [Service revolvers](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Handguns/Revolvers/Service Revolver.md) (Smith & Wesson Model 10, .38 Special) or service pistols
- **Shotguns** - [Pump-action shotguns](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Shotguns/Pump Shotgun.md) (Remington 870) in patrol vehicles
- **Rifles** - [C1A1](../../Weapons/Rifles/Combat-Rifles/C1A1.md) or other tactical rifles for specialized operations
- **Less-lethal** - Batons, handcuffs, and other restraint equipment
- **Radio** - For communication and coordination
- **Uniform** - Standard RCMP uniform with corporal's insignia
- **Body armor** - May wear soft body armor for protection

## Abilities

### Leadership

RCMP Corporals can command other officers. When leading other RCMP officers, the corporal grants +1 to SKILL to all RCMP officers in the encounter for coordination and combat actions.

### Tactical Command

RCMP Corporals are skilled at coordinating operations. They gain +1 to SKILL for tactical decisions, coordinating units, and directing RCMP operations.

### Experience

RCMP Corporals have extensive experience. They gain +1 to SKILL for awareness tests and may act first in surprise situations. They can read situations and respond appropriately.

### Authority

RCMP Corporals have significant federal authority. They can make tactical decisions, direct operations, and coordinate multiple units. Opponents may be intimidated by their authority and command presence.

### Backup Coordination

RCMP Corporals can coordinate backup and resources. They can call for additional units, specialized teams, or resources, potentially bringing significant RCMP response within 1d4+1 rounds.

## In Play

RCMP Corporals serve as:

- **Command elements** - Leading and coordinating RCMP operations
- **Tactical threats** - More experienced and strategic than constables
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of RCMP operations and procedures
- **Potential allies** - May assist legitimate investigations
- **Obstacles** - May coordinate RCMP response against player characters
- **Story elements** - May be involved in larger investigations or operations

### Game Considerations

- Corporals are experienced leaders, not just combatants
- They will coordinate operations and direct other officers
- Their authority makes them more dangerous than individual officers
- They may be more willing to negotiate than lower-ranking officers
- They often appear with other RCMP officers as a command element
- Their presence indicates serious RCMP response and coordination

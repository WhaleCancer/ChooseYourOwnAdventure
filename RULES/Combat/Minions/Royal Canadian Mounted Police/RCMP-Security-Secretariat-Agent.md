# RCMP Security Secretariat Agent

**Type:** RCMP Security Secretariat intelligence agent / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**RCMP Security Secretariat Agents** are intelligence and security operatives of the RCMP Security Secretariat, the secretive intelligence branch of the RCMP that continues to operate in 1985. Unlike the real world where the Security Service was disbanded in 1984, in this timeline the Security Secretariat remains active, operating in the shadows to protect national security and investigate threats that fall outside normal law enforcement. These agents are highly trained, well-equipped, and operate with significant autonomy and secrecy.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 9  
**STAMINA:** 10  
**HABITAT:** Classified operations, surveillance locations, and secret facilities  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3 (1d3)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** High

## Characteristics

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents are:

- **Secretive** - Operate in the shadows with minimal visibility
- **Intelligent** - Skilled at investigation, analysis, and intelligence gathering
- **Well-trained** - Highly trained in surveillance, infiltration, and combat
- **Autonomous** - Operate with significant independence and authority
- **Dangerous** - Extremely effective in both intelligence and combat operations
- **Dedicated** - Committed to protecting national security by any means necessary

## Abilities

### Intelligence Operations

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents are skilled at intelligence gathering. They gain +2 to SKILL for investigation, surveillance, and understanding threats. They can piece together information and build comprehensive intelligence pictures.

### Covert Operations

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents excel at covert operations. They gain +2 to SKILL for stealth-related actions and may appear suddenly from concealment, gaining +2 to SKILL for the first attack if they successfully ambush opponents.

### Classified Knowledge

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents have access to classified information. They may know about classified operations, threats, and intelligence that regular law enforcement does not have access to.

### Autonomous Authority

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents operate with significant autonomy. They can make decisions, authorize operations, and access resources that regular RCMP officers cannot. They may operate outside normal legal constraints when national security is at stake.

### Tactical Expertise

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents are highly trained in combat. They gain +1 to SKILL for tactical operations and combat actions, representing their elite training and experience.

### Network Access

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents have access to intelligence networks. They can call for support, access classified resources, or coordinate with other intelligence agencies within 1d4+1 rounds.

## In Play

RCMP Security Secretariat Agents serve as:

- **Intelligence threats** - Gathering information and investigating threats
- **Elite enemies** - Highly trained and dangerous opponents
- **Covert operations** - Operating in the shadows
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of classified operations and threats
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as major antagonists
- **Story elements** - May be investigating the same threats as player characters

### Game Considerations

- Security Secretariat Agents are elite and extremely dangerous
- They operate with significant autonomy and secrecy
- Their intelligence capabilities make them dangerous to criminal and paranormal operations
- They may have knowledge of classified or paranormal threats
- Their presence indicates serious national security concerns
- They represent the secretive, intelligence-focused aspect of law enforcement

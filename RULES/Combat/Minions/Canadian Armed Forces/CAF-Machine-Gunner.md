# CAF Machine Gunner

**Type:** Canadian Armed Forces machine gunner / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**CAF Machine Gunners** are specialized soldiers trained to operate machine guns for suppressive fire and area denial. In 1985, these soldiers may use the new C9 light machine gun or the older C2A1 automatic rifle, providing sustained fire support for infantry units. They're essential for controlling areas, suppressing enemy positions, and providing covering fire.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 11  
**HABITAT:** Support positions, defensive positions, and infantry operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

CAF Machine Gunners are:

- **Specialized** - Trained in machine gun operations and tactics
- **Strong** - Physically capable of handling heavy weapons
- **Tactical** - Skilled at positioning and suppressive fire
- **Dedicated** - Committed to providing fire support
- **Dangerous** - Can deliver devastating sustained fire
- **Professional** - Trained to handle combat situations

## Equipment

CAF Machine Gunners typically carry:

- **Machine guns** - [[../../Weapons/Machine-Guns/C9-Light-Machine-Gun|C9 LMG]] (new issue) or [[../../Weapons/Machine-Guns/C2A1-Automatic-Rifle|C2A1]] (older issue) for sustained fire
- **Backup weapons** - [[../../Weapons/Rifles/Combat-Rifles/C7|C7 assault rifle]] or handguns for personal defense
- **Ammunition** - Extra ammunition and belts for sustained fire
- **Bipods** - For stable firing positions
- **Military uniforms** - Standard Canadian military uniforms with appropriate insignia

## Abilities

### Machine Gun Mastery

CAF Machine Gunners are experts at operating machine guns. They gain +2 to SKILL when using machine guns, and can deliver sustained, accurate fire.

### Suppressive Fire

CAF Machine Gunners excel at suppressive fire. When providing suppressive fire, they can force multiple opponents to take cover, reducing SKILL by -1 for all targets in the area for one round.

### Area Denial

CAF Machine Gunners can deny areas to opponents. When positioned effectively, they can control access to areas, forcing opponents to take cover or find alternate routes.

### Sustained Fire

CAF Machine Gunners can deliver sustained fire for extended periods. They can maintain suppressive fire while other units move or reposition, providing covering fire.

### Heavy Weapon Handling

CAF Machine Gunners are physically strong and capable. They can handle heavy weapons effectively, and gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of operating heavy weapons.

## In Play

CAF Machine Gunners serve as:

- **Support elements** - Providing fire support for other units
- **Area denial** - Controlling access to areas through suppressive fire
- **Combat encounters** - Dangerous when positioned effectively
- **Tactical elements** - Essential for coordinated operations
- **Elite threats** - More dangerous than standard infantry

### Game Considerations

- Machine Gunners are specialized support units
- They often appear with other infantry soldiers
- Their suppressive fire makes them dangerous even when not directly attacking
- They require positioning and support to be most effective
- Their presence indicates serious military operations
- They represent the firepower capability of military units

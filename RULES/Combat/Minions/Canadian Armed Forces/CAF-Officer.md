# CAF Officer

**Type:** Canadian Armed Forces commissioned officer / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**CAF Officers** are commissioned officers who lead military units, make tactical decisions, and coordinate operations. These officers have received officer training and are responsible for commanding soldiers and completing missions. They're strategic, tactical, and responsible for the success of military operations.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 10  
**HABITAT:** Command positions, operational headquarters, and field command  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile-Unfriendly  
**INTELLIGENCE:** High

## Characteristics

CAF Officers are:

- **Strategic** - Skilled at planning and coordinating operations
- **Tactical** - Make tactical decisions under pressure
- **Authoritative** - Command respect and can direct soldiers
- **Experienced** - Many have operational experience
- **Responsible** - Accountable for their units and missions
- **Professional** - Trained to handle command situations

## Equipment

CAF Officers typically carry:

- **Rifles** - [[../../Weapons/Rifles/Combat-Rifles/C7|C7 assault rifle]] or [[../../Weapons/Rifles/Combat-Rifles/C1A1|C1A1]] for personal defense
- **Handguns** - [[../../Weapons/Handguns/Canadian-Browning-High-Power|Browning Hi-Power]] (Canadian service pistol)
- **Communication devices** - Radios and other devices for command and coordination
- **Military uniforms** - Standard Canadian military uniforms with officer insignia
- **Command equipment** - Maps, compasses, and other command tools

## Abilities

### Command Authority

CAF Officers can command other soldiers. When leading other military personnel, the officer grants +1 to SKILL to all soldiers in the unit for coordination and combat actions.

### Tactical Command

CAF Officers are skilled at coordinating operations. They gain +1 to SKILL for tactical decisions, coordinating units, and directing military operations.

### Strategic Thinking

CAF Officers are trained in strategic thinking. They gain +1 to SKILL for awareness tests and may act first in surprise situations. They can read situations and respond appropriately.

### Resource Coordination

CAF Officers can coordinate resources and support. They can call for artillery, air support, or other resources, potentially bringing significant military response within 1d4+1 rounds (GM's discretion).

### Leadership

CAF Officers inspire their soldiers. When leading a unit, soldiers gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of determining when they fall unconscious, representing their determination to follow their officer.

## In Play

CAF Officers serve as:

- **Command elements** - Leading and coordinating military operations
- **Tactical threats** - More strategic and coordinated than individual soldiers
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of military operations and procedures
- **Potential allies** - May assist legitimate operations
- **Obstacles** - May coordinate military response against player characters
- **Story elements** - May be involved in larger military operations or classified missions

### Game Considerations

- Officers are strategic leaders, not just combatants
- They will coordinate operations and direct other soldiers
- Their authority makes them more dangerous than individual soldiers
- They may be more willing to negotiate than lower-ranking soldiers
- They often appear with other soldiers as a command element
- Their presence indicates serious military operations and coordination

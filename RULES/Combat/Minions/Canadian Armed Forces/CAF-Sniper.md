# CAF Sniper

**Type:** Canadian Armed Forces sniper / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**CAF Snipers** are specialized marksmen trained for long-range precision shooting and reconnaissance. These soldiers are experts at concealment, observation, and eliminating high-value targets from extreme distances. They're called in for precision eliminations, reconnaissance, and providing overwatch for operations.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 9  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Overwatch positions, concealed locations, and reconnaissance operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-High

## Characteristics

CAF Snipers are:

- **Precise** - Expert marksmen with exceptional accuracy
- **Patient** - Will wait for the perfect shot
- **Stealthy** - Masters of concealment and observation
- **Tactical** - Skilled at positioning and reconnaissance
- **Dangerous** - Can eliminate targets from extreme distances
- **Professional** - Trained to handle high-stress situations

## Equipment

CAF Snipers typically carry:

- **Sniper rifles** - [C3 sniper rifle](../../Weapons/Rifles/Sniper/C3-Sniper-Rifle.md) (Parker-Hale M82) for long-range precision shooting
- **Backup weapons** - [C7 assault rifle](../../Weapons/Rifles/Combat-Rifles/C7.md) or handguns for close-range defense
- **Optics** - Scopes, binoculars, and observation equipment
- **Camouflage** - Ghillie suits or other concealment equipment
- **Specialized equipment** - Range finders, wind meters, and other precision tools
- **Military uniforms** - Specialized sniper uniforms or camouflage

## Abilities

### Precision Marksmanship

CAF Snipers are expert marksmen. They gain +2 to SKILL when using sniper rifles, and may target specific body parts with called shots. They can eliminate targets from extreme distances.

### Stealth Mastery

CAF Snipers are masters of concealment. They gain +2 to SKILL for stealth-related actions and may remain hidden even when observing targets.

### Patient Hunter

CAF Snipers will wait for the perfect shot. They can observe targets for extended periods, learning patterns and weaknesses, granting +1 to SKILL for all attacks against observed targets.

### Reconnaissance

CAF Snipers are skilled at observation and reconnaissance. They can provide valuable intelligence about targets, locations, and activities without being detected.

### Overwatch

CAF Snipers can provide overwatch for other units. When providing overwatch, they can eliminate threats to other soldiers, gaining +1 to SKILL for attacks against targets threatening their team.

## In Play

CAF Snipers serve as:

- **Long-range threats** - Can eliminate targets from extreme distances
- **Reconnaissance** - Providing intelligence and observation
- **Overwatch** - Protecting other units from concealed positions
- **Elite threats** - Highly dangerous individual opponents
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as elite enemies

### Game Considerations

- Snipers are extremely dangerous at long range
- They often operate alone or in pairs
- Their concealment makes them difficult to detect
- They may provide intelligence before engaging
- Their presence indicates serious military operations
- They represent the highest level of marksmanship capability

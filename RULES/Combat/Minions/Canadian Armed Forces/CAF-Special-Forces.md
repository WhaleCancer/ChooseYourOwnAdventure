# CAF Special Forces

**Type:** Canadian Armed Forces special operations soldier / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**CAF Special Forces** are elite special operations soldiers trained for high-risk missions, counter-terrorism, and specialized operations. In 1985, these may include members of Joint Task Force 2 (JTF-2) or other special operations units. These are the most highly trained and capable soldiers in the Canadian military, called in for the most dangerous and sensitive operations.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 10  
**STAMINA:** 12  
**HABITAT:** Special operations, high-risk missions, and classified deployments  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 2-6 (1d4+1)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** High

## Characteristics

CAF Special Forces are:

- **Elite** - Most highly trained soldiers in the Canadian military
- **Tactical** - Skilled at coordinated, precise operations
- **Well-equipped** - Access to the best weapons and equipment
- **Disciplined** - Follow strict procedures and protocols
- **Dangerous** - Extremely effective in combat situations
- **Professional** - Trained to handle the most dangerous missions

## Equipment

CAF Special Forces typically carry:

- **Rifles** - [C7 assault rifle](../../Weapons/Rifles/Combat-Rifles/C7.md) or specialized tactical rifles
- **SMGs** - [C1 SMG](../../Weapons/SMGs/C1-Submachine-Gun.md) or other tactical SMGs for close-quarters combat
- **Handguns** - [Browning Hi-Power](../../Weapons/Handguns/Canadian-Browning-High-Power.md) or specialized pistols
- **Sniper rifles** - [C3 sniper rifle](../../Weapons/Rifles/Sniper/C3-Sniper-Rifle.md) for designated marksmen
- **Specialized equipment** - Night vision, breaching tools, explosives, and other specialized gear (if available)
- **Body armor** - Tactical vests and protective equipment
- **Military uniforms** - Specialized tactical uniforms or camouflage

## Abilities

### Elite Training

CAF Special Forces are highly trained for specialized operations. They gain +2 to SKILL for tactical operations, coordinated attacks, and using specialized equipment.

### Coordinated Assault

CAF Special Forces excel at coordinated operations. When three or more special forces soldiers are present, each gains +1 to SKILL for coordination and combat actions, representing their ability to work as an elite team.

### Precision Shooting

CAF Special Forces are expert marksmen. They gain +1 to SKILL when using rifles or SMGs, and may target specific body parts with called shots.

### Stealth Operations

CAF Special Forces are trained in stealth and infiltration. They gain +1 to SKILL for stealth-related actions and may appear suddenly from concealment.

### Tactical Awareness

CAF Special Forces are constantly alert. They gain +2 to SKILL for awareness tests and detecting ambushes, and may act first in surprise situations.

### Mission Focus

CAF Special Forces are focused on their mission. They gain +1 to SKILL and +1 to STAMINA when operating in their area of expertise or completing mission objectives.

## In Play

CAF Special Forces serve as:

- **Elite threats** - Most dangerous military opponents
- **Special operations** - High-risk, sensitive missions
- **Coordinated attacks** - Working as elite teams
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as elite enemies
- **Classified operations** - Involved in secret or sensitive missions

### Game Considerations

- Special Forces are elite and extremely dangerous
- They often appear in coordinated teams
- Their specialized training makes them effective in combat
- They will follow proper procedures and coordinate attacks
- Their presence indicates serious military operations
- They represent the highest level of military capability

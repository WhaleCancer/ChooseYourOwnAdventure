# Biker Mechanic

**Type:** Outlaw motorcycle club mechanic / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Biker Mechanics** are the technical specialists of outlaw motorcycle clubs, responsible for maintaining and modifying motorcycles, vehicles, and equipment. Inspired by the skilled mechanics of 1980s action movies like Mad Max 2: The Road Warrior, these bikers are experts at keeping the club's vehicles running and may create custom modifications or improvised weapons. They're often found in clubhouse garages or on the road fixing broken-down bikes.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Clubhouses, garages, and wherever vehicles need maintenance  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3 (1d3)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile-Unfriendly  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-High

## Characteristics

Biker Mechanics are:

- **Skilled** - Expert mechanics with extensive technical knowledge
- **Resourceful** - Can fix or modify almost anything with available materials
- **Loyal** - Devoted to the club and keeping its vehicles operational
- **Practical** - Focused on getting things working rather than aesthetics
- **Dangerous** - May create improvised weapons or vehicle modifications
- **Valuable** - Essential to club operations, often protected by other members

## Equipment

Biker Mechanics typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Service pistols (Browning Hi-Power, M1911) or revolvers
- **Shotguns** - Pump-action shotguns (Remington 870, Mossberg 500)
- **Melee weapons** - Wrenches, tire irons, or other tools that double as weapons
- **Tools** - Extensive tool kits for vehicle maintenance and modification
- **Motorcycles** - Custom Harley-Davidsons, often heavily modified
- **Club colors** - Full patches or support patches

## Abilities

### Technical Expertise

Biker Mechanics are expert technicians. They can repair vehicles, modify equipment, and create improvised devices. They gain +2 to SKILL for any technical or mechanical tasks.

### Improvised Weapons

Biker Mechanics can create improvised weapons from available materials. Given time and materials, they can create explosive devices, vehicle modifications, or other dangerous devices.

### Vehicle Knowledge

Biker Mechanics know how to disable or sabotage vehicles. They can quickly disable enemy vehicles, create traps, or modify vehicles for combat use.

### Protected Asset

Biker Mechanics are valuable to the club. Other bikers will prioritize protecting mechanics, and mechanics gain +1 to SKILL when fighting alongside other bikers who are protecting them.

## In Play

Biker Mechanics serve as:

- **Technical support** - Maintaining and modifying club vehicles
- **Improvised weapons** - Creating dangerous devices from available materials
- **Vehicle combat** - Modifying vehicles for combat or disabling enemy vehicles
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of club operations and equipment
- **Protected targets** - Valuable assets that other bikers protect

### Game Considerations

- Mechanics are often found in garages or working on vehicles
- They're valuable assets that other bikers will protect
- Their technical skills make them dangerous in unexpected ways
- They may create improvised weapons or vehicle modifications
- They often have knowledge of club operations and equipment
- Their technical expertise can be used against them if captured

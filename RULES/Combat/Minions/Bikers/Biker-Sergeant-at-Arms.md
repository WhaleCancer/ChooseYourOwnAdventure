# Biker Sergeant-at-Arms

**Type:** Outlaw motorcycle club sergeant-at-arms / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Biker Sergeants-at-Arms** are responsible for club security, discipline, and protection of club leadership. Inspired by the security-focused bikers of 1980s action movies, these bikers are the guardians of the club, responsible for maintaining order, protecting clubhouses, and ensuring the safety of club officers. They're often the first line of defense and the last line of discipline.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 11  
**HABITAT:** Clubhouses, bars, and wherever club leadership is present  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-4 (1d4)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Biker Sergeants-at-Arms are:

- **Vigilant** - Constantly watching for threats and maintaining security
- **Disciplined** - Enforce club rules and maintain order
- **Loyal** - Completely devoted to protecting the club and its leadership
- **Alert** - Skilled at detecting threats and responding quickly
- **Experienced** - Many have military, law enforcement, or security backgrounds
- **Protective** - Will sacrifice themselves to protect club leadership

## Equipment

Biker Sergeants-at-Arms typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Service pistols (Browning Hi-Power, M1911) for quick access
- **Shotguns** - Pump-action shotguns (Remington 870, Mossberg 500) for close-quarters security
- **SMGs** - Light SMGs (Uzi) or machine pistols (MAC-10) for rapid response
- **Melee weapons** - Chains, tire irons, or specialized weapons
- **Motorcycles** - Custom Harley-Davidsons
- **Communication devices** - Radios or other devices for coordinating security
- **Club colors** - Full patches identifying their sergeant-at-arms status

## Abilities

### Security Awareness

Biker Sergeants-at-Arms are constantly alert for threats. They gain +2 to SKILL for awareness tests and detecting ambushes, and may act first in surprise situations.

### Protective Duty

When protecting club leadership or important locations, Sergeants-at-Arms gain +1 to SKILL and +1 to STAMINA. They will sacrifice themselves to protect their charges.

### Quick Response

Biker Sergeants-at-Arms are trained to respond quickly to threats. They gain +1 to SKILL for the first round of combat when responding to an attack or threat.

### Discipline Enforcement

Biker Sergeants-at-Arms can enforce discipline on other bikers. When dealing with club members who violate rules, they gain +1 to SKILL for intimidation and may force compliance through fear.

## In Play

Biker Sergeants-at-Arms serve as:

- **Security** - Protecting clubhouses and club leadership
- **Discipline** - Enforcing club rules and maintaining order
- **First response** - Reacting quickly to threats and attacks
- **Elite guards** - Protecting important locations and people
- **Tactical elements** - Coordinating security and defense

### Game Considerations

- Sergeants-at-Arms are often found protecting clubhouses or leadership
- They're highly alert and difficult to surprise
- They excel at defensive combat and protecting others
- They may be more disciplined and less impulsive than other bikers
- Their protective duty makes them willing to sacrifice themselves
- They often have security or military backgrounds

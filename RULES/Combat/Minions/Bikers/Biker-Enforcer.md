# Biker Enforcer

**Type:** Outlaw motorcycle club enforcer / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Biker Enforcers** are the heavy muscle of outlaw motorcycle clubs, responsible for enforcing discipline, collecting debts, and eliminating threats. Inspired by the brutal enforcers of 1980s action movies like Mad Max and Road Warrior, these bikers are the most physically imposing and violent members of the club. They're sent to deal with problems that require extreme violence and intimidation.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 9  
**STAMINA:** 12  
**HABITAT:** Clubhouses, bars, highways, and criminal operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3 (1d3)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Biker Enforcers are:

- **Violent** - Specialized in extreme violence and intimidation
- **Intimidating** - Physically imposing and terrifying presence
- **Loyal** - Completely devoted to the club and its leadership
- **Ruthless** - Willing to commit extreme acts without hesitation
- **Experienced** - Many are military veterans or have extensive criminal backgrounds
- **Feared** - Even other bikers are afraid of them

## Equipment

Biker Enforcers typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Heavy revolvers (.357: Colt Python, Ruger Security Six) or service pistols (Browning Hi-Power, M1911)
- **Shotguns** - Pump-action shotguns (Remington 870, Mossberg 500), often sawed-off for intimidation
- **SMGs** - Uzi or MAC-10/MAC-11 for close-quarters combat
- **Melee weapons** - Chains, tire irons, baseball bats, or specialized weapons
- **Motorcycles** - Custom Harley-Davidsons, often heavily modified
- **Club colors** - Full patches identifying their enforcer status

## Abilities

### Intimidating Presence

Biker Enforcers are terrifying. Opponents must make a Willpower or LUCK test (GM's choice) or suffer -2 to SKILL for the first round of combat when facing an enforcer for the first time.

### Brutal Strength

Biker Enforcers are physically powerful. They gain +2 to damage on all melee attacks and +1 to damage on ranged attacks.

### Unstoppable

Biker Enforcers push through pain and injury. They do not suffer penalties to SKILL when reduced to half STAMINA or less, and gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of determining when they fall unconscious.

### Enforcer's Duty

When sent to eliminate a specific target or enforce discipline, Biker Enforcers gain +1 to SKILL and will not stop until the target is eliminated or they are killed.

## In Play

Biker Enforcers serve as:

- **Elite threats** - The most dangerous biker opponents
- **Intimidation** - Their presence alone can end conflicts
- **Elimination** - Sent to eliminate specific targets or threats
- **Discipline** - Enforcing club rules and punishing violations
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as leaders or elite enemies

### Game Considerations

- Enforcers are extremely dangerous and should be treated as elite enemies
- Their intimidating presence can end conflicts before they start
- They often appear alone or in very small groups
- They may be sent after specific targets
- Their brutality makes them feared even by other criminals
- They often have military or extensive criminal backgrounds

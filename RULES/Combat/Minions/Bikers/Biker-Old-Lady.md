# Biker Old Lady

**Type:** Biker associate / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Biker Old Ladies** are long-term partners or wives of biker gang members. While not patched members themselves, they are deeply integrated into club culture and often participate in criminal activities. They're tough, loyal, and can be just as dangerous as their partners when the club is threatened.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Clubhouses, biker bars, and criminal operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-4 (1d4)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile-Unfriendly  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Biker Old Ladies are:

- **Loyal** - Fiercely devoted to their partners and the club
- **Tough** - Willing to fight and use violence when necessary
- **Integrated** - Deeply embedded in club culture and operations
- **Resourceful** - Often handle logistics, communication, and support roles
- **Protective** - Will defend their partners and club with extreme prejudice
- **Independent** - Not simply passive partners, but active participants in club activities

## Equipment

Biker Old Ladies typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Compact revolvers or pistols for personal protection
- **Shotguns** - Smaller-gauge shotguns or sawed-off variants
- **Melee weapons** - Knives, chains, or improvised weapons
- **Club colors** - Patches, jewelry, or clothing showing club affiliation
- **Communication devices** - Pagers or access to club communication networks

## Abilities

### Partner's Protection

When fighting alongside their biker partner, the Old Lady gains +1 to SKILL. If their partner is wounded or killed, the Old Lady becomes enraged, gaining +2 to damage rolls but -1 to SKILL (representing reckless aggression).

### Club Network

Biker Old Ladies often handle communication and logistics for the club. They can call for reinforcements or alert other club members, potentially bringing additional bikers to the scene within 1d6+2 rounds.

### Intimidation

Like their partners, Old Ladies use their reputation and presence to intimidate. They may add +1 to SKILL for intimidation tests, particularly when dealing with those who underestimate them.

## In Play

Biker Old Ladies serve as:

- **Supporting antagonists** - Often encountered alongside bikers
- **Combat encounters** - Dangerous when protecting their partners or club
- **Information sources** - May have knowledge of club operations and criminal activities
- **Logistical threats** - Can call for reinforcements or coordinate club responses
- **Social encounters** - May be more approachable than patched members in some situations

### Game Considerations

- Old Ladies are often underestimated but can be just as dangerous as bikers
- Their loyalty to partners creates interesting tactical and social dynamics
- The ability to call reinforcements makes them a significant threat
- They may be more willing to negotiate than patched members in some situations
- Their integration into club culture provides opportunities for information gathering
- They often handle less visible but critical aspects of club operations

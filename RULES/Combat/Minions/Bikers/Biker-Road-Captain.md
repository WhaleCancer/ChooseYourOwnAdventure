# Biker Road Captain

**Type:** Outlaw motorcycle club road captain / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Biker Road Captains** are responsible for leading convoys, planning routes, and coordinating club movements. Inspired by the skilled riders and convoy leaders of 1980s action movies like Mad Max 2: The Road Warrior, these bikers are expert motorcyclists who excel at high-speed chases, convoy operations, and road combat. They're the tactical leaders of club operations on the road.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 10  
**HABITAT:** Highways, rural roads, clubhouses, and convoy operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile-Unfriendly  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-High

## Characteristics

Biker Road Captains are:

- **Skilled riders** - Expert motorcyclists with extensive road experience
- **Tactical** - Skilled at planning routes and coordinating operations
- **Loyal** - Devoted to the club and its operations
- **Experienced** - Many have military or law enforcement backgrounds
- **Resourceful** - Know the roads, routes, and safe houses
- **Dangerous** - Skilled at combat while operating motorcycles

## Equipment

Biker Road Captains typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Service pistols (Browning Hi-Power, M1911) for easy access while riding
- **SMGs** - Light SMGs (Uzi) or machine pistols (MAC-10) for vehicle combat
- **Rifles** - SKS or other semi-auto rifles as truck guns
- **Melee weapons** - Chains, tire irons, or specialized weapons
- **Motorcycles** - Custom Harley-Davidsons, often with modifications for speed and handling
- **Communication devices** - Radios or other devices for coordinating convoys
- **Club colors** - Full patches identifying their road captain status

## Abilities

### Expert Rider

Biker Road Captains are master motorcyclists. They gain +2 to SKILL when operating motorcycles, including combat while riding, high-speed maneuvers, and difficult terrain navigation.

### Convoy Leader

When leading a convoy of bikers, the Road Captain grants +1 to SKILL to all other bikers in the convoy for movement and coordination actions.

### Road Knowledge

Biker Road Captains know the roads, routes, and safe houses. They can plan escape routes, find shortcuts, and locate safe locations. This knowledge grants +1 to SKILL for navigation, pursuit, and evasion actions.

### High-Speed Combat

Biker Road Captains excel at combat while operating motorcycles. They gain +1 to SKILL when making attacks from or while operating a motorcycle, and suffer no penalties for shooting while moving.

## In Play

Biker Road Captains serve as:

- **Convoy leaders** - Leading groups of bikers on operations
- **Chase scenes** - High-speed motorcycle pursuits and escapes
- **Tactical threats** - Coordinating biker operations
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of routes, safe houses, and operations
- **Elite riders** - Most skilled motorcyclists in the club

### Game Considerations

- Road Captains are extremely dangerous in vehicle combat
- They often lead convoys or coordinate operations
- Their road knowledge makes them valuable for information
- They excel at high-speed chases and escapes
- They may be more tactical and less impulsive than other bikers
- Their coordination abilities make them force multipliers

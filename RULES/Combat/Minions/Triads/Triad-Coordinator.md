# Triad Coordinator

**Type:** Chinese organized crime coordinator / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Triad Coordinators** (also known as "Uncles" or "Big Brothers") are the brokers and fixers of Chinese organized crime networks. Inspired by the strategic masterminds of martial arts films, these figures coordinate operations, mediate disputes, and maintain the network's infrastructure. They're less combat-focused but more dangerous due to their connections, knowledge, and ability to mobilize resources. They're the organization's strategic minds, operating behind the scenes.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Chinatown, port areas, legitimate businesses, and coordination centers  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile-Unfriendly  
**INTELLIGENCE:** High

## Characteristics

Triad Coordinators are:

- **Strategic** - Skilled at planning and coordination
- **Connected** - Extensive network of contacts and resources
- **Knowledgeable** - Understand organization operations and criminal networks
- **Influential** - Can mobilize resources and personnel
- **Protected** - Usually surrounded by bodyguards and enforcers
- **Dangerous** - Their connections make them more dangerous than their combat skills suggest

## Equipment

Triad Coordinators typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Service pistols (Browning Hi-Power, M1911) for personal protection
- **Communication devices** - Radios, pagers, or other devices for coordination
- **Documents** - Business papers, contacts, and operational information
- **Protective gear** - May wear concealed armor or have bodyguards
- **Legitimate appearance** - Blend into business or community settings

## Abilities

### Network Coordination

Triad Coordinators can mobilize resources and personnel. When a coordinator is present, they can call for reinforcements, coordinate operations, or access resources within 1d4+1 rounds.

### Strategic Planning

Triad Coordinators are skilled at planning and coordination. They gain +1 to SKILL for awareness tests and may act first in surprise situations. They can provide tactical advantages to other Triad members.

### Information Network

Triad Coordinators have extensive knowledge of organization operations and criminal networks. They may know about operations, contacts, safe houses, and other valuable information.

### Protected Asset

Triad Coordinators are valuable to the organization. Other Triad members will prioritize protecting coordinators, and coordinators gain +1 to SKILL when fighting alongside other Triad members who are protecting them.

### Resource Access

Triad Coordinators can access organization resources. They may have access to safe houses, vehicles, weapons, or other resources that can be mobilized quickly.

## In Play

Triad Coordinators serve as:

- **Strategic threats** - Coordinating operations and mobilizing resources
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of organization operations and networks
- **Network hubs** - Connections to larger criminal operations
- **Protected targets** - Usually surrounded by bodyguards
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as major antagonists or strategic elements

### Game Considerations

- Coordinators are less combat-effective but more dangerous overall
- They're usually protected by other Triad members
- Their knowledge makes them valuable targets for information
- Their connections create escalation opportunities
- They may be more willing to negotiate than combat-focused members
- Their strategic role makes them important for larger storylines

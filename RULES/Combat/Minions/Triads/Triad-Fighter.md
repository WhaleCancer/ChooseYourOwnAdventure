# Triad Fighter

**Type:** Chinese organized crime martial artist / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Triad Fighters** are hand-to-hand combat specialists trained in traditional and modern martial arts. Inspired by the skilled fighters of martial arts films like Enter the Dragon and Ip Man, as well as the aggressive combatants of Shadow Warrior, these operatives excel in close combat, using their training to overwhelm opponents with speed, precision, and technique. They're the organization's close-quarters specialists, sent when discretion or intimidation is required.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 10  
**HABITAT:** Chinatown, gambling dens, and criminal operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-4 (1d4)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Triad Fighters are:

- **Skilled** - Trained in multiple martial arts styles
- **Fast** - Quick strikes and rapid movement
- **Precise** - Target vital points and weaknesses
- **Disciplined** - Maintain focus and technique under pressure
- **Confident** - Prefer hand-to-hand combat over firearms
- **Resilient** - Push through pain and injury

## Equipment

Triad Fighters typically carry:

- **Melee weapons** - Knives, batons, or traditional weapons
- **Handguns** - Compact pistols for backup (prefer not to use)
- **Traditional weapons** - May carry hidden weapons or improvised tools
- **Street clothes** - Blend into urban environment
- **Protective gear** - Minimal, relying on skill and speed

## Abilities

### Martial Arts Mastery

Triad Fighters are masters of hand-to-hand combat. They gain +2 to SKILL for all melee combat, and +2 to damage on unarmed attacks. They can perform acrobatic maneuvers, use their environment, and combine strikes effectively.

### Rapid Strikes

Triad Fighters can deliver multiple strikes in rapid succession. They may make an additional melee attack every other round, representing their speed and technique.

### Vital Point Targeting

Triad Fighters are skilled at targeting vital points. When making melee attacks, they may choose to target specific body parts, gaining +1 to damage and potentially disabling opponents (GM's discretion).

### Acrobatic Movement

Triad Fighters are skilled at acrobatic movement. They gain +1 to SKILL for movement, dodging, and using the environment, and may perform impressive feats of agility.

### Iron Will

Triad Fighters maintain discipline under pressure. They do not suffer penalties to SKILL when reduced to half STAMINA or less, and gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of determining when they fall unconscious.

## In Play

Triad Fighters serve as:

- **Close combat specialists** - Excelling in hand-to-hand combat
- **Intimidation** - Their skill creates fear and respect
- **Discipline** - Enforcing organization rules through combat
- **Elite threats** - More dangerous than regular criminals
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as elite enemies or bodyguards

### Game Considerations

- Fighters are extremely dangerous in close combat
- They prefer hand-to-hand combat but will use weapons if necessary
- Their speed and technique make them difficult to hit
- They may have knowledge of organization operations
- Their training makes them more tactical than street-level criminals
- They represent the traditional martial arts aspect of organized crime

# Triad Gunman

**Type:** Chinese organized crime firearms specialist / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Triad Gunmen** are firearms specialists trained in the "gun fu" style of combat, combining martial arts movement with precision shooting. Inspired by the skilled gunmen of Hong Kong action films like Hard Boiled and the aggressive Triad gunmen of Shadow Warrior, these operatives excel at close-quarters gunplay, using acrobatic movement and rapid fire to overwhelm opponents. They're the organization's firearms experts, sent when firepower and precision are required. Like their Shadow Warrior counterparts, they favor Uzis and other SMGs for their rapid-fire capabilities.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Urban environments, port areas, and criminal operations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 2-5 (1d4+1)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Triad Gunmen are:

- **Skilled** - Expert marksmen with exceptional accuracy
- **Mobile** - Combine movement with shooting
- **Precise** - Target vital points and weak spots
- **Disciplined** - Maintain accuracy under pressure
- **Efficient** - Prefer rapid, decisive action
- **Tactical** - Use cover and movement effectively

## Equipment

Triad Gunmen typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Service pistols (Browning Hi-Power, M1911) or compact weapons
- **SMGs** - Light SMGs (Uzi) or machine pistols (MAC-10) for close-quarters combat, favored for their rapid-fire capabilities like the Triad gunmen of Shadow Warrior
- **Shotguns** - May carry shotguns for devastating close-range firepower
- **Backup weapons** - Additional handguns or knives
- **Ammunition** - Extra magazines for sustained fire
- **Street clothes** - Blend into urban environment
- **Communication devices** - Radios or pagers for coordination

## Abilities

### Gun Fu Mastery

Triad Gunmen are masters of combining martial arts with firearms. They gain +2 to SKILL when using handguns or SMGs, and may move and shoot in the same round without penalty. They can perform acrobatic maneuvers while shooting.

### Rapid Fire

Triad Gunmen can deliver rapid, accurate fire. They may make an additional attack every other round when using automatic or semi-automatic weapons, representing their speed and skill.

### Precision Shooting

Triad Gunmen are skilled at targeting vital points. When making ranged attacks, they may choose to target specific body parts, gaining +1 to damage and potentially disabling opponents (GM's discretion).

### Acrobatic Shooting

Triad Gunmen are skilled at shooting while moving. They gain +1 to SKILL for movement and shooting actions, and may use cover and movement to their advantage.

### Tactical Awareness

Triad Gunmen are skilled at using their environment. They gain +1 to SKILL for awareness tests and may use cover, movement, and positioning to gain advantages in combat.

## In Play

Triad Gunmen serve as:

- **Firearms specialists** - Excelling in ranged combat
- **Rapid response** - Quick to engage and eliminate threats
- **Elite threats** - More dangerous than regular criminals
- **Tactical elements** - Using firepower and movement effectively
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as elite enemies or bodyguards

### Game Considerations

- Gunmen are extremely dangerous at range
- Their gun fu style makes them mobile and hard to hit
- They often appear in small groups for coordinated fire
- They may have knowledge of organization operations
- Their training makes them more tactical than street-level criminals
- They represent the modern, firearms-focused aspect of organized crime

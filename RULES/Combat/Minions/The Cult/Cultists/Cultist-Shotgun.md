# Shotgun Cultist

**Type:** Cult member / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Shotgun Cultists** are cult members who favor double-barrel shotguns for their traditional, ritualistic appearance. These acolytes are drawn to the weapon's symbolic significance and use it as their primary tool for violence and intimidation.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Remote compounds, abandoned buildings, secret facilities  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 2-6 (1d4+1)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-Low

## Equipment

Shotgun Cultists carry:

- **Shotguns** - [[../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Shotguns/Double-Barrel Shotgun|Double-barrel shotguns]], drawn to their traditional, ritualistic appearance
- **Melee weapons** - Ritual knives or improvised weapons
- **Ritual items** - Symbols, fetishes, or artifacts related to their beliefs

## Abilities

### Fanatical Devotion

Shotgun Cultists fight to the death and will not surrender. They gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of determining when they fall unconscious.

### Close-Range Specialist

Shotgun Cultists excel at close-range combat. When within Short range, they gain +1 to damage with their shotguns.

### Ritual Preparation

At the start of combat, roll 1d6: on a 5-6, the cultist gains +1 to SKILL for the first three rounds of combat, representing ritual enhancement.

## In Play

Shotgun Cultists serve as close-range threats, using their shotguns to devastating effect at short distances. They're fanatical fighters who will not surrender.

# Handgun Cultist

**Type:** Cult member / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Handgun Cultists** are cult members who favor compact handguns, particularly the Walther PP, for their concealability and symbolic significance. These acolytes use handguns as their primary weapons, preferring precision and intimidation over overwhelming firepower.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Remote compounds, abandoned buildings, secret facilities  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 2-6 (1d4+1)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-Low

## Equipment

Handgun Cultists carry:

- **Handguns** - Walther PP or other compact pistols (use [Combat Handgun](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Handguns/Pistols/Combat Handgun.md) stats), favored for their concealability and symbolic appearance
- **Backup weapons** - Ritual knives or improvised weapons
- **Ritual items** - Symbols, fetishes, or artifacts related to their beliefs

## Abilities

### Fanatical Devotion

Handgun Cultists fight to the death and will not surrender. They gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of determining when they fall unconscious.

### Precision Shooting

Handgun Cultists are skilled with handguns. When using handguns, they gain +1 to SKILL for the first attack in combat.

### Ritual Preparation

At the start of combat, roll 1d6: on a 5-6, the cultist gains +1 to SKILL for the first three rounds of combat, representing ritual enhancement.

## In Play

Handgun Cultists serve as ranged threats, using handguns for precision shooting and intimidation. They're fanatical fighters who will not surrender.

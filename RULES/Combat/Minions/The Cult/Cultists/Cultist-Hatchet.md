# Axe Cultist

**Type:** Cult member / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Axe Cultists** are cult members who favor axes and other bladed melee weapons for their ritualistic and intimidating appearance. These acolytes prefer close combat, using their weapons to deliver brutal, symbolic violence.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Remote compounds, abandoned buildings, secret facilities  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 2-6 (1d4+1)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-Low

## Equipment

Axe Cultists carry:

- **Axes** - Ritual axes, hatchets, or other bladed melee weapons
- **Backup weapons** - Ritual knives or improvised weapons
- **Ritual items** - Symbols, fetishes, or artifacts related to their beliefs

## Abilities

### Fanatical Devotion

Axe Cultists fight to the death and will not surrender. They gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of determining when they fall unconscious.

### Melee Specialist

Axe Cultists excel at close combat. When making melee attacks, they gain +1 to damage with axes or bladed weapons.

### Ritual Preparation

At the start of combat, roll 1d6: on a 5-6, the cultist gains +1 to SKILL for the first three rounds of combat, representing ritual enhancement.

## In Play

Axe Cultists serve as melee threats, charging into close combat with brutal, ritualistic violence. They're fanatical fighters who will not surrender.

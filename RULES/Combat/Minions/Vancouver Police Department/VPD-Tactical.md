# VPD Tactical Officer

**Type:** Vancouver Police Department tactical/SWAT officer / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**VPD Tactical Officers** are specialized police officers trained for high-risk operations, hostage situations, and tactical responses. These are the elite of the Vancouver Police Department, called in when standard police procedures are insufficient. They're highly trained, well-equipped, and prepared for the most dangerous situations.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 9  
**STAMINA:** 11  
**HABITAT:** Tactical operations, high-risk situations, and specialized deployments  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 2-6 (1d4+1)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average-High

## Characteristics

VPD Tactical Officers are:

- **Elite** - Highly trained for specialized operations
- **Tactical** - Skilled at coordinated, precise operations
- **Well-equipped** - Access to specialized weapons and equipment
- **Disciplined** - Follow strict procedures and protocols
- **Dangerous** - Extremely effective in combat situations
- **Professional** - Trained to handle high-stress situations

## Equipment

VPD Tactical Officers typically carry:

- **SMGs** - [MP5](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/SMGs/SMG.md) or other tactical SMGs for close-quarters combat
- **Rifles** - [C1A1](../../Weapons/Rifles/Combat-Rifles/C1A1.md) or other tactical rifles for precision shooting
- **Shotguns** - [Pump-action shotguns](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Shotguns/Pump Shotgun.md) (Remington 870) for breaching and close-range
- **Handguns** - Service pistols as backup weapons
- **Body armor** - Tactical vests and protective equipment
- **Tactical gear** - Flashbangs, breaching tools, and specialized equipment (if available)
- **Radio** - For communication and coordination

## Abilities

### Tactical Training

VPD Tactical Officers are highly trained for specialized operations. They gain +2 to SKILL for tactical operations, coordinated attacks, and using specialized equipment.

### Coordinated Assault

VPD Tactical Officers excel at coordinated operations. When three or more tactical officers are present, each gains +1 to SKILL for coordination and combat actions, representing their ability to work as a team.

### Precision Shooting

VPD Tactical Officers are expert marksmen. They gain +1 to SKILL when using rifles or SMGs, and may target specific body parts with called shots.

### Breaching

VPD Tactical Officers are trained in breaching techniques. They can quickly enter buildings, vehicles, or other structures, gaining +1 to SKILL for breaching actions.

### Tactical Awareness

VPD Tactical Officers are constantly alert. They gain +2 to SKILL for awareness tests and detecting ambushes, and may act first in surprise situations.

## In Play

VPD Tactical Officers serve as:

- **Elite threats** - Most dangerous police opponents
- **Tactical operations** - Specialized high-risk situations
- **Coordinated attacks** - Working as a team to overwhelm opponents
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as elite enemies
- **Specialized deployments** - Called in for specific situations

### Game Considerations

- Tactical Officers are elite and extremely dangerous
- They often appear in coordinated teams
- Their specialized training makes them effective in combat
- They will follow proper procedures and coordinate attacks
- Their presence indicates serious police response
- They represent the highest level of police capability

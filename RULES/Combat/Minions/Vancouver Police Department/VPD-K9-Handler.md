# VPD K9 Handler

**Type:** Vancouver Police Department K9 unit officer / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**VPD K9 Handlers** are police officers who work with trained police dogs for tracking, search, and apprehension. These specialized units combine the skills of trained officers with the abilities of police dogs, creating a highly effective team for law enforcement operations. They're called in for tracking suspects, searching buildings, and apprehending dangerous individuals.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Urban streets, search operations, and tracking scenarios  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Unfriendly-Hostile (depending on circumstances)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

VPD K9 Handlers are:

- **Specialized** - Trained in K9 operations and dog handling
- **Dedicated** - Committed to their dogs and their work
- **Experienced** - Skilled at working with police dogs
- **Tactical** - Use dogs as force multipliers
- **Resourceful** - Combine human and canine abilities effectively
- **Professional** - Trained to handle dangerous situations

## Equipment

VPD K9 Handlers typically carry:

- **Handguns** - [Service revolvers](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Handguns/Revolvers/Service Revolver.md) (Smith & Wesson Model 10, .38 Special)
- **Less-lethal** - Batons, handcuffs, and other restraint equipment
- **Radio** - For communication with dispatch and other officers
- **K9 equipment** - Leash, commands, and dog handling tools
- **Uniform** - Standard police uniform with K9 unit identification
- **Body armor** - May wear soft body armor for protection

## Abilities

### K9 Team

VPD K9 Handlers work with trained police dogs. The handler and dog work as a team, with the dog providing tracking, search, and apprehension capabilities. The dog can track suspects, search buildings, and assist in apprehension.

### Tracking

VPD K9 Handlers can track suspects using their dogs. They gain +2 to SKILL for tracking and search operations when using their K9 partner.

### Apprehension

VPD K9 Handlers can use their dogs to apprehend suspects. The dog can attack, hold, or subdue suspects, providing a non-lethal option for apprehension. The dog gains +1 to SKILL for attack and grapple actions.

### Search

VPD K9 Handlers can use their dogs to search buildings or areas. The dog can detect hidden suspects, drugs, or other evidence, providing valuable intelligence.

### Team Coordination

VPD K9 Handlers coordinate with their dogs effectively. The handler and dog work together, with the handler directing the dog and the dog providing capabilities the handler cannot.

## In Play

VPD K9 Handlers serve as:

- **Tracking units** - Following suspects and finding hidden individuals
- **Search teams** - Locating suspects, evidence, or contraband
- **Apprehension** - Using dogs to subdue suspects non-lethally
- **Combat encounters** - Dangerous when working with their dogs
- **Information sources** - Dogs can provide valuable intelligence

### Game Considerations

- K9 Handlers are specialized units, not general patrol
- They work as a team with their dogs, creating unique tactical situations
- Dogs can track, search, and apprehend, making them versatile
- The handler and dog must work together effectively
- They often appear in pairs (handler and dog)
- Their presence indicates specialized police operations

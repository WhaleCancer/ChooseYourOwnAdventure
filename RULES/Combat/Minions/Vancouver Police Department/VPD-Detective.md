# VPD Detective

**Type:** Vancouver Police Department detective / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**VPD Detectives** are plainclothes investigators who handle criminal investigations, gather evidence, and build cases. These officers work behind the scenes, using their investigative skills to solve crimes and bring criminals to justice. They're less visible than patrol officers but often more dangerous due to their experience and knowledge of criminal operations.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 8  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Police stations, crime scenes, and investigation locations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-2 (1d2)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Unfriendly-Hostile (depending on circumstances)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** High

## Characteristics

VPD Detectives are:

- **Investigative** - Skilled at gathering evidence and building cases
- **Experienced** - Years of experience with criminal investigations
- **Knowledgeable** - Understand criminal operations and networks
- **Persistent** - Will pursue cases until they're solved
- **Tactical** - More strategic than patrol officers
- **Dedicated** - Committed to solving crimes and bringing justice

## Equipment

VPD Detectives typically carry:

- **Handguns** - [Service revolvers](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Handguns/Revolvers/Service Revolver.md) (Smith & Wesson Model 10, .38 Special) or service pistols for plainclothes work
- **Backup weapons** - May carry additional weapons for protection
- **Less-lethal** - Handcuffs and other restraint equipment
- **Radio** - For communication with dispatch and other officers
- **Plainclothes** - Blend into civilian environment
- **Investigation tools** - Notebooks, cameras, evidence collection equipment

## Abilities

### Investigative Skills

VPD Detectives are skilled investigators. They gain +2 to SKILL for investigation, evidence gathering, and understanding criminal operations. They can piece together clues and build cases effectively.

### Criminal Knowledge

VPD Detectives have extensive knowledge of criminal operations. They may know about criminal networks, operations, safe houses, and other valuable information related to their investigations.

### Tactical Awareness

VPD Detectives are more tactical than patrol officers. They gain +1 to SKILL for awareness tests and may act first in surprise situations. They're skilled at reading situations and responding appropriately.

### Backup Request

VPD Detectives can call for backup. When in danger or facing threats, they can request assistance, potentially bringing additional officers to the scene within 1d6+2 rounds.

### Authority

VPD Detectives have legal authority. They can make arrests, issue commands, and use force when necessary. Their investigative authority may allow them to access information and locations that patrol officers cannot.

## In Play

VPD Detectives serve as:

- **Investigators** - Building cases and gathering evidence
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of criminal operations and networks
- **Combat encounters** - More experienced and tactical than patrol officers
- **Potential allies** - May assist legitimate investigations
- **Obstacles** - May interfere with illegal activities or investigations
- **Story elements** - May be investigating the same cases as player characters

### Game Considerations

- Detectives are professional investigators, not combat specialists
- They will follow proper procedures and build cases methodically
- Their investigative skills make them dangerous to criminal operations
- They may be more willing to work with legitimate investigators
- They often work alone or in pairs
- Their presence indicates active investigations in the area

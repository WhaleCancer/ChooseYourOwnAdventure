# VPD Patrol Officer

**Type:** Vancouver Police Department patrol officer / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**VPD Patrol Officers** are the uniformed police officers who patrol Vancouver's streets, responding to calls, maintaining order, and enforcing the law. These are the most common type of police officer encountered, serving as the first line of defense against crime and the public face of law enforcement. They're trained professionals who must balance public service with the dangerous realities of police work.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 9  
**HABITAT:** Urban streets, patrol routes, and police stations  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-4 (1d4)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Unfriendly-Hostile (depending on circumstances)  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

VPD Patrol Officers are:

- **Professional** - Trained in law enforcement procedures and tactics
- **Cautious** - Aware of the dangers of police work
- **Dedicated** - Committed to protecting the public and enforcing the law
- **Experienced** - Many have years of street experience
- **Resourceful** - Must handle diverse situations with limited resources
- **Vigilant** - Constantly alert for threats and criminal activity

## Equipment

VPD Patrol Officers typically carry:

- **Handguns** - [Service revolvers](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Handguns/Revolvers/Service Revolver.md) (Smith & Wesson Model 10, .38 Special) as standard issue
- **Shotguns** - [Pump-action shotguns](../../Weapons/Canada 198X/_Ranged/Shotguns/Pump Shotgun.md) (Remington 870) in patrol vehicles
- **Less-lethal** - Batons, handcuffs, and other restraint equipment
- **Radio** - For communication with dispatch and other officers
- **Uniform** - Standard police uniform with badge and identification
- **Body armor** - May wear soft body armor for protection

## Abilities

### Police Training

VPD Patrol Officers are trained in law enforcement procedures. They gain +1 to SKILL for awareness tests and detecting criminal activity, and may act first in surprise situations.

### Backup Request

VPD Patrol Officers can call for backup. When in danger or facing threats, they can request assistance, potentially bringing additional officers to the scene within 1d6+2 rounds.

### Authority

VPD Patrol Officers have legal authority. They can make arrests, issue commands, and use force when necessary. Opponents may be intimidated by their authority, suffering -1 to SKILL for the first round when facing police.

### Defensive Tactics

VPD Patrol Officers are trained in defensive tactics. They gain +1 to SKILL for the purpose of defending against attacks, and may use their training to subdue opponents without lethal force.

## In Play

VPD Patrol Officers serve as:

- **Law enforcement** - Enforcing laws and maintaining order
- **First responders** - Responding to calls and incidents
- **Combat encounters** - Well-trained but not specialized for combat
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of local crime and activities
- **Potential allies** - May assist legitimate investigations
- **Obstacles** - May interfere with illegal activities

### Game Considerations

- Patrol Officers are professional law enforcement, not criminals
- They will follow proper procedures and call for backup
- Their authority can be used for or against player characters
- They may be more willing to negotiate than specialized units
- They often work in pairs or small groups
- Their presence indicates active law enforcement in the area

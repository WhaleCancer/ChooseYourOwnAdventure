# Street Gang Dealer

**Type:** Urban drug dealer / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Street Gang Dealers** are drug dealers operating in urban neighborhoods, selling drugs on street corners and in abandoned buildings. Inspired by the drug dealers of the Death Wish series, these criminals are part of the street-level drug trade, serving as the retail end of larger criminal networks. They're armed, paranoid, and willing to use violence to protect their territory and product.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 6  
**STAMINA:** 8  
**HABITAT:** Street corners, abandoned buildings, housing projects, and drug houses  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-4 (1d4)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile-Unfriendly  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Street Gang Dealers are:

- **Paranoid** - Constantly watching for police and rivals
- **Armed** - Carry weapons to protect themselves and their product
- **Territorial** - Defensive of their dealing locations
- **Connected** - Part of larger drug distribution networks
- **Ruthless** - Willing to eliminate threats to their operation
- **Desperate** - Driven by money and addiction

## Equipment

Street Gang Dealers typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Compact pistols or revolvers for easy concealment
- **SMGs** - Light SMGs (Uzi) or machine pistols (MAC-10) when available
- **Melee weapons** - Knives or small weapons for close-quarters
- **Drugs** - Product they're selling (crack, marijuana, etc.)
- **Cash** - Money from sales
- **Communication devices** - Pagers or access to communication networks

## Abilities

### Paranoia

Street Gang Dealers are constantly alert for threats. They gain +1 to SKILL for awareness tests and detecting ambushes, and may act first in surprise situations.

### Territorial Defense

When defending their dealing location or territory, dealers gain +1 to SKILL and will fight more aggressively to protect their operation.

### Network Connection

Street Gang Dealers are connected to larger criminal networks. If a dealer is eliminated or captured, the network may send enforcers or other dealers to investigate or retaliate.

### Quick Draw

Street Gang Dealers keep weapons ready for quick access. They gain +1 to SKILL for the first attack in combat, representing their readiness to use violence.

## In Play

Street Gang Dealers serve as:

- **Drug trade encounters** - Street-level drug operations
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of local criminal networks and activities
- **Combat encounters** - Armed and dangerous but less skilled than organized crime
- **Network entry points** - Connections to larger criminal organizations
- **Urban threats** - Contributing to neighborhood crime and violence

### Game Considerations

- Dealers are often found in specific locations (street corners, abandoned buildings)
- They're paranoid and may flee if outnumbered or outgunned
- Their connections to larger networks create escalation opportunities
- They may provide information about drug operations if properly approached
- They often have lookouts or associates nearby
- Their presence indicates active drug trade in the area

# Street Gang Enforcer

**Type:** Urban street gang muscle / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Street Gang Enforcers** are the heavy muscle of street gangs, responsible for enforcing discipline, collecting debts, and eliminating threats. Inspired by the violent enforcers of the Death Wish series, these criminals are more dangerous and experienced than regular gang members, sent to deal with problems that require extreme violence. They're the street-level equivalent of organized crime enforcers.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 7  
**STAMINA:** 10  
**HABITAT:** Urban streets, gang territories, and wherever violence is needed  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3 (1d3)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Street Gang Enforcers are:

- **Violent** - Specialized in extreme violence and intimidation
- **Experienced** - More experienced than regular gang members
- **Loyal** - Devoted to their gang and its leadership
- **Ruthless** - Willing to commit extreme acts without hesitation
- **Intimidating** - Physically imposing and terrifying presence
- **Dedicated** - Will eliminate threats to the gang

## Equipment

Street Gang Enforcers typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Service pistols (Browning Hi-Power, M1911) or heavy revolvers
- **SMGs** - Light SMGs (Uzi) or machine pistols (MAC-10) for close-quarters combat
- **Shotguns** - Sawed-off shotguns for intimidation and close-range firepower
- **Melee weapons** - Baseball bats, chains, or specialized weapons
- **Stolen vehicles** - For transportation and quick escapes
- **Gang colors** - Clothing or symbols identifying their affiliation

## Abilities

### Intimidating Presence

Street Gang Enforcers are terrifying. Opponents must make a Willpower or LUCK test (GM's choice) or suffer -1 to SKILL for the first round of combat when facing an enforcer.

### Brutal Violence

Street Gang Enforcers are experts at violence. They gain +1 to damage on all attacks, representing their skill at inflicting harm.

### Unstoppable

Street Gang Enforcers push through pain and injury. They do not suffer penalties to SKILL when reduced to half STAMINA or less, and gain +1 to STAMINA for the purpose of determining when they fall unconscious.

### Enforcer's Duty

When sent to eliminate a specific target or enforce discipline, enforcers gain +1 to SKILL and will not stop until the target is eliminated or they are killed.

## In Play

Street Gang Enforcers serve as:

- **Elite threats** - More dangerous than regular gang members
- **Elimination** - Sent to eliminate specific targets or threats
- **Discipline** - Enforcing gang rules and punishing violations
- **Intimidation** - Their presence alone can end conflicts
- **Boss encounters** - Often appear as leaders or elite enemies

### Game Considerations

- Enforcers are elite street-level threats
- They often appear alone or in very small groups
- They may be sent after specific targets
- Their brutality makes them feared even by other criminals
- They often have more experience and better equipment than regular members
- They represent the escalation of street gang violence

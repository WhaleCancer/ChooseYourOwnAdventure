# Street Gang Lookout

**Type:** Urban street gang scout / Minion  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Street Gang Lookouts** are scouts and information gatherers for street gangs, watching for police, rivals, and opportunities. Inspired by the lookouts and scouts of the Death Wish series, these criminals serve as the eyes and ears of the gang, providing early warning and intelligence. They're less combat-focused but more alert and mobile than regular members.

## Statistics

**SKILL:** 6  
**STAMINA:** 7  
**HABITAT:** Street corners, rooftops, and strategic observation points  
**NUMBER ENCOUNTERED:** 1-3 (1d3)  
**TYPE:** Human  
**REACTION:** Hostile-Unfriendly  
**INTELLIGENCE:** Average

## Characteristics

Street Gang Lookouts are:

- **Alert** - Constantly watching for threats and opportunities
- **Mobile** - Quick to move and escape
- **Observant** - Skilled at gathering information
- **Loyal** - Devoted to their gang and its operations
- **Cautious** - Prefer to observe and report rather than fight
- **Resourceful** - Know the streets and can find information

## Equipment

Street Gang Lookouts typically carry:

- **Handguns** - Compact pistols or revolvers for personal protection
- **Melee weapons** - Knives or small weapons
- **Communication devices** - Radios, pagers, or other devices for reporting
- **Binoculars** - For observation (if available)
- **Stolen vehicles** - For quick movement and escape
- **Gang colors** - Clothing or symbols identifying their affiliation

## Abilities

### Alert Watch

Street Gang Lookouts are constantly alert. They gain +2 to SKILL for awareness tests and detecting ambushes, and may act first in surprise situations.

### Early Warning

Street Gang Lookouts can alert other gang members. If a lookout spots a threat, they can alert other gang members within 1d4+1 rounds, potentially bringing reinforcements.

### Street Knowledge

Street Gang Lookouts have extensive knowledge of their territory. They know escape routes, hiding places, and can provide information about local activities and threats.

### Quick Escape

Street Gang Lookouts are skilled at escaping. When attempting to flee, they gain +1 to SKILL for movement and evasion actions, and may use their knowledge of the area to escape.

### Information Network

Street Gang Lookouts are connected to information networks. They may know about police activity, rival gang movements, or other criminal activities in their territory.

## In Play

Street Gang Lookouts serve as:

- **Scouts** - Watching for threats and opportunities
- **Early warning** - Alerting other gang members to danger
- **Information sources** - Knowledge of local activities and threats
- **Combat encounters** - Less dangerous individually but can call reinforcements
- **Tactical elements** - Providing intelligence and coordination

### Game Considerations

- Lookouts are often found in strategic observation points
- They're less combat-effective but more alert and mobile
- Their ability to call reinforcements makes them a significant threat
- They may provide valuable information if captured or intimidated
- They often work in pairs or small groups
- Their knowledge of the area makes them useful for information gathering

## Description

Recon armor integrated with a jump pack, allowing the wearer to fly across the battlefield. Provides good protection while maintaining mobility. The jump pack enables rapid repositioning and can be used to escape dangerous situations or flank enemies. Requires Royalty DLC and spacer technology.

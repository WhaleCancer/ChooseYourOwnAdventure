## Statistics

- **Original Rimworld Blunt Protection:** ~45%
- **Original Rimworld Heat Protection:** ~120% (heat-hardened)
- **Coverage:** Full body
- **Tech Level:** Spacer
- **DLC:** Royalty
- **Special:** Heat-hardened, integrated flame launcher

## Description

Heat-hardened heavy armor with an integrated flame launcher. Provides excellent protection, especially against heat damage due to special heat-resistant materials. The integrated flame launcher allows the wearer to project fire attacks without using hands. Very effective in environments with extreme heat or when facing fire-based threats. Requires Royalty DLC and spacer technology.

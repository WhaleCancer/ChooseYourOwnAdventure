## Description

A long robe with golden chestplate, worthy of a king. Extremely beautiful.

Based on royal robes and ceremonial garments worn by medieval kings and high-ranking nobles. These ornate robes featured rich fabrics, often silk or velvet, decorated with gold thread, jewels, and elaborate embroidery. The golden chestplate or brooch represented royal authority and wealth. King's robes were ceremonial garments worn for important occasions, coronations, and court functions, displaying the monarch's power and legitimacy. The combination of luxurious fabrics and precious metals created garments that were both beautiful and symbolic of absolute authority in medieval hierarchy.

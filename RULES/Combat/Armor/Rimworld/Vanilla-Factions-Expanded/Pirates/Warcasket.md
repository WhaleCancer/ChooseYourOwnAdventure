## Description

Customizable industrial-tier combat suit used by pirate factions. Provides excellent protection and integrates weapons directly into the armor system. Requires bionics to use effectively. The wearer is effectively a cyborg inside the warcasket. Highly customizable with various weapon and armor module options. Used by elite pirate units and mercenaries.

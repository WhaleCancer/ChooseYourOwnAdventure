## Armor Skill Requirement

Total 7 (Armour Special Skill plus SKILL characteristic)

## Notes

Takes up two slots in the Hero's inventory. Protection is added to armor worn. Only one armour roll is made whether a shield is used or not.

## Description

Also made of wood and leather, and usually have a loop for the forearm and a handle for the hand. They can be kite shaped, teardrop shaped, round or square.

## Armor Skill Requirement

Total 9 (Armour Special Skill plus SKILL characteristic)

## Description

A single shaped piece of metal covering the front of the chest and abdomen, and a matching backplate. Providing excellent protection for the areas covered, a breastplate is however a heavy piece of armour.

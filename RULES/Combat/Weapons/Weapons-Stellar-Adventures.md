# Weapons - Stellar Adventures

## Weapons and Armour

**Source:** CB77011 - Stellar Adventures, Page 31 [Page 0031](../../CB77011_stellar-adventures_Pages/page_0031.png)

Within Stellar Adventures there is a wide range of science fiction weapons available from knives to fusion rifles, and these are all handled in a similar way. Exactly which weapons are available is something decided by the Director.

Each weapon has a damage track which converts the D6 damage roll into STAMINA damage. The damage track also has a '7+' column for modifiers. If for any reason the damage roll is below 1, the '1' column is used.

For example, Gorgyr throws a knife at an alien bounty hunter and wins the combat round. Gorgyr's player rolls a 4 on the damage roll and so the knife inflicts 2 points of STAMINA damage.

Players should copy the damage tracks for their weapons onto their character sheet.

## Weapon Characteristics

**Source:** CB77011 - Stellar Adventures, Page 31 [Page 0031](../../CB77011_stellar-adventures_Pages/page_0031.png)

In addition to the damage track, each weapon also has the following characteristics:

**Ammo:** This is the number of shots the weapon can fire before it needs reloading. Reloading takes a full round.

**Ammo/slot:** This is how many ammo refills can be carried per equipment slot. Note that the more damaging weapons require more space for ammo. For example a shotgun shell is larger than a bullet, and so fewer shotgun shells can be carried.

**Range:** Range is an abstract category which defines the usual maximum range of the weapon. The categories are Very Short, Short, Medium, Long, Very Long.

**Special:** Some weapons have a 'Special' column with unique rules, and these are explained below.

## Option: Unlimited Ammo

**Source:** CB77011 - Stellar Adventures, Page 31 [Page 0031](../../CB77011_stellar-adventures_Pages/page_0031.png)

For some settings the GM may decide that weapons have unlimited ammo. This makes weapons more powerful, so the GM should consider this carefully before allowing this option.

## Melee Weapon Statistics

**Source:** CB77011 - Stellar Adventures, Page 34 [Page 0034](../../CB77011_stellar-adventures_Pages/page_0034.png)

Some higher technology melee weapons have an ammo rating, and an ammo/slot. These weapons require power, and use one 'shot' if the attack deals damage. If the wielder loses the combat round, no ammo is used. If a weapon runs out of ammo, it is treated as a club until reloaded (1 round).

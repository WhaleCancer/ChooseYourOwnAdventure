## Description

A monosword bonded with an AI persona, granting it unique abilities and bonuses. More powerful than a standard monosword, with special traits that vary based on the persona. Requires Royalty DLC. The persona can provide various bonuses such as increased damage, psychic sensitivity, or other special effects.

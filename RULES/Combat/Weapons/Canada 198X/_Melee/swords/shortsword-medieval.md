## Description

A versatile and compact blade designed for both thrusting and slashing. Its nimble design makes it effective in close-quarters combat, where quick strikes can overwhelm slower foes.

Based on arming swords and other one-handed medieval swords designed for use with shields. The shortsword was a versatile weapon that could be used for both cutting and thrusting, making it effective in various combat situations. Its compact size and balanced design allowed for quick, precise strikes while leaving the off-hand free for a shield or other equipment. Shortswords were standard equipment for medieval foot soldiers and knights when fighting in close quarters or when a longer weapon was impractical.

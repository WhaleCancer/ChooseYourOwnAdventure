## Description

A long wooden pole adorned with a flag bearing the heraldry of its bearer. While it inspires unity and bolsters morale as a rallying point for soldiers, the standard itself is poorly suited for combat, offering little in terms of offense or defense.

Based on medieval battle standards and banners carried into battle to identify units and serve as rallying points. These standards featured the heraldic colors, symbols, and devices of the lord or unit they represented. While primarily ceremonial and tactical tools rather than weapons, standards could be used as improvised polearms in emergencies.

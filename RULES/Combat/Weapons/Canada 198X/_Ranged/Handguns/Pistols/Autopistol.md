## Description

A compact semi-automatic pistol with a high rate of fire, capable of firing multiple shots quickly. While it doesn't hit as hard as a service revolver, it makes up for it with rapid fire capability and easy concealment, making it effective for close-range combat.

The most commonly encountered autopistols are the Walther PP and PPK. The PPK is the smaller, more concealable variant, while the PP has a slightly larger grip and capacity. Both are used by some law enforcement agencies and civilians who need a compact sidearm. However, they've also developed a problematic reputation among certain groups.

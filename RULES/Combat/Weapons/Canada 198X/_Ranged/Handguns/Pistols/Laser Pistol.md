## Combat Options

- **Energy Weapon:** Uses focused light beams instead of projectiles, providing instant hit capability
- **Easy Concealment:** Compact design grants a +2 bonus on [Sleight of Hand](../../../../../../../Skills/MENTAL/Thievery/Sleight-of-Hand.md) checks made to conceal this weapon on your body
- **Two-Handed Aim:** Gain a +1 bonus to your attack roll when aiming with two hands. You cannot move in the same round when using this option. See [Two-Handed Aim](../../../../Combat-Options/Two-Handed-Aim.md) for details.

## Description

An ultratech pistol capable of emitting a concentrated beam of light with enough power to deal considerable damage. The laser technology provides precision energy-based firepower with the reliability of advanced military weapons. The compact pistol version provides portability and concealability, while the larger [Laser Rifle](../../Rifles/Laser Rifle.md) offers superior range and damage for long-range engagements.

This futuristic weapon draws inspiration from science fiction media, including laser pistols from movies, television shows, and video games featuring advanced energy weaponry. The laser technology represents a futuristic evolution of pistol technology, using focused light beams instead of projectiles, providing instant hit capability and eliminating the need for traditional ammunition.

## Description

A classic western-style revolver used by frontier settlers. Reliable handgun with good damage and moderate range. Single-action revolver typical of the frontier era. Iconic weapon of the American West, favored by settlers and lawmen.

Based on the Colt Single Action Army (1873) (Peacemaker), the legendary "gun that won the West." Chambered in .45 Colt, this single-action revolver requires manually cocking the hammer for each shot. The classic Western revolver design, iconic and reliable.

## Description

A precision long-range weapon with high damage but slow rate of fire. Excellent accuracy at extreme ranges, making it ideal for taking out targets from a distance.

Based on sniper rifles available in the 1980s, such as the M40 (Remington 700-based, 1966), Winchester Model 70 (1936), or various precision bolt-action rifles used by military and law enforcement snipers. These rifles are optimized for extreme accuracy at long ranges, featuring heavy barrels, precision optics, and match-grade ammunition. Sniper rifles require extensive training and skill to use effectively, with the shooter needing to account for distance, wind, bullet drop, and target movement.

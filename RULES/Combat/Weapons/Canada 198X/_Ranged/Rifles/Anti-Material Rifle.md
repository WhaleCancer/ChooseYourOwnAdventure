## Description

A heavy-caliber rifle designed to engage vehicles, equipment, and hardened targets at long ranges. Significantly larger and heavier than standard [Bolt-Action Rifle](Bolt-Action Rifle.md)s or sniper rifles, these weapons require bipods or rests for stability due to the massive recoil of their heavy-caliber cartridges.

Based on anti-material rifles such as the Barrett M82 (adopted by the U.S. military in 1982) or McMillan TAC-50 (introduced 1980).

## Description

A crossbow is a type of ranged weapon in similar principle to a bow, consisting of a bow-like assembly mounted horizontally on a main frame, which is handheld in a similar fashion to the stock of a long gun.\n\nThis weapon attempt of early mankind can send a bolt even through strongest metal.

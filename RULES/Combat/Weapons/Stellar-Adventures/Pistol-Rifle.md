## Description

These weapons fire physical bullets down a rifled barrel. Pistols may have 6 bullets in a revolving barrel, or a linear magazine. Rifles use linear magazines. Pistols are one handed, rifles two handed. Both fire one bullet per trigger pull, and both make a loud report.

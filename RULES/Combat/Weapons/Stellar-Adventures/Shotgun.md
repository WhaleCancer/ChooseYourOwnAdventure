## Statistics

- **Ammo:** 2
- **Ammo/Slot:** 10
- **Range:** Very Short
- **Special:** Targets several adjacent enemies


## Description

This weapon uses a cartridge of small pellets, and can allow an attack against up to three adjacent targets. The attack total is compared to all three targets, and any target it wins against takes damage.

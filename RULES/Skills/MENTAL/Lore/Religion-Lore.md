# Religion Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill provides an understanding of the beliefs and religious practices of the organised religions. It's typically possessed by Priests but is also useful for adventurers to gain insight into friendly and unfriendly religions.

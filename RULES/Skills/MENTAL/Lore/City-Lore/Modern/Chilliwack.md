# Chilliwack (1985)

**Settlement Type:** Town  
**Population:** ~40,000 (1985)  
**Origin:** Founded as agricultural settlement in Fraser Valley, grew as farming and military center.  
**Reason for Placement:** Fertile Fraser Valley farmland, access to Fraser River, proximity to Vancouver.  
**Current Occupation:** Farmers, military personnel (CFB Chilliwack), agricultural workers, commuters to Vancouver.

- **Proximity to Vancouver** - Commuter town, growing suburbanization
- **Mennonite Communities** - Significant presence in surrounding area
- **Religious Character** - Strong church presence

- **Growing Community** - Rapid growth in 1980s as Vancouver expands

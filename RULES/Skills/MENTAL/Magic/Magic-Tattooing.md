# Magic-Tattooing

**Source:** Heroes Companion (Chapter 1)

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

This Special Skill is restricted to Director-controlled NPCs. It allows tattooists to ink designs for magical powers. It is a rare art and commands high prices. See Chapter 2 for available tattoos.

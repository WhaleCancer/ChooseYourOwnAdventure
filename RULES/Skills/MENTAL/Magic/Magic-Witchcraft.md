# Magic-Witchcraft

**Source:** CB77028 - Magic Companion  
**Pages:** 13-16

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

The tradition of witchcraft is seen by most on Titan as an evil and destructive form of magic, used only by Hags and other evil casters. However, a few Witches follow a different path and a very few end up as Heroes. This special skill is required to use Witchcraft (Chapter 5).

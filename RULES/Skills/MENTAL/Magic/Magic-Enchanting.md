# Magic-Enchanting

**Source:** Heroes Companion (Chapter 1)

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

Enchanters use natural and magical ingredients to create magical equipment, with Potions being the easiest. Temporary and permanent magical items can be created. This skill can be learned with Wizardry or Sorcery, but requires a workshop and extensive raw materials. See Chapter 2 for more details.

# Magic-Mask Magic

**Source:** Heroes Companion (Chapter 1)

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

Also known as Southern Mask Magic, this rare school uses specially created and enchanted Masks to impart specific powers to the wearer. It can be learned by Wizards or Sorcerers in addition to their other spellcasting abilities. See Chapter 2 for further details.

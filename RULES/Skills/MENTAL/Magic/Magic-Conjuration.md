# Magic-Conjuration

**Source:** Heroes Companion (Chapter 1)

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

A Magical style used by Conjurers to summon and bind creatures from other planes and to create artificial life forms. It is difficult, time-consuming, and expensive to pursue, with Conjurers mastering only a few rituals but becoming very powerful. This skill can be taken by spellcasters in addition to other Magical styles. See Chapter 2 for more information.

# Magic-Battle Magic

**Source:** Heroes Companion (Chapter 1)

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

This Special Skill is for experienced Magic practitioners, enabling spells to defeat enemy units. Battle Mages are rare and highly regarded, often serving kings or warlords. It can be learned in addition to Wizardry or Sorcery, but not by starting Heroes. See Chapter 2 for details.

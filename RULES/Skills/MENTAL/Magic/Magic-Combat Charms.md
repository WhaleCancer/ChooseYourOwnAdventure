# Magic-Combat Charms

**Source:** CB77028 - Magic Companion  
**Pages:** 13-16

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

Practitioners of Combat Charms (known in some places as Magi) are a type of Wizard with something more of a focus on casting spells in battle, even devising a specialist form of rapid spell called a Combat Charm. This special skill is required to use Combat Charms magic.

# Magic-Prophecy

**Source:** CB77028 - Magic Companion  
**Pages:** 13-16

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

This magical style is in some ways more of a natural magical gift than a magical discipline, involving momentary glimpses of the future and of alternate futures to supply the Prophet or Prophetess with apparent magical powers. This special skill is required to use Prophecy Magic (Chapter 5).

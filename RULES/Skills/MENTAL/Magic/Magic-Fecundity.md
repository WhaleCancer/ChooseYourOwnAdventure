# Magic-Fecundity

**Source:** CB77028 - Magic Companion  
**Pages:** 13-16

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

Strikingly similar to the Priests known as Druids, Grove Tenders have a very close bond with a small area of lush vegetation that they call home. Although their power is indeed more potent when they are within their home glade, that are able to take that power with them when they travel.

# Psionics

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This special skill is actually multiple special skills. Each specific Psionics power requires the purchase of a different skill. Players should consult both the Director and the Psionics chapter to determine which Psionics skills can be purchased.

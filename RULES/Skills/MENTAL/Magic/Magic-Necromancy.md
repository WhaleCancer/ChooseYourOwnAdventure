# Magic-Necromancy

**Source:** Heroes Companion (Chapter 1)

**Category:** Knowledge Skill (MENTAL)

Necromancy is an evil use of Magic to create undead or curse enemies. It damages the Necromancer's soul and prevents casting other Wizardry spells. See Chapter 2 for spells and rules.

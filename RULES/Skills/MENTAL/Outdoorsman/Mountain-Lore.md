# Mountain Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill allows a Character to understand and survive in mountainous and alpine environments, covering finding shelter, navigating treacherous terrain, and understanding mountain flora and fauna. It includes knowledge of weather patterns, avalanche risks, safe routes through passes, and how to identify edible plants and animals at high altitudes. It also covers understanding mountain-specific dangers like altitude sickness, rockfalls, and sudden weather changes.

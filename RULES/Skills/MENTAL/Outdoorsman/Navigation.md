# Navigation

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

## Advanced Fighting Fantasy (Heroes Companion, Page 10) [Page 0010](../../../../AFF005_Heroes_Companion_Pages/page_0010.png)

A skill for efficient travel, applicable to sea or overland journeys. Good maps or navigation tools can provide a bonus, and a good navigator is essential for long-distance travel.

## Stellar Adventures (Planetary Navigation) (CB77011, Page 11) [Page 0011](../../../../CB77011_stellar-adventures_Pages/page_0011.png)

This skill enables the Hero to find their bearings and accurately judge their position on any world if they have access to star charts and longitude/latitude data. This is helpful on newly-discovered alien worlds that have not yet been properly mapped or have satellites to aid navigation. It's always nice to know where you are.

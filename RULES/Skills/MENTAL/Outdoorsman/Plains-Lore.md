# Plains Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill allows a Character to understand and survive in plains, grasslands, and prairie environments, covering finding food, water, and shelter in open terrain. It includes knowledge of grassland flora and fauna, understanding how to navigate featureless terrain, locating water sources, and identifying edible plants and animals. It also covers understanding the behavior of herd animals, finding natural shelter in open landscapes, and knowledge of seasonal patterns in grassland environments.

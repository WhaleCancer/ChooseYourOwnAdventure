# Underground Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill enables a Character to understand and survive in underground environments including caves, dungeons and caverns. While a light source or "Dark Seeing talent" is still needed, this skill provides the ability to forage for mushrooms, insects and other food sources, find food, and navigate their way around. It also helps a Character avoid natural rockfalls and other dangers.

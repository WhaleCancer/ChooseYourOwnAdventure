# Desert Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill allows a Character to understand and survive in desert environments, covering finding water sources, shelter from the sun, and understanding desert flora and fauna. It includes knowledge of how to navigate by landmarks, avoid sandstorms, identify mirages, and locate oases. It also covers understanding desert-adapted animals and plants, including which are safe to eat or use for resources.

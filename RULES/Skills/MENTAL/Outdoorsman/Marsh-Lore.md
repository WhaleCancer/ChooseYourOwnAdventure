# Marsh Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill allows a Character to understand and survive in marsh, swamp, and wetland environments, covering finding safe paths through treacherous terrain, locating dry ground, and understanding wetland flora and fauna. It includes knowledge of quicksand, dangerous creatures, edible plants, and how to navigate through waterlogged areas. It also covers understanding which areas are safe to cross, how to identify poisonous plants and animals, and methods for finding clean water in wetland environments.

# Jungle Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill allows a Character to understand and survive in jungle and tropical rainforest environments, covering finding food, shelter, and water in dense vegetation. It includes knowledge of jungle flora and fauna, understanding how to navigate through dense undergrowth, identifying edible and medicinal plants, and avoiding dangerous creatures. It also covers understanding tropical weather patterns, finding safe paths through the jungle, and knowledge of the unique challenges of humid, overgrown environments including disease-carrying insects and poisonous plants.

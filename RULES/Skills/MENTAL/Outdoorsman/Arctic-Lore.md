# Arctic Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill allows a Character to understand and survive in arctic, tundra, and frozen environments, covering finding shelter from extreme cold, locating food sources, and understanding arctic flora and fauna. It includes knowledge of how to avoid frostbite and hypothermia, identifying safe ice for travel, understanding weather patterns, and finding edible plants and animals in frozen landscapes. It also covers understanding the unique challenges of polar environments, including limited daylight, blizzards, and navigating featureless snow-covered terrain.

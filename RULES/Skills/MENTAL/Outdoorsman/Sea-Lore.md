# Sea Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill gives a Character knowledge of the sea and coast. It covers tides, currents, trade routes, and dangerous areas, which will be well known locally and easily acquired for distant seas. It also allows a Character to sail a ship, or row a boat, making it essential for sailors, merchants, and pirates.

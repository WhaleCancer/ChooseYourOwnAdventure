# Forest Lore

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill allows a Character to understand and be at home in woodland and temperate forest environments, covering finding food, shelter, water, and understanding flora. It also includes knowledge of fauna and even a knowledge of healing herbs and other resources that may be found there. For tropical rainforests and dense jungle environments, see [[RULES/Special-Skills/MENTAL/Lore/Jungle-Lore|Jungle Lore]].

**Related Terrain:** [[RULES/Wilderness/terrain/Forest|Forest]]

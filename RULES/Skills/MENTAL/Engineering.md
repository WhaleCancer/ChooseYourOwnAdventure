# Engineering

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

## Stellar Adventures (Page 10)

Every machine needs to be looked after, and it's often said that a well-oiled engine runs best. Engineers are an important part of society as they are on hand to replace that blown energy converter, bang that hull plating back into shape or jury-rig that plasma coupling. Giving the Hero the ability to work on any vehicle be it land, sea, air or space, the Engineering skill keeps the party flying.

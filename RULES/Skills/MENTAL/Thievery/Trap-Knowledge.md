# Trap Knowledge

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill is used for identifying and deactivating all sorts of traps, including bear traps, pit traps, poisoned needles, and falling blocks. It represents knowledge of trap mechanisms and how to safely disarm them.

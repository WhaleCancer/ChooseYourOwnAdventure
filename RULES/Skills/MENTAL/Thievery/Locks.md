# Locks

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill is used for examining, assessing, and opening locks without a key. It represents knowledge of lock mechanisms and how to bypass them, from simple mechanical locks to advanced security systems.

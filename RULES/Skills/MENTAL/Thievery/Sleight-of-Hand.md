# Sleight of Hand

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill covers picking pockets, stealing small items, secreting items, and other legerdemain. It is popular with thieves, gamblers, and entertainers, representing manual dexterity and misdirection.

# Piloting

**Characteristic:** PHYSICAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

The Piloting special skill represents general vehicle operation and control, including both mechanical vehicles and mounts. This skill provides a foundation for operating various types of vehicles and mounts, though specialized training in specific vehicle types is recommended for optimal performance. Heroes with this skill have a basic understanding of vehicle controls, navigation, and handling across different environments.

## Related Skills

This general skill relates to the following specialized piloting skills:
- **[[Piloting/Pilot-Air]]** - Operating aircraft, helicopters, and atmospheric vehicles
- **[[Piloting/Pilot-Ground]]** - Driving ground vehicles including cars, trucks, motorcycles, and tracked vehicles
- **[[Piloting/Pilot-Water]]** - Piloting watercraft including boats, ships, and submarines
- **[[Piloting/Pilot-Space]]** - Operating spacecraft, fighters, freighters, and starships
- **[[Piloting/Mounted-Combat]]** - Fighting effectively while mounted on animals or other mounts

If rules reference any of these specialized skills but your campaign uses the general Piloting skill instead, simply substitute Piloting for the specialized skill reference.

# Melee Weapons

**Characteristic:** PHYSICAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

## Stellar Adventures (CB77011, Page 11) [Page 0011](../../../../CB77011_stellar-adventures_Pages/page_0011.png)

Fists and blasters are fine, but sometimes you just want to save your ammo or the skin of your knuckles and just bash someone over the head. The Melee special skill gives Heroes a better chance of landing a blow with an iron pipe, a sword or a laserblade.

## Related Skills

This general skill relates to the following specialized melee weapon skills:
- **[[Melee Weapons/Axes]]** - Using axes and hatchets
- **[[Melee Weapons/Clubs]]** - Clubs, maces, and hammers
- **[[Melee Weapons/Polearms]]** - Spears, halberds, and pole weapons
- **[[Melee Weapons/Staves]]** - Staff weapons
- **[[Melee Weapons/Swords]]** - Swords and bladed melee weapons

If rules reference any of these specialized skills but your campaign uses the general Melee-Weapons skill instead, simply substitute Melee-Weapons for the specialized skill reference.

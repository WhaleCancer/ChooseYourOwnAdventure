# Pilot - Air

**Characteristic:** PHYSICAL

**Tech Levels:** Low, Medium, High

Vehicles that fly, but cannot break orbit and escape a planet's atmosphere, require a special skill to pilot. The Pilot - Air skill gives the Hero the ability to fly both fixed- and rotary-wing aircraft as well as aircars and the like, and even pull off death-defying stunts or dodge incoming fire.

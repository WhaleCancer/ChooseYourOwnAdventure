# Pilot - Space

**Characteristic:** PHYSICAL

**Tech Levels:** Medium, High

This is the skill most budding space jocks want. What could be better than blasting through space at a million miles an hour, atop an big metal bullet packed propelled by enough energy to destroy a city? Whether it's a small one-man fighter, a freighter with a small crew or a huge star-destroying warship, the Pilot - Space skill will get you where you need to go.

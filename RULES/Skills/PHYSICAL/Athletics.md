# Athletics

**Characteristic:** PHYSICAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

The Athletics special skill represents general physical prowess and athletic ability. This skill provides a foundation for various physical activities, though specialized training in specific athletic disciplines is recommended for optimal performance. Heroes with this skill have a basic understanding of physical movement, coordination, and athletic techniques across different environments.

## Related Skills

This general skill relates to the following specialized athletic skills:
- **[[Athletics/Climb]]** - Climbing cliff faces, ropes, trees, and walls
- **[[Athletics/Jump]]** - Jumping over chasms, reaching heights, and reducing falling damage
- **[[Athletics/Ride]]** - Controlling and staying on mounts
- **[[Athletics/Run]]** - Increasing running pace and speed
- **[[Athletics/Strength]]** - Physical strength for lifting, breaking, and hauling
- **[[Athletics/Swim]]** - Swimming and avoiding drowning

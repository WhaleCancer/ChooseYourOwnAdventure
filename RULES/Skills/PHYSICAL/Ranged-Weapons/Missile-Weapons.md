# Missile Weapons

**Characteristic:** PHYSICAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

The Missile Weapons special skill represents general proficiency with non-firearm ranged weapons. This skill provides a foundation for operating various missile weapon types, though specialized training in specific weapon categories is recommended for optimal performance. Heroes with this skill have a basic understanding of aiming, projectile mechanics, and combat techniques across different missile weapon systems.

## Related Skills

This general skill relates to the following specialized missile weapon skills:
- **[[Missile Weapons/Bows]]** - Bows including longbows, shortbows, and crossbows
- **[[Missile Weapons/Thrown]]** - Thrown weapons including knives, axes, spears, rocks, grenades, and other thrown projectiles

If rules reference any of these specialized skills but your campaign uses the general Missile Weapons skill instead, simply substitute Missile Weapons for the specialized skill reference.

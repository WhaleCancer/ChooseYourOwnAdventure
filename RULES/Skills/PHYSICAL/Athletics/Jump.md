# Jump

**Characteristic:** PHYSICAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

This skill is used for horizontal jumps (over chasms), vertical jumps (reaching things), and reducing falling damage. It represents the ability to leap distances and heights effectively.

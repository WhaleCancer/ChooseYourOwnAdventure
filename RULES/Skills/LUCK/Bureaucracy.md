# Bureaucracy

**Characteristic:** LUCK

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

The galaxy may be full of high technology, aliens, robots and starships, but there is still paperwork to be done and forms to be filled out. Landing at a secure starport, obtaining spare parts, bypassing space station security or any other official function can all make use of this special skill.

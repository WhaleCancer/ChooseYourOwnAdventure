# Trade Knowledge

**Characteristic:** MENTAL

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

A special skill for a Hero with the Bargain skill, making them a talented negotiator. Buying cheap, selling high. Effectiveness is nullified if the cargo hold is empty or landing on an unsuitable planet (e.g., air conditioners on a frozen planet). Can be used to work out profitable trade routes and identify the best time to sell goods.

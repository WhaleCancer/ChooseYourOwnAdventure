# Etiquette

**Characteristic:** LUCK

**Tech Levels:** Basic, Low, Medium, High

Social skills and knowing just how to behave are probably even more important in the future than in the past. Exploration ships will need to follow local customs, traders will need to keep the local merchants happy, and anyone in official service will need to keep their superiors happy!

# Fleet Footed

**Source:** Core rulebook (AF2e)

**Cost:** 2 Talent Points

The Character moves considerably faster than others of their race, able to increase speed in combat, when escaping, or during long-distance travel. The Character moves three meters in combat (instead of the usual two), adds an additional +4 to any escaping roll if on foot, and can move 50% further in a day than normal for their race.

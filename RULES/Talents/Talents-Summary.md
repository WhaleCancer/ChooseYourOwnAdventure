## Notes

- **Cost:** Most talents have a fixed cost in Talent Points. Some talents (Magical Resistance, Templar) have variable costs that should be discussed with the Director.
- **Source:** Talents come from various sourcebooks:
  - **Core rulebook (AF2e):** The main Advanced Fighting Fantasy 2nd Edition rulebook
  - **Heroes Companion (Chapter 1):** Additional talents from the Heroes Companion supplement
  - **Combat Companion:** Combat-focused talents from the Combat Companion supplement
  - **CB77028 - Magic Companion:** Magic-related talents from the Magic Companion supplement

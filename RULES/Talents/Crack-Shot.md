# Crack Shot

**Source:** Core rulebook (AF2e)

**Cost:** 4 Talent Points

The Character has an unerring eye with missile weapons, gaining a +1 bonus to all damage rolls made with missile weapons (e.g., bows, crossbows, slings, firearms, heavy weapons). This Talent does not apply to vehicle or ship mounted weapons (for a Talent that does, see [[RULES/Talents/Dogfighter|Dogfighter]]).

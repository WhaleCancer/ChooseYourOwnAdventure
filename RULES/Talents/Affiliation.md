# Affiliation

**Source:** Core rulebook (AF2e)

**Cost:** 2 Talent Points

This Talent means that the Character is affiliated with a particular organisation, and almost certainly has official status within it. The Character may be an agent or other employee or could be an honoured member or associate. The Character may, at appropriate times and with appropriate contact, Test their Luck to gain special assistance. The downside to this Talent is that sworn enemies of the organisation also see the Character as an enemy!

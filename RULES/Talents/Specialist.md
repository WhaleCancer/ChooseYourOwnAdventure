# Specialist

**Source:** Combat Companion

**Cost:** 4 Talent Points

This Character is highly skilled with a particular class of weapons (defined by a single weapon Special Skill), gaining a +1 bonus to Attack Total with those weapons. They cannot learn other weapon Special Skills but can still use other weapons with only their base PHYSICAL. If acquired after character creation, existing weapon Special Skills are retained but cannot be improved. This Talent represents the Character's complete dedication to mastering one weapon type.

# Dual Caster

**Source:** CB77028 - Magic Companion (Pages 13-16)

**Cost:** 4 Talent Points

Although it is normally impossible to cast more than one spell in a single round, a Hero with this Talent is able to channel their energies with care, and are able to produce more than one effect in a single round.

A Hero with this Talent is able to cast one Minor Magic spell and one other spell from another tradition in the same round without penalty. All other normal rules apply.

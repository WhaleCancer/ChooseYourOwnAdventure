# Sniper

**Source:** Combat Companion

**Cost:** 4 Talent Points

This Character can aim for up to three rounds with a bow, crossbow, or blowpipe (excluding slings or thrown weapons), gaining a +1 bonus to Attack Total and damage roll for each consecutive round. Concentration is broken if the target leaves sight or the sniper is attacked. This Talent represents the Character's ability to take careful, precise shots.

# Hunter

**Source:** Combat Companion

**Cost:** 4 Talent Points

This Character chooses a specific enemy type (e.g., Greys, Cultists, Slashers) against which they gain a +1 bonus to Attack Total and damage rolls due to loathing and special knowledge. The Director may allow these bonuses for similar enemy types. This Talent represents the Character's focused training and hatred for a particular threat.

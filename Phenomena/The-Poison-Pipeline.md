# The Poison Pipeline

**Type:** Phenomenon / Conspiracy structure  
**Era:** Ongoing since the 1970s

## Overview

Beneath the surface of the mid-1980s Pacific Northwest, an invisible war is waged with chemicals, contracts, and "improvements" to everyday life. Shadowy government agencies, big agribusiness, and carefully steered scientific societies are complicit—wittingly or not—in making the population more vulnerable to [[Creatures/Supernatural/Elohim|Archon]] influence and emotional harvesting.

## How It Works

### 1. The Additive Network

- Fluoridated water, "fortified" cereals, antipsychotic-laced vaccine lots, "experimental" pesticides and food preservatives—all distributed via well-meaning or secret government programs "for public health."
- In reality, these contain subtle Archon-engineered psychoactive agents. The effect is cumulative: increased fatigue, reduced willpower, spiritual malaise, patterns of anxiety and passivity, and—most importantly—a suppressed ability to resist psychic invasion, abduction, or emotional harvesting. These chemicals work in conjunction with the [[Phenomena/Frequency-Fences|Frequency Fence]] to make humans more vulnerable to the Archons' emotional vampirism.
- Scientific papers are forged or "adjusted" by deep-cover contacts; opposition is marginalized as cranks or lunatics.

### 2. The Delivery Systems

- **Municipal water plants** (often a single, "pilot" facility in a rural or First Nations area) are targeted for early "fluoridation" or unpublicized "improvements."
- **Major food processing plants** roll out "fortified" breads, milk, and vitamin-laced snacks with select chemical enhancements.
- **Vaccination programs** run through schools, logging camps, and remote reserves—ostensibly to control outbreaks, actually trialing new compounds.
- The **supply chain** is controlled by a web of shell companies, government contracts, and private paramilitary security.

### 3. Key Conspirators & Useful Idiots

- **Industry Executives:** Some are true believers, members of secret societies, or are compelled due to Archon psychic pressure.
- **Government Agencies:** Health Canada, select RCMP divisions, and Defense Research have "black project" offshoots funnelling people, resources, and cover stories.
- **Pharmaceutical PR Firms:** These groups quash bad press, discredit whistleblowers, and foster "citizen trust" in public water and mass medicine.
- **Front Companies:** Often run by ex-military, these ghost firms handle shipping, plant maintenance, and "sensitive" security.

## Signs & Effects

### Population-Level

- Outbreaks of "grey malaise": entire towns lose interest in community, spiritual activities, and resistance. This is the result of both chemical suppression and emotional energy being harvested by the Frequency Fence.
- Abductions spike after new additives are introduced, as weakened humans are easier targets.
- Test subjects exhibit telltale marks: dulled eyes, "static" episodes (frozen, absent stares), recurring dreams of underwater factories. These are signs of both chemical poisoning and emotional energy depletion from Archon harvesting.

### Individual Level

- Early warning: nosebleeds, metallic taste, persistent dreams of being watched.
- Resistance: some with rare genetics or traditional diets (foraging, well water, traditional foodways) seem immune, marked by vivid dreams of "fire barriers" or spirit guides.

### Cover-Ups

- Local dissenters are institutionalized, disappear, or have their reputations destroyed ("paranoid breakdown," "chemical dependency," "ran off with the hippies").
- Medical symptoms are blamed on "seasonal affective disorder," "lead pipes," or "flu season stress."

## Factions & Resistance

- **The Loyal Opposition:** Rogue scientists, water engineers, and activist doctors who know something is wrong, but can't trust any official channels.
- **Cult of Purity:** Fringe "clean food" and "living water" cults, some infiltrated by Archon spies or deranged by chemical withdrawal, yet some genuinely able to resist possession and emotional harvesting through ritualized diets and secret purges.
- **Spiritual Protectors:** [[Factions/Circle-of-Watchers|First Nations elders and healers]], who use traditional knowledge (and perhaps ancient spiritual "detox" rituals) to counter Archon vibration dampening and emotional vampirism. Resistance is concentrated among those who have kept their old foodways and water sources, maintaining stronger spiritual and emotional defenses.

## Hooks & Encounters

1. **The Whistleblower** A nervous water plant technician arranges to meet the PCs, clutching vials of a strange crystalline additive and classified shipment logs. He's terrified: his coworkers have started repeating the same phrases, and one was found dead in a supply closet with blue-tinged blood.

2. **Company Town on Edge** A logging town suddenly erupts in violent incidents—disassociated people attacking neighbors or wandering into the woods. Outbreak traces to a new bread recipe distributed by the local bakery, which only bakes with "enriched flour" from a mysterious new supplier.

3. **Factory Raid** Resistance cultists (an alliance of hippie back-to-the-landers and First Nations activists) plan a break-in at a government-contracted food additive factory. PCs can join or intercept—inside, they discover a realm of experimental laboratories, corporate heavies, vats bubbling with unearthly substances, and data linking the facility to government "Project Starlit."

4. **Data Trail** A stack of leaked documents reveals a web of shell companies. The PCs can trace shipping records showing that shipments of "purified" chemicals go first to small, out-of-the-way communities, showing a pattern—targets for Elohim attention, with abduction and madness rates much higher than the rest of Canada.

## Flavors and Themes

- **Paranoia is justified:** The conspiracy goes all the way up and encompasses both high-level planners and countless useful idiots.
- **Mundane horror:** Ordinary substances—additives, food, water—are tainted; security and trust become impossible.
- **Alien logic:** Victims sometimes start speaking in unknown languages, perform odd ritual gestures unconsciously, or succumb to sudden, inexplicable psychic events. These are often signs of emotional energy being harvested or direct Archon influence.
- **Hope survives:** Localized resistance—through spiritual, dietary, or whistleblower action—can block, counter, or even reverse the effects on small scales, but the battle is uphill and the cost is high.

## Adventure/Arc Seeds

- **Root Out the Source:** Track the source of a new spiritual "malaise" to a suspect food additive company, unflinching police protection, and a string of "missing persons" cases.
- **Protect the Clean:** Safeguard a resistant community with unpolluted well water and traditionally grown food—now targeted for abduction and sabotage by agents and Greys.
- **Expose the Pipeline:** Secure hard evidence (chemical samples, coded memos, survivor testimony) and get it to a national journalist before the conspiracy silences everyone involved.

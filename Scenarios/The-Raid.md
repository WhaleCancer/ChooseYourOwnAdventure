# Scenario: The Raid

**Type:** Tactical scenario / Standoff  
**Location:** Alien cult compound  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

After a week or so of negotiations, the RCMP decides to send in the SERT (Special Emergency Response Team) to raid an alien cult compound. This is a tactical scenario involving multiple teams, vehicles, and the sudden appearance of a UFO during the operation.

## Setup

- Standoff at an alien cult compound
- After a week or so of negotiations, the RCMP decides to send in the SERT
- You use some kind of vehicle to ram over the fences, then one team is assigned to each building on the compound
- Another team will cover the south door
- Everyone rolls a stealth check up to the door

### The Breacher (Entry Specialists)

- Shotgun / Breaching Rounds followed by kicking the door in

- C1 Rifle
- Light Ballistic Armor

### The Shield (Shield Specialist) - 1-2 per team

- Ballistic Shield
- Pistol
- Baton

### The Sniper

- Aimed shots
- Decision making
- 1-on-1 with a man in black

## Complications

- UFO appears during the raid
- Cult members may be beamed up
- Men in Black may be present
- Cultists may be armed and dangerous
- Evidence may need to be secured before the UFO destroys it

## Objectives

- Secure the compound
- Rescue any hostages
- Capture or neutralize cult leaders
- Secure evidence
- Deal with the UFO threat
- Engage Men in Black if present

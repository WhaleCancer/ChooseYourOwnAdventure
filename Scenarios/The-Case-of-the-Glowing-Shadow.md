# The Case of the Glowing Shadow

**Type:** Investigation scenario  
**Location:** Vancouver, British Columbia  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

Investigation of the death of an [[Creatures/Supernatural/Elohim|Elohim]] (Outsider). The crime scene shows the distinctive shadow-like after-image that Outsiders leave when destroyed, and evidence suggests the killing was by [[Factions/The-Hand|The Hand]].

## The Crime Scene

### The Shadow After-Image

> [!info] Outsider Death
> "Rather than leaving a physical body behind when destroyed, Outsiders explode into light and leave a shadow-like after-image on a nearby surface. This burn always seems brighter than the surrounding terrain, even in daylight."

The crime scene features:

- **Shadow-like after-image** that seems to burn brighter than the surrounding terrain, even when exposed to direct light
- **Bright light** - Eye witnesses report a bright light momentarily emitting from the site of the murder
- **No body** - No physical remains, only the shadow imprint
- **Energy signatures** - Residual psionic or dimensional energy

## Investigation Clues

### About Outsiders

- Outsiders are alien beings that come from a higher dimension
- They are mostly malevolent beings of light, but some have ascended to be neutral or even benevolent in disposition
- When destroyed, they explode into light and leave a shadow-like after-image
- The after-image always seems brighter than the surrounding terrain, even in daylight

### The Weapon

- **Tesla Cannon** - The weapon used to kill the Outsider
- **Russian writing** - The weapon has Russian writing, implicating foreign involvement
- **RCMP involvement** - The RCMP takes over the case due to the foreign weapon

### The Perpetrator

- **VPD Cop** - Players must canvas the homeless in the area, they will find out that it was a VPD (Vancouver Police Department) cop
- **Footage** - Security footage from inside the VPD station shows him stashing some kind of device
- **Another cop** - That device is picked up by another cop
- **Chase scenario** - That other cop will run and there are multiple scenarios where you have to apprehend him

## The Hand Connection

Evidence suggests The Hand was involved:

- **Organized operation** - The killing appears to be part of a coordinated effort
- **Advanced weaponry** - The Tesla Cannon suggests access to advanced technology
- **Police involvement** - VPD officers being involved suggests infiltration or corruption
- **Foreign connections** - Russian writing suggests international connections

## In Play

This scenario serves as:

- **Investigation** - Crime scene investigation of an unusual murder
- **Outsider encounter** - Direct evidence of Outsider existence and death
- **The Hand operation** - Evidence of The Hand's activities and capabilities
- **Police corruption** - VPD officers involved in conspiracy activities
- **International connections** - Russian involvement suggesting broader conspiracies

### Game Considerations

- The shadow after-image provides unique and memorable evidence
- The Outsider death reveals information about their nature
- The Hand connection adds conspiracy layers
- Police corruption creates moral complexity
- The Tesla Cannon and Russian connection suggest advanced technology and international conspiracies

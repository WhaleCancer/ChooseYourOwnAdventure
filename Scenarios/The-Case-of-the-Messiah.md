# The Case of the Messiah

**Type:** Investigation scenario  
**Location:** British Columbia  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

Investigation involving a UFO religion and cult, where the cult leader is an [Outsider](./Creatures/Supernatural/Outsiders/Outsiders-Overview.md).

## Key Elements

### UFO Religion

The case involves a religious movement centered around:

- **UFO beliefs** - Worship of or communication with alien entities
- **Cult structure** - Organized religious group with a charismatic leader
- **Outsider leader** - The cult leader is actually an Outsider
- **Religious practices** - Rituals, ceremonies, and beliefs centered on UFOs

### The Outsider Leader

The cult leader is an Outsider, which means:

- **Supernatural entity** - Not human, but an interdimensional being
- **Manipulation** - Using the cult for unknown purposes
- **Powers** - Possessing psionic or other supernatural abilities
- **Hidden nature** - Appearing human but being something else entirely

## Investigation Hooks

- **Missing persons** - Cult members disappearing
- **Strange rituals** - Unusual religious practices
- **Paranormal activity** - Unexplained phenomena around the cult
- **Outsider revelation** - Discovering the leader's true nature
- **Cult activities** - Illegal or dangerous activities

## In Play

This scenario serves as:

- **Cult investigation** - Cases involving religious movements
- **Outsider encounter** - Direct interaction with an Outsider
- **Religious themes** - Exploration of faith, manipulation, and belief
- **Paranormal activity** - Unexplained phenomena around the cult
- **Revelation** - Discovering the true nature of the cult leader

### Game Considerations

- The UFO religion angle connects to real-world UFO cults
- The Outsider leader adds cosmic horror elements
- The cult structure creates opportunities for infiltration
- Religious themes add moral complexity
- The revelation of the leader's true nature creates dramatic moments

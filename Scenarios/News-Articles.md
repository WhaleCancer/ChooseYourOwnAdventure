# News Articles

**Type:** Flavor text / Scenario hooks  
**Era:** 1985-1987  
**Overview:** Newspaper articles and media reports that can be used as flavor text, investigation hooks, or background information for scenarios.

## Overview

These news articles represent public-facing media coverage of events in the EPIC universe. They can be used as:

- **Investigation hooks** - Articles that lead to scenarios
- **Background information** - Context for ongoing campaigns
- **Flavor text** - Handouts for players
- **Timeline markers** - Events that shape the setting

## Articles

### Beam Me Up, Eh? (November 3, 1985)

**Source:** BC Times  
**Topic:** UFO Sightings Skyrocket Across British Columbia

A lighthearted article about increased UFO sightings across BC, written in a whimsical tone that contrasts with the serious nature of the phenomenon. Useful for showing how the public initially reacts to UFO activity before understanding the true threat.

### Canadian Government Revives Avro Arrow Program (October 15, 1967)

**Source:** Ottawa, ON  
**Topic:** Revival of Avro Arrow program following Shag Harbour incident

An article connecting the Shag Harbour UFO incident to the revival of the Avro Arrow program. Suggests government awareness of the alien threat and attempts to develop countermeasures.

### Controversial Mega-Mine Greenlit Amidst Uproar (April 12, 1984)

**Source:** Vancouver Sun  
**Topic:** Eagles Peak Mine approval

Details the controversial approval of the Eagles Peak Mine by the Social Credit government, with allegations of impropriety and environmental concerns. Connects to the Eagles Peak location and Archon/Grey activities.

### Eagles Peak Crisis Deepens (December 7, 1987)

**Source:** Vancouver Sun  
**Topic:** Eagles Peak Mine job losses and Vander Zalm government response

Follow-up article showing the Eagles Peak Mine's failure to deliver promised jobs due to automation. Shows Vander Zalm government attempting to shut down the project.

### Eagles Peak Disillusionment (September 18, 1987)

**Source:** Vancouver Sun  
**Topic:** Automation cuts jobs at Eagles Peak, minister resigns

Details the fallout from the Eagles Peak Mine's automation, including job losses and political consequences. Shows the human cost of Archon/Grey technological influence.

### Skyward Suspicion (March 12, 1986)

**Source:** BC Times  
**Topic:** Continued UFO sightings puzzle British Columbians

Article showing the shift from lighthearted curiosity to genuine concern about persistent UFO sightings. Shows government agencies beginning to take the phenomenon seriously.

### Vander Zalm Government Launches Special Commission to Probe UFO Sightings (September 5, 1986)

**Source:** BC Times  
**Topic:** Government commission to investigate UFOs

Details the Vander Zalm government's formation of a special commission to investigate UFO sightings, partnering with the RCMP. Shows official response to the phenomenon.

### Vander Zalm's Puzzling Policies Shake Up British Columbia (June 10, 1987)

**Source:** BC Times  
**Topic:** Vander Zalm's unconventional economic policies

Article about Vander Zalm's confusing but potentially effective economic policies. May hint at Archon influence or resistance efforts.

## In Play

These articles can be used as:

- **Player handouts** - Give players articles to read during investigations
- **Investigation leads** - Articles that point to locations or events
- **Timeline reference** - Show when events occurred
- **Public perception** - Demonstrate how the public views events
- **Cover-up elements** - Show how authorities explain away real events

### Game Considerations

- Articles may contain misinformation or cover-ups
- Public perception may differ from reality
- Articles can be red herrings or genuine leads
- Timing of articles can establish campaign chronology
- Tone can vary from serious to lighthearted depending on public awareness

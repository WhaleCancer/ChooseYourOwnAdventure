# The Case of the Americans

**Type:** Investigation scenario  
**Location:** British Columbia  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

Investigation involving political intrigue, alien conspiracy, and the intersection of U.S. and Canadian interests. The scenario involves the [Majestic-12](./Factions/Government/Majestic-12.md) organization and their activities in Canada.

## Key Elements

### Political Intrigue

The case involves:

- **U.S. Government** - Majestic-12 operations in Canada
- **Canadian Government** - M-EPIC response to U.S. activities
- **Cross-border operations** - Activities spanning both countries
- **Diplomatic tensions** - Conflicts between U.S. and Canadian agencies

### Alien Conspiracy

The case connects to:

- **Majestic-12** - U.S. organization investigating aliens
- **M-EPIC** - Canadian organization with similar mission
- **Alien activity** - UFO sightings, abductions, or other phenomena
- **Cover-up operations** - Government suppression of information

## Investigation Hooks

- **U.S. agents** - Majestic-12 operatives operating in Canada
- **Cross-border incidents** - UFO activity spanning both countries
- **Information sharing** - Cooperation or conflict between agencies
- **Diplomatic issues** - Tensions between governments
- **Conspiracy elements** - Hidden operations and cover-ups

## In Play

This scenario serves as:

- **Political thriller** - Cases involving government agencies and diplomacy
- **Cross-border operations** - Activities spanning U.S. and Canada
- **Agency conflict** - Competition or cooperation between M-EPIC and MJ-12
- **Conspiracy investigation** - Uncovering hidden operations
- **International scope** - Broader geopolitical implications

### Game Considerations

- The political angle adds realism and complexity
- Cross-border operations create jurisdictional challenges
- Agency conflicts create opportunities for tension and drama
- The conspiracy elements add layers of mystery
- International scope expands the setting beyond British Columbia

# Eagles Peak

**Type:** Mountain / Mining Site / UFO Hotspot  
**Location:** Glacier National Park, Selkirk Mountains, British Columbia  
**Elevation:** 2,846 metres (9,337 feet)

## Overview

**Eagles Peak** is a 2,846-metre (9,337-foot) mountain summit located in Glacier National Park in the Selkirk Mountains in British Columbia, Canada. Its nearest higher peak is Uto Peak, 1.0 km (0.62 mi) to the east. Mount Sir Donald is 2.0 km (1.2 mi) to the southeast, and Mount Macdonald 4.0 km (2.5 mi) to the north. The Avalanche Glacier is situated on the northeast side of the peak. The peak is visible from eastbound Highway 1, the Trans-Canada Highway approaching Rogers Pass.

Eagles Peak has become a known hotspot for UFO sightings.

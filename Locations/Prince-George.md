# Prince George

**Type:** City  
**Region:** Northern British Columbia  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

Prince George is a city in northern British Columbia, serving as a major hub for forestry, mining, and transportation in the region.

## Paranormal Activity

### 1969 UFO Incident

In 1969, Prince George, British Columbia experienced a UFO incident. This event may be connected to other UFO activity in the region, including the Shag Harbour incident and the 1969 sonic boom in Kelowna.

## Key Areas

### Industrial Areas

Prince George's economy is based on forestry, mining, and resource extraction, creating industrial settings for investigations.

### Transportation Hub

The city serves as a transportation hub for northern British Columbia, with connections to remote areas where paranormal activity may occur.

## In Play

Prince George provides:

- **Remote investigation settings** - Northern location provides access to wilderness and remote areas
- **Industrial settings** - Forestry and mining operations create opportunities for workplace-related phenomena
- **UFO activity** - The 1969 incident provides a historical hook for alien-related investigations
- **Transportation hub** - Central location for accessing northern British Columbia

### Game Considerations

- Prince George's northern location creates a different atmosphere from coastal cities
- The 1969 UFO incident connects to broader alien activity in the region
- Industrial and resource extraction settings provide unique investigation environments
- The city's role as a transportation hub makes it a natural staging point for northern investigations

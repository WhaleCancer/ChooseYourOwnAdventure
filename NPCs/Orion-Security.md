# Orion Security Personnel

**Type:** Corporate security / NPC statblock  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Orion Security** personnel are the private security force employed by [[Factions/Corporations/Orion-International|Orion International]]. They are known for their black trucks, blank-faced field agents, and secured research enclaves.

## Characteristics

Orion Security personnel are:

- **Well-armed** - Equipped with advanced weapons and equipment
- **Professional** - Trained and disciplined security operatives
- **Secretive** - Operating with minimal public visibility
- **Intimidating** - Known for their blank-faced, menacing presence
- **Well-funded** - Access to advanced technology and resources

## Operations

Orion Security operates:

- **Research facility protection** - Securing Orion research enclaves
- **Field operations** - Conducting operations in remote areas
- **Surveillance** - Monitoring locations and individuals
- **Intimidation** - Suppressing threats or investigations
- **Asset protection** - Guarding valuable technology or information

## Relationship with Other Factions

- **M-EPIC:** Orion Security may cooperate with or oppose M-EPIC agents
- **RCMP:** Distrust and conflict with local law enforcement
- **Government:** Operating under government orders in some cases
- **Civilians:** Intimidating and suppressing witnesses or investigators

## In Play

Orion Security serves as:

- **Corporate antagonists** - Opposing investigations or operations
- **Intimidation** - Suppressing witnesses or evidence
- **Combat encounters** - Well-armed and trained opponents
- **Surveillance** - Monitoring player characters
- **Conspiracy element** - Part of larger corporate cover-ups

### Game Considerations

- Orion Security provides a grounded, corporate threat
- The well-armed nature creates dangerous combat encounters
- The secretive operations create investigation opportunities
- The connection to Orion International adds conspiracy layers
- The intimidation tactics create moral and tactical challenges

# Contactees

**Type:** UFO witnesses / NPC statblock  
**Era:** 1985

## Overview

**Contactees** are individuals who claim to have had contact with UFOs or alien entities. They are a common source of information for M-EPIC investigations, though their reliability varies widely.

## Characteristics

Contactees typically display:

- **Trauma** - Psychological effects from their experiences
- **Fragmented memories** - Incomplete or altered recollections
- **Physical evidence** - Implants, scars, or other proof of contact
- **Belief** - Strong conviction about their experiences
- **Varied reliability** - Some are credible, others are delusional or hoaxers

## Types of Contactees

### Credible Witnesses

- **Clear memories** - Detailed and consistent accounts
- **Physical evidence** - Implants, scars, or other proof
- **Corroboration** - Other witnesses or evidence supporting their claims
- **Stable mental state** - No signs of delusion or mental illness

### Traumatized Witnesses

- **Fragmented memories** - Incomplete or altered recollections
- **Psychological trauma** - PTSD, anxiety, or other mental health issues
- **Reluctance** - Fear of speaking about their experiences
- **Physical symptoms** - Unexplained health problems

### Hoaxers or Delusional

- **Inconsistent accounts** - Stories that don't add up
- **No evidence** - Lack of physical proof
- **Attention-seeking** - Motivated by fame or attention
- **Mental illness** - Delusional or psychotic symptoms

## M-EPIC Investigation

M-EPIC agents investigate contactees to:

- **Gather information** - Learning about alien activities
- **Collect evidence** - Obtaining implants, samples, or other proof
- **Protect witnesses** - Preventing further contact or harassment
- **Verify claims** - Determining credibility and reliability
- **Pattern recognition** - Identifying connections between cases

## In Play

Contactees serve as:

- **Information sources** - Providing leads and evidence
- **Investigation hooks** - Cases starting with contactee reports
- **Witnesses** - Testifying about their experiences
- **Victims** - Requiring protection or assistance
- **Red herrings** - False leads or unreliable information

### Game Considerations

- Contactees provide a human element to alien encounters
- The varied reliability creates investigation challenges
- The trauma angle adds psychological horror elements
- The physical evidence provides tangible proof
- The pattern recognition creates opportunities for connecting cases

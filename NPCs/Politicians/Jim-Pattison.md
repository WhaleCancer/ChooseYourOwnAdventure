# Jim Pattison

**Type:** Business magnate / Member of The Hand  
**Era:** 1985  
**Status:** Active

## Overview

**James Allen Pattison** (born October 1, 1928) is a Canadian business magnate and investor. He is based in Vancouver, British Columbia, where he holds the position of chief executive officer, chairman and sole owner of the Jim Pattison Group. The Group is active in numerous sectors including packaging, food, and forestry products.

## Connection to The Hand

Jim Pattison is a member of [[Factions/The-Hand|The Hand]], a secret cabal of wealthy individuals resisting alien influence. His connections to Expo 86 and extensive business networks make him a valuable asset to The Hand's operations.

## Expo 86 Connection

Pattison's business interests and connections to Expo 86 make him a key figure in Vancouver's economic and political landscape. His involvement in The Hand suggests that Expo 86 may have connections to paranormal activities or conspiracy operations.

## Business Networks

The Jim Pattison Group's diverse business interests provide cover and resources for The Hand's operations. Pattison's legitimate business activities may mask secret operations or provide access to information and resources needed by The Hand.

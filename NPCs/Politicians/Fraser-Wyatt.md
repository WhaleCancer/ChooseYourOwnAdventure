# Fraser Wyatt

**Type:** Radio personality / Conspiracy theorist / Member of The Hand  
**Era:** 1985  
**Status:** Active

## Overview

**Fraser Wyatt** is the host of "Cascadia AM with Fraser Wyatt," a late-night AM radio program focusing on conspiracy theories and paranormal investigation. He is a known member of [The Hand](./Factions/The-Hand.md), suggesting that his radio program may serve as a tool for The Hand to gather information, spread disinformation, or identify potential threats.

## Radio Program

Fraser Wyatt hosts "Cascadia AM with Fraser Wyatt" on [Cascadia AM](./Factions/Cascadia-AM.md), a late-night AM radio station. The program features conspiracy theories, paranormal investigations, and discussions of strange events in British Columbia.

## Connection to The Hand

Fraser Wyatt is a known member of The Hand, a secret cabal of wealthy individuals resisting alien influence. His radio program may serve multiple purposes for The Hand:

- **Information gathering** - Collecting reports of strange events and potential threats
- **Disinformation** - Spreading false information to confuse enemies or the public
- **Threat identification** - Identifying individuals who may be aware of conspiracies
- **Recruitment** - Finding potential allies or members for The Hand

## Conspiracy Theories

Fraser Wyatt's program focuses on conspiracy theories and paranormal phenomena. His theories may be based on real information from The Hand, or they may be disinformation designed to mislead enemies or the public.

## In Play

Fraser Wyatt serves as:

- **The Hand member** - Radio personality with access to information networks
- **Information source** - Collects and disseminates information about strange events
- **Disinformation agent** - May spread false information to protect The Hand
- **Investigation target** - PCs may investigate Wyatt or his program
- **Scenario hook** - PCs may encounter Wyatt through radio or conspiracy connections

### Game Considerations

- Wyatt's radio program provides a public face for conspiracy activities
- His membership in The Hand suggests he's aware of real threats
- The program may be used for information gathering or disinformation
- PCs may need to determine if Wyatt's theories are true or false
- The radio program creates opportunities for media-based scenarios

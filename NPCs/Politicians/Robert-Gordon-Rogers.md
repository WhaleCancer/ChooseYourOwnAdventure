# Robert Gordon Rogers

**Type:** Lieutenant Governor / Member of The Hand  
**Era:** 1985  
**Status:** Active (serving since 1983)

## Overview

**Robert Gordon Rogers** (born August 19, 1919) is the 24th Lieutenant Governor of British Columbia, having served since 1983. As the Lieutenant Governor, Rogers wields critical political influence, possessing the constitutional power to dissolve the government under the right circumstances.

## Connection to The Hand

Robert Gordon Rogers is a member of [[Factions/The-Hand|The Hand]], a secret cabal of wealthy individuals resisting alien influence. His position as Lieutenant Governor provides military and political connections that are valuable to The Hand's operations.

## Political Influence

As Lieutenant Governor, Rogers has significant constitutional powers, including the ability to dissolve the government. This position provides access to government information, military connections, and political influence that The Hand can leverage.

## Military Connections

Rogers' position and background provide connections to military and defense operations. These connections may be crucial for The Hand's operations against alien threats or government conspiracies.

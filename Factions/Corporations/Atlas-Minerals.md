# Mount Atlas Minerals Ltd. (Atlas Minerals)

**Type:** Mining corporation  
**Territory:** British Columbia (headquarters: Vancouver)  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

**Mount Atlas Minerals Ltd. (Atlas Minerals)** is a major mining, prospecting, and "resource development" corporation headquartered in Vancouver. Formed in the post-war boom, it has followed veins of copper, silver, gold, and rare earths into BC's most remote, wild, and troubled terrain. Atlas is as much a lobbying juggernaut as a mining operation, infamous among locals for disastrous tailings spills, militant land "negotiation" tactics, and stonewalling environmental movements.

## Criticism

Critics contend that Atlas Minerals routinely pushes into remote and often culturally significant regions, showing little regard for local traditions or concerns. They argue that the company's rapid pace of development can disrupt both the environment and the communities who rely on it. Detractors maintain that this approach, coupled with a focus on short-term gains, leaves the true impact of Atlas's operations unaddressed and local voices unheard.

# Cascadia AM

**Type:** Radio station / Conspiracy media outlet  
**Territory:** British Columbia (broadcast range)  
**Era:** Active in 1985

## Overview

**Cascadia AM** is a late-night AM radio station featuring "Cascadia AM with Fraser Wyatt," a conspiracy theory and paranormal investigation program hosted by [[Fraser Wyatt]].

Inspired by Art Bell's *Coast to Coast AM*, Cascadia AM serves as a source of information, misinformation, and community for those interested in the paranormal, conspiracies, and unexplained phenomena in British Columbia.

## Structure

Cascadia AM operates as an independent radio station, likely with limited resources and a small staff. The station's primary asset is Fraser Wyatt's ability to attract callers, guests, and information about strange events.

### Key Personnel

- **[[Fraser Wyatt]]** - Host of "Cascadia AM with Fraser Wyatt"; radio personality, conspiracy theorist, and potential member of [[Factions/The-Hand|The Hand]]

## Programming

Cascadia AM broadcasts late-night programming focused on:

- UFO sightings and alien encounters  
- Conspiracy theories  
- Paranormal phenomena  
- Government cover-ups  
- Strange events in British Columbia  
- Call-in segments with listeners reporting their own experiences

## Relationship with Other Factions

- **The Hand:** Fraser Wyatt is a known member of The Hand, suggesting that Cascadia AM may serve as a tool for The Hand to gather information, spread disinformation, or identify potential threats.  
- **M-EPIC:** M-EPIC agents may monitor Cascadia AM for leads on paranormal activities, though they must be cautious about the reliability of information.  
- **General Public:** The station serves as a community hub for those interested in the paranormal, creating a network of amateur investigators and witnesses.

## In Play

Cascadia AM can serve multiple functions in a campaign:

- **Information Source:** Players may listen to the show for leads on strange events or call in to report their own experiences  
- **Disinformation Tool:** The Hand may use the station to spread false information or distract from real threats  
- **Community Connection:** The station creates a network of paranormal enthusiasts who may provide information or assistance  
- **Investigation Target:** Players may investigate Fraser Wyatt or the station itself if they suspect it's connected to conspiracies
